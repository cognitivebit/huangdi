    淫邪發夢

    1. 黃帝曰：願聞淫邪泮衍，奈何？歧伯曰：正邪從外襲內，而未有定舍，反淫于藏，不得定處，與營衛俱行，而與魂魄飛揚，使人臥不得安而喜夢；氣淫于府，則有餘于外，不足于內；氣淫于藏，則有餘于內，不足于外。 Yellow Emperor said: I want to hear about obscene and chaotic behavior. What do you say? Obscure Lord replied: The obscene and chaotic arise externally and invade internally, but there is no fixed abode. They act contrary to the hidden treasury and cannot be settled, accompanying soldiers and guards in their activities. This causes the souls and spirits to fly up and down, making people unable to rest in peace and delight in dreams. The vital energy is depleted externally, leaving nothing for the internal, or depleted internally, leaving nothing for the external.

    2. 黃帝曰：有餘不足，有形乎？歧伯曰：陰氣盛，則夢涉大水而恐懼；陽氣盛，則夢大火而燔焫；陰陽俱盛，則夢相殺。上盛則夢飛，下盛則夢墮；甚飢則夢取，甚飽則夢予；肝氣盛，則夢怒，肺氣盛，則夢恐懼、哭泣、飛揚；心氣盛，則夢善笑恐畏；脾氣盛，則夢歌樂，身體重不舉；腎氣盛，則夢腰脊兩解不屬。凡此十二盛者，至而寫之，立已。 Yellow Emperor said: Is there any form when there is excess or deficiency? Guo Bo replied: When yin qi is abundant, one may dream of crossing a vast body of water and feel afraid; when yang qi is abundant, one may dream of fire and be burnt; when both yin and yang are abundant, one may dream of killing each other. If the upper part is abundant, one may dream of flying; if the lower part is abundant, one may dream of falling. If one is extremely hungry, one may dream of taking something; if one is extremely full, one may dream of giving. When liver qi is abundant, one may dream of being angry; when lung qi is abundant, one may dream of being afraid, crying, and soaring; when heart qi is abundant, one may dream of laughing and being scared; when spleen qi is abundant, one may dream of singing and dancing, but the body cannot rise; when kidney qi is abundant, one may dream of pain in the lumbar region and hips that do not feel like they belong. In summary, these twelve excesses, once reached, should be recorded and completed.

    3. 厥氣客於心，則夢見丘山煙火；客於肺，則夢飛揚，見金鐵之奇物；客於肝，則夢山林樹木；客於脾，則夢見丘陵大澤，壞屋風雨；客于腎，則夢臨淵，沒居水中；客于膀胱，則夢遊行；客于胃，則夢飲食；客于大腸，則夢田野；客于小腸，則夢聚邑沖衢；客于膽，則夢鬥訟自刳；客于陰器，則夢接內；客于項，則夢斬首；客于脛，則夢行走而不能前，及居深地窌苑中；客于股肱，則夢禮節拜起；客于胞䐈，則夢溲便。凡此十五不足者，至而補之立已也。 When the vital energy resides in the heart, one dreams of seeing mountains and fires; when it resides in the lungs, one dreams of flying and sees strange iron objects; when it resides in the liver, one dreams of forests and trees; when it resides in the spleen, one dreams of hills and vast fields with ramshackle houses, wind, and rain; when it resides in the kidneys, one dreams of dwelling by the deep abyss; when it resides in the bladder, one dreams of traveling; when it resides in the stomach, one dreams of eating and drinking; when it resides in the large intestine, one dreams of fields; when it resides in the small intestine, one dreams of cities by the sea or river; when it resides in the pancreas, one dreams of fighting lawsuits and self-mutilation; when it resides in the genitals, one dreams of engaging in sex; when it resides in the neck, one dreams of beheading others; when it resides in the shanks, one dreams of walking but being unable to move forward or dwelling deep in a dimly lit forest; when it resides in the hip joints, one dreams of performing ritual bows and prostrations; when it resides in the ovaries or testes, one dreams of defecating. All these fifteen cases are not exhaustive, but can be supplemented as necessary.
