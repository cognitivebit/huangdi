    離合真邪

    1: 黃帝問曰：余聞九鍼九篇，夫子乃因而九之，九九八十一篇，余盡通其意矣。經言氣之盛衰，左右傾移，以上調下，以左調右，有餘不足，補寫於榮輸，余知之矣。此皆榮衛之傾移，虛實之所生，非邪氣從外入於經也。余願聞邪氣之在經也，其病人何如，取之奈何。歧伯對曰：夫聖人之起度數，必應於天地，故天有宿度，地有經水，人有經脈。天地溫和，則經水安靜；天寒地凍，則經水凝泣；天暑地熱，則經水沸溢；卒風暴起，則經水波涌而隴起。夫邪之入於脈也，寒則血凝泣，暑則氣淖澤，虛邪因而入客，亦如經水之得風也，經之動脈，其至也亦時隴起，其行於脈中循循然，其至寸口中手也，時大時小，大則邪至，小則平，其行無常處，在陰與陽，不可為度，從而察之，三部九候，卒然逢之，早遏其路。吸則內鍼，無令氣忤，靜以久留，無令邪布，吸則轉鍼，以得氣為故，候呼引鍼，呼盡乃去，大氣皆出，故命曰寫。帝曰：不足者補之，柰何。歧伯曰：必先捫而循之，切而散之，推而按之，彈而怒之，抓而下之，通而取之，外引其門，以閉其神，呼盡內鍼，靜以久留，以氣至為故，如待所貴，不知日暮，其氣以至，適而自護，候吸引鍼，氣不得出，各在其處，推闔其門，令神氣存，大氣留止，故命曰補。 Emperor Yellow asked: I have heard of the Nine Needles and Nine Sections. Master, you have caused them to be based on the Nine. There are ninety-nine sections, and I have thoroughly understood their meaning. The classics speak of Qi's abundance and scarcity, left and right changes, adjusting top and bottom, adjusting left and right, there is some insufficiency, supplemented in Hui Wu. I know this. All of these are the left and right changes of Hui Wei, a virtual and real origin, not external evil entering the classics. I want to hear about the evil in the classics, what kind of patient it is, and how to cure it. The Duke said: A sage person's pulse depth will correspond with heaven and earth. Thus, there are heavenly depths and terrestrial waterways; there are human meridians and blood vessels. When heaven and earth are warm and harmonious, the terrestrial waterway is calm; when heaven is cold and the earth is frozen, the terrestrial waterway solidifies; when heaven is hot and the earth is burning, the terrestrial waterway boils over; when sudden wind and storms arise, the terrestrial waterway surges and rises. When evil enters the meridian, it is cold and blood coagulates, or it is hot and Qi oozes. Vacuity and evil enter guests, just like how the terrestrial waterway gets wind. When the meridian's pulse arrives, there may be a surge, and its course within the meridians goes round and round until it reaches the Jing well point, large or small, large means evil has arrived, and small means it is stable, with an unpredictable course, between Yin and Yang, impossible to measure. From it, I will investigate the three patterns of nine indications, encountering it suddenly, and early stopping its path. When pulling, use interior needles, without letting Qi rebel, keeping still to allow it to linger, without letting evil spread. Pull and change needles to get at Qi's cause, summon and withdraw needles completely, all large Qi will come out, so the name is Jie. Emperor Yellow asked: What about supplementing? Duke Zhang said: It must first cultivate and follow it, cut and disperse it, push and press it, pluck and move it, grasp and lower it, permeate and take it, outside to close its door, calling for interior needles, keeping still to allow it to linger, using Qi's arrival as the reason, like caring for something precious, not knowing when day turns to night, its Qi will reach a state of self-defense. Call summoning and pulling needles, Qi will not come out, each in their place, push locking its door, let the spirit and Qi remain, and large Qi stays still, so the name is Supplement.

    2: 帝曰：候氣奈何。歧伯曰；夫邪去絡入於經也，舍於血脈之中，其寒溫未相得，如涌波之起也，時來時去，故不常在。故曰：方其來也，必按而止之，止而取之，無逢其衝而寫之。真氣者，經氣也，經氣太虛，故曰：其來不可逢，此之謂也。故曰：候邪不審，大氣已過，寫之則真氣脫，脫則不復，邪氣復至，而病益蓄，故曰：其往不可追，此之謂也。不可挂以髮者，待邪之至時而發鍼寫矣，若先若後者，血氣已盡，其病不可下，故曰：知其可取如發機，不知其取如扣椎，故曰：知機道者不可挂以髮，不知機者扣之不發，此之謂也。帝曰：補寫奈何。歧伯曰：此攻邪也，疾出以去盛血，而復其真氣，此邪新客，溶溶未有定處也，推之則前，引之則止，逆而刺之，溫血也。刺出其血，其病立已。 Emperor said: How is the timing of Qi? Obaku said, Evil enters within the canons, discarding in the blood vessels, its cold and warmth have not yet blended, like a surge wave, coming and going, therefore not always present. Therefore it says: it is coming, one must stop it, stopping it, take it, without encountering its surge, inscribing it. The true Qi is the canal Qi, the canal Qi is too empty, so it says: its arrival cannot be met, this means. Therefore it says: the timing of evil is not certain, the great Qi has passed, inscribing it will cause the true Qi to dissipate, if it dissipates there will be no return, and evil Qi will arrive again, causing the disease to accumulate further. It cannot be chased, this means. Not allowed to hang it on a hair, waiting for the arrival of evil, then inserting the needle and inscribing, whether before or after, the blood and Qi have been exhausted, the disease cannot be cured, so it says: knowing when to take it is like aiming at a target, not knowing when to take it is like attaching a peg, this means. Emperor said: What about supplementing and inscribing? Obaku said: This is an attack on evil, quickly expelling it to replenish the true Qi, this evil is new guests, without a fixed location, pushing it will make it move forward, pulling it will make it stop, reversing it and piercing it warms the blood. Piercing out its blood, the disease is cured

    3: 帝曰：善。然真邪以合，波隴不起，候之柰何。歧伯曰：審捫循三部九候之盛虛而調之，察其左右上下相失及相減者，審其病藏以期之。不知三部者，陰陽不別，天地不分。地以候地，天以候天，人以候人，調之中府，以定三部，故曰：刺不知三部九候病脈之處，雖有大過且至，工不能禁也。誅罰無過，命曰：大惑，反亂大經，真不可復，用實為虛，以邪為真，用鍼無義，反為氣賊，奪人正氣，以從為逆，榮衛散亂，真氣已失，邪獨內著，絕人長命，予人天殃，不知三部九候，故不能久長。因不知合之四時五行，因加相勝，釋邪攻正，絕人長命。邪之新客來也，未有定處，推之則前，引之則止，逢而寫之，其病立已。 The emperor said: Good. Although the distinction between good and evil is clear, the eight trigrams do not move if circumstances are right, and I wonder what will happen next. Duke of Zhou replied: By observing the changes in the three lines within the nine phases, one can determine whether they are flourishing or declining and adjust accordingly. One should pay attention to where the energy is lost on the left, right, top, and bottom, as well as any significant shifts, and predict when the illness will resolve. Not knowing the three lines means that yin and yang are not distinct, and heaven and earth are not separate. Earth adjusts to earth, heaven adjusts to heaven, and humans adjust to humans. By defining the three lines in the center, it is said: A physician who does not know the nine phases of the illness cannot control the pulses. Punishment without error, the command says: Great confusion, reversal of the great classic, which is truly irrevocable, using reality as falsehood, using evil as truth, using needles without meaning, and becoming a thief of vital energy, depriving people of their genuine energy and following it with perversity, scattering the honor guard, losing the true vital energy, leaving only evil inside, and cutting off life expectancy, thus bringing disaster from heaven upon people. Because they do not know the three lines and nine phases, they cannot sustain longevity. Since they do not understand the harmony of the four times and the five phases, they are likely to emphasize one over the other, attack the genuine with evil, cut off life expectancy, and bring disaster upon people. The new evil comes from an unknown source; if it is pushed, it will advance, and if pulled, it will stop. Record this when you encounter it, as the illness has taken root.
