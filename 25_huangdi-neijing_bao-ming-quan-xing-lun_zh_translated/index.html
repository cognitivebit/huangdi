    寶命全形論

    1. 黃帝問曰：天覆地載，萬物悉備，莫貴於人，人以天地之氣生，四時之法成，君王眾庶，盡欲全形，形之疾病，莫知其情，留淫日深，著於骨髓，心私慮之。余欲鍼除其疾病，為之奈何。歧伯對曰：夫鹽之味鹹者，其氣令器津泄；絃絕者，其音嘶敗；木敷者，其葉發；病深者，其聲噦。人有此三者，是謂壞府，毒藥無治，短鍼無取，此皆絕皮傷肉，血氣爭黑。 Yellow Emperor asked: The heavens and earth are complete, all things are prepared, nothing is more precious than humans. People are born from the qi of heaven and earth, the laws of the four seasons are established, rulers among the multitude, wanting to preserve their physical form, the diseases of the body, nothing knows their nature, accumulating lust for many days, lodging in the bone marrow, worrying about the heart. I want to remove the disease with acupuncture, what should I do? Hua Xi answered: The flavor of salt is salty, its qi regulates the vessel and excretes sweat; the string is broken, its sound becomes weak; the wood is sealed, its leaves grow. When a person has these three things, it is called a ruined palace, poisonous drugs cannot be treated, short needles cannot be grasped, all are cutting the skin and injuring the flesh, blood qi struggle and black.

    2. 帝曰：余念其痛，心為之亂惑，反甚，其病，不可更代，百姓聞之，以為殘賊，為之柰何。歧伯曰：夫人生於地，懸命於天，天地合氣，命之曰人。人能應四時者，天地為之父母；知萬物者，謂之天子。天有陰陽，人有十二節；天有寒暑，人有虛實。能經天地陰陽之化者，不失四時；知十二節之理者，聖智不能欺也；能存八動之變，五勝更立；能達虛實之數者，獨出獨入，呿吟至微，秋毫在目。 The emperor said: I think of my pain and my heart is in chaos, it is quite severe. His illness cannot be passed on to another, if the common people hear of this, they will consider it a disaster, what should be done? Duke Zhou replied: Man is born of earth and hangs upon heaven. When earth and heaven unite their breaths, it is called man. If a person responds to the four seasons, heaven and earth are his parents; if one knows all things, he is called the Son of Heaven. There are yin and yang in heaven, and there are ten vital points in man. There are cold and heat in heaven, and there are reality and emptiness in man. If one can harmonize with the changes of heaven and earth, then one will not miss the four seasons; if one knows the principles of the twelve points, saints cannot be deceived; if one can endure the eight changes, five victories will arise; if one can grasp the numbers of reality and emptiness, one will be unique and enter alone, sighing and murmuring in extreme weakness, like a hair left in the eye.

    3. 帝曰：人生有形，不離陰陽，天地合氣，別為九野，分為四時，月有小大，日有短長，萬物並至，不可勝量，虛實呿吟，敢問其方。歧伯曰：木得金而伐，火得水而滅，土得木而達，金得火而缺，水得土而絕，萬物盡然，不可勝竭。故鍼有懸布天下者五，黔首共餘食，莫知之也。一曰治神，二曰知養身，三曰知毒藥為真，四曰制砭石小大，五曰知府藏血氣之診。五法俱立，各有所先。今末世之刺也，虛者實之，滿者泄之，此皆眾工所共知也。若夫法天則地，隨應而動，和之者若響，隨之者若影，道無鬼神，獨來獨往。 The emperor said: Human life has form and does not depart from yin and yang. Heaven and Earth unite their qi, divide into nine regions, and differentiate into four seasons. The moon has small and large phases, and the sun has short and long days. All things reach perfection and cannot be fully enumerated. It is like a ghost singing, daring to ask its method. Duke of Zhou said: Wood obtains metal but cuts it down; fire obtains water and extinguishes it; earth obtains wood and flourishes; metal obtains fire and lacks it; water obtains soil and ends it; all things are complete and cannot be exhausted. Therefore, among the five needles that hang from heaven to earth, there is one for the marquis of the left arch, one for the duke of the right arch, and one for the lord of the central arch - these three are not known. The first [needle] is for regulating spirits, the second is for nourishing the body, the third is for knowing real medicine from poisonous ones, the fourth is for adjusting jade stones large and small, and the fifth is for knowing the location of blood and qi flow in the palace. Each of these five methods has its own area of expertise. Today's needle practitioners imitate the ancients; they virtualize what is real and release what is full - this is all known by ordinary people. If one follows the law of heaven and earth, it moves according to its nature, resonating like an echo and following like a shadow. There are no ghosts or gods that come and go independently.

    4. 帝曰：願聞其道。歧伯曰：凡刺之真，必先治神，五藏已定，九候已備，後乃存鍼，眾脈不見，眾凶弗聞，外肉相得，無以形先，可玩往來，乃施於人。人有虛實，五虛勿近，五實勿遠，至其當發，間不容𣊇。手動若務，鍼耀而勻，靜意視義，觀適之變，是謂冥冥，莫知其形，見其烏烏，見其稷稷，從見其飛，不知其誰，伏如橫弩，起如發機。帝曰：何如而虛，何如而實。歧伯曰：刺虛者須其實，刺實者須其虛，經氣已至，慎守勿失，深淺在志，遠近若一，如臨深淵，手如握虎，神無營於眾物。 The Emperor said: I wish to hear of its method. Guo Bo said: In regards to the true acupuncture, one must first regulate the spirit, once the five organs are established and the nine aspects are prepared, then store the needle, where there is no palpable pulse, and the danger is not heard, external flesh is obtained, but there is no form prior to this, it can be used to treat people. People have true and false [conditions], do not approach the five falses, do not avoid the five truths, when it arrives at its proper time, there is no room for doubt. Hand movements are skillful, the needle shines and circles, with a quiet mind observing justice, watching changes in suitability, this is called mysterious, unknown of its form, seeing its black and seeing its white, following seeing its flight, not knowing who it belongs to, crouching like a crossbow, standing like a firearm. The Emperor asked: How is it empty and how is it full? Guo Bo said: The needle empties what is full, and the needle fills what is empty, the channel qi has arrived, be cautious and do not miss, shallow and deep are intentions, far and near like one, standing at the brink of the abyss, the hand grasps the tiger, the spirit does not reside among the myriad things.
