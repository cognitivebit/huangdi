    氣府論

    1: 足太陽脈氣所發者七十八穴；兩眉頭各一，入髮至項三寸半，傍五相去三寸，其浮氣在皮中者凡五行，行五，五五二十五，項中大筋兩傍各一，風府兩傍各一，俠背以下至尻尾二十一節十五閒各一，五藏之俞各五，六府之俞各六，委中以下至足小指傍各六俞。 The seventy-two points emanating from the Sinew Channels are: On each eyebrow, one point, running from the hairline to the base of the neck three and a half inches, alongside the five Yin organs, five lines; in the center of the chest, one point, the Large Intestine meridian on each side, one point, Wind Pond on each side, one point, five behind each shoulder blade down to the end of the coccyx twenty-one vertebrae, fifteen pieces each, five points for the Five Organs, six points for the Six Divisions, and six points at either side of the little toe.

    2: 足少陽脈氣所發者六十二穴；兩角上各二，直目上髮際內各五，耳前角上各一，耳前角下各一，銳髮下各一，客主人各一，耳後陷中各一，下關各一，耳下牙車之後各一，缺盆各一，掖下三寸脇下至胠八閒各一，髀樞中傍各一，膝以下至足小指次指各六俞。 There are 62 acupoints that originate from the foot Yangming channel. There are two on each of the upper angles, five each inside the hairline above the forehead, one each at the front and back of the ear, one at the bottom of the sharp hair, one for the master of the house, one in the hollow at the back of the ear, one at the huiyin, one at the lower part of the guan, one below the tooth-wheels, one each for the guest and the host, six on each of the shu points from the jingluo below the mingmen to the bao, one on the lateral condyle, one on each side of the hip joint, and six on the shu points from the jinmen down to the little toe and second toe.

    3: 足陽明脈氣所發者六十八穴；額顱髮際傍各三，面鼽骨空各一，大迎之骨空各一，人迎各一，缺盆外骨空各一，膺中骨閒各一，俠鳩尾之外當乳下三寸，俠胃脘各五，俠齊廣三寸各三，下齊二寸俠之各三，氣街動脈各一，伏菟上各一，三里以下至足中指各八俞，分之所在穴空。 The points emanating from the foot Yangming channel are sixty-eight in number; on each side of the forehead, the temple, the cheekbone hollow, and the front of the ear, there is one space; at the great union and the small union, there is one space each; for the front and rear union, there is one space; outside the lower opening of the Baoji, there is one space in the breastbone hollow; on either side of the suprasternal notch, there are three spaces; outside the nipple tail, three inches from below, there are three spaces each; at the sides of the lower abdomen, there are five spaces each; at the sides of the grand and broad three inches each, and at the sides of the lower abdomen two inches each, there are three spaces each; for the channels of gastric qi circulation and the liver, there is one space each; above the Fu organs, there are one space each; from the third milepost to the tip of the toe, eight confluences each, and at the locations where they are, there are spaces.

    4: 手太陽脈氣所發者三十六穴；目內眥各一，目外各一，鼽骨下各一，耳郭上各一，耳中各一，巨骨穴各一，曲掖上骨穴各一，柱骨上陷者各一，上天䆫四寸各一，肩解各一，肩解下三寸各一，肘以下至手小指本各六俞。 The thirty-six acupoints generated by the hand Taiyang meridian; one in each inner canthus of the eye, one outside each eye, one at the suprasternal notch, one at the external auditory meatus, one in the ear canal, one at the juji point, one at the top of the patella, one at the base of the stapes bone, one at the top of the vertebra prominens, four at each of the shoulders, three inches below the shoulders, six at each joint from the elbow to the little finger of the hand.

    5: 手陽明脈氣所發者二十二穴：鼻空外廉項上各二，大迎骨空各一，柱骨之會各一，髃骨之會各一，肘以下至手大指次指本各六俞。 The twenty-two acupoints emanating from the Yang He (Hand Yangming) channel are: two at the Nasal Opening, two at the Mingmen (Royal Pearl), one in the Huanghuang (Yellow Emperor's Yellow Maple) point, one at the Jiexi (Gathering Marsh), and one at each of the Hequan (Pillar Meeting) and Haigui (Hairy Meeting) points. From the elbow to the tip of the hand, there are six points along the Shousanli (Bamboo Grove at the End) channel.

    6: 手少陽脈氣所發者三十二穴；鼽骨下各一，眉後各一，角上各一，下完骨後各一，項中足太陽之前各一，俠扶突各一，肩貞各一，肩貞下三寸分閒各一，肘以下至手小指次指本各六俞。 The thirty-two acupoints formed by the yang qi of the small intestine meridian are: one at the sternum below, one above each eyebrow, one at the corner of the eye, one below the coccyx, one in front of the suprasternal notch, one at the axilla, one on the axillary fossa, one at the back of each shoulder, one within the shoulder blade's base, and six at the elbow joint down to the little finger's first joint.

    7: 督脈氣所發者二十八穴；項中央二，髮際後中八，面中三，大椎以下至尻尾及傍十五穴，至骶下凡二十一節，脊椎法也。 The twenty-eight acupuncture points are emitted from the governor vessel; two at the central junction of the neck, eight at the hair's peak and back center, three on the face, fifteen below the large intestine to the tail and around it, with twenty-one nodes from the spinal vertebrae.

    8: 任脈之氣所發者二十八穴，喉中央二，膺中骨陷中各一，鳩尾下三寸胃脘五寸胃脘以下至橫骨六寸半一，腹脈法也，下陰別一，目下各一，下脣一，齗交一。 The twenty-eight energy points generated by the pulse are: 1. Central throat (located in the center of the throat) 2. Breast bone depression (in the breastbone) 3. Epigastric point (three inches below the navel) 4. Nine points on the stomach (five inches below the epigastric point) 5. Stomach meridian points until six and a half inches from the center of the throat (also known as the abdominal meridian) 6. Lower abdomen (one additional point) 7. Eyes (two additional points) 8. Chin (one additional point) 9. Tongue meeting (one additional point).

    9: 衝脈氣所發者二十二穴；俠鳩尾外各半寸至齊寸一，俠齊下傍各五分至橫骨寸一，腹脈法也。 The text describes the location and measurement of specific points related to abdominal palpation. Here is the translation: The twenty-two acupoints emanating from the pulse are located; each point of Qi men (Pectoralis minor) at the posterior axillary line, measured from half an inch to one inch apart, and each point of Jie xi (Rib 7) at the lateral margin, measured from five millimeters to one inch apart, abdominal palpation method.

    10: 足少陰舌下厥陰毛中急脈各一，手少陰各一，陰陽蹻各一，手足諸魚際脈氣所發者，凡三百六十五穴也。 There are eleven points on the sole: one at the heel, one in the depression beneath the tip of the little toe, one in the depression below the great toe, and one each for the three yin intersections (solar plexus), the three shao yang intersections (gallbladder channel), and the six yang intersections (heart channel). All together there are 365 points.
