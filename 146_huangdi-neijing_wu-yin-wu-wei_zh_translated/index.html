    1: 右徵與少徵，調右手太陽上。左商與左徵，調左手陽明上。少徵與大宮，調左手陽明上。右角與大角，調右手少陽下。大徵與少徵，調左手太陽上。眾羽與少羽，調右足太陽下。少商與右商調右手太陽下。桎羽與眾羽，調右足太陽下。少宮與大宮，調右足陽明下。判角與少角，調右足少陽下。釱商與上商，調右足陽明下。釱商與上角，調左足太陽下。 Right Wrist and Little Wrist, regulate the Right Hand Yuan-source. Left Market and Left Wrist, regulate the Left Hand Yang-Ming. Little Wrist and Great Palace, regulate the Left Hand Yang-Ming. Right Corner and Great Corner, regulate the Right Hand Shao-yang.     2: 上徵與右徵同穀麥、畜羊、果杏，手少陰藏心，色赤味苦，時夏。上羽與大羽，同穀大豆，畜彘，果栗，足少陰藏腎，色黑味鹹，時冬。上宮與大宮同穀稷，畜牛，果棗，足太陰藏脾，色黃味甘，時季夏。上商與右商同穀黍，畜雞，果桃，手太陰藏肺，色白味辛，時秋。上角與大角，同穀麻、畜犬、果李，足厥陰藏肝，色青味酸，時春。 Above Prognostication and Right Prognostication both pertain to grain, livestock, fruits of peaches, the hands have secretive yin, color is red, bitter taste, in summer. Above Feathers and Great Feathers both pertain to great beans, livestock are horses, fruits are walnuts, hands have secretive yin concealing the kidneys, color is black, salty taste, in winter. Above Palace and Great Palace both pertain to millet, livestock are oxen, fruits are jujubes, sufficient for yang concealed at the spleen, color is yellow, sweet taste, in summer and autumn. Above Market and Right Market both pertain to wheat, livestock are chickens, fruits are peaches, hands have concealed yang at the lungs, color is white, pungent taste, in autumn. Above Horns and Great Horns both pertain to hemp, livestock are dogs, fruits are apricots, feet have concealed yin at the liver, color is green, sour taste, in spring.

    3: 大宮與上角，同右足陽明上，左角與大角，同左足陽明上，少羽與大羽同右足太陽下，左商與右商，同左手陽明上，加宮與大宮同左足少陽上，質判與大宮，同左手太陽下，判角與大角同左足少陽下，大羽與大角，同右足太陽上，大角與大宮同右足少陽上，右徵、少徵、質徵、上徵、判徵、右角、釱角、上角、大角、判角。右商、少商、釱商、上商、左商。少宮、上宮、大宮、加宮、左角宮。眾羽、桎羽、上羽、大羽、少羽。 The Great Palace and the Upper Room are together at the right side of Yinman above, the Left Room and the Grand Room are together at the left side of Yinman above. The Lesser Feather and the Grand Feather are together at the right side of the Sun below, the Left Merchant and the Right Merchant are together at the left side of the Sun hand, the Added Palace and the Grand Palace are together at the left side of the Sun foot Little Yangming above, the Quality Judge and the Grand Palace are together at the left hand of the Sun below, the Judging Angle and the Grand Angle are together at the left side of the Sun foot Little Yangming below. The Grand Feather and the Grand Angle are together at the right side of the Sun above, the Grand Angle and the Grand Palace are together at the right side of the Sun foot Little Yangming above, Right Probe, Lesser Probe, Quality Probe, Upper Probe, Judge Probe, Right Angle, Nail Angle, Upper Angle, Grand Angle, Judge Angle. Right Merchant, Lesser Merchant, Nail Merchant, Upper Merchant, Left Merchant. Lesser Palace, Upper Palace, Grand Palace, Added Palace, Left Angle Palace.     4: 黃帝曰：婦人無鬚者，無血氣乎？歧伯曰：沖脈任脈皆起於胞中，上循背裏，為經絡之海，其浮而外者，循腹右上行，會於咽喉，別而絡唇口，血氣盛則充膚熱肉，血獨盛者澹滲皮膚，生毫毛。今婦人之生有餘於氣，不足於血以其數脫血也，沖任之脈，不榮口唇，故鬚不生焉。 Yellow Emperor said: Women without beards, do they have blood and vital energy? Duke of Ou replied: The Chong and Ren vessels both originate from the umbilicus, ascending along the spine to form the sea of Ying and Yang, with their superficial parts running along the right side of the abdomen, meeting at the throat, then branching off to connect the lips and mouth. When there is ample blood and vital energy, they nourish the skin and flesh, causing the blood to suffuse the pores and generate hair and beards. In women, their remaining vital energy is insufficient for blood, leading them to lose some of their blood, and thus the beards do not grow.

    5: 黃帝曰：士人有傷於陰，陰氣絕而不起，陰不用，然其鬚不去，其故何也？宦者獨去何也？願聞其故。歧伯曰：宦者去其宗筋，傷其沖脈，血寫不復，皮膚內結，唇口不榮故鬚不生。 Yellow Emperor said: A gentleman has an injury to the yin. Yin qi is cut off and cannot rise; yin is not used, yet his hair does not fall out. Why so? And why do officials alone lose their hair? May I hear the reason? Duke of Zhou replied: The official cuts his ancestral sinews and injures his great vessel. Blood stagnates and no longer circulates, and the skin forms a knot. Therefore, the lips and mouth are not honored, and the hair does not grow.

    6: 黃帝曰：其有天宦者，未嘗被傷，不脫於血，然其鬚不生其故何也？歧伯曰：此天之所不足也，其任沖不盛、宗筋不成，有氣無血，唇口不榮，故鬚不生。 Yellow Emperor said: Those who hold high office in Heaven have never been injured and do not shed blood. Yet their hair does not grow - what is the reason for this? Duke of Zhou said: This is an insufficiency of Heaven. Their mandate is weak and their governing power is unsuccessful, they have qi without blood, their lips and mouth are not honored, so their hair does not grow.

    7: 黃帝曰：善乎哉！聖人之通萬物也，若日月之光影，音聲鼓響，聞其聲而知其形，其非夫子，孰能明萬物之精。是故聖人，視其顏色黃赤者，多熱氣，青白者少熱氣，黑色者多血少氣，美眉者，太陽多血；通髯極須者，少陽多血，美鬚者陽明多血，此其時然也。 Yellow Emperor said: Excellent! A sage's understanding of myriad things is like the light and shadow of the sun and moon, the sounds and reverberations, hearing their sound and knowing their shape. What about the gentleman? Can he understand the essence of a thousand things? Therefore, a sage observes the color yellow or red, which has much heat energy; green or white, which has little heat energy; black, which has much blood but little heat energy; beautiful eyebrows mean an abundance of yang blood; through the beard, one can tell the extreme yin and yang, with beautiful hair meaning an abundance of yang light. This is indeed so

    8: 夫人之常數，太陽常多血少氣，少陽常多氣少血，陽明常多血多氣，厥陰常多氣少血，少陰常多血少氣，太陰常多血少氣，此天之常數也。 The nature of a woman is constant, the sun has more blood and less Qi, Shaoyang has more Qi and less blood, Yangming has more blood and more Qi, Bianyin has more Qi and less blood, ShaoYin has more blood and less Qi, Taiyin has more blood and less Qi. This is the constant nature of heaven as well.

    In classical Chinese medicine terms: - Sun (Taiyang) - More Blood, Less Qi - Shaoyang - More Qi, Less Blood - Yangming - More Blood, More Qi - Bianyin (Liver and Lung) - More Qi, Less Blood - ShaoYin (Heart and Kidney) - More Blood, Less Qi - Taiyin (Spleen and Large Intestine) - More Blood, Less Qi.
