    腹中論

    1. 黃帝問曰：有病心腹滿，旦食則不能暮食，此為何病。歧伯對曰：名為鼓脹。帝曰：治之柰何。歧伯曰：治之以雞矢醴一劑知二劑已。帝曰：其時有復發者何也。歧伯曰：此飲食不節，故時有病也。雖然其病且已，時故當病，氣聚於腹也。 Emperor asked: One has a full heart and abdomen, and cannot eat in the morning if they eat at night. What kind of disease is this? Hua Bo replied: It is called gǔ xīn. Emperor asked: How do you treat it? Hua Bo said: Treat with one to two doses of chicken needle medicine. Emperor asked: If there is a recurrence later, what is the reason? Hua Bo said: This is due to the lack of regulation of food and drink, which can cause disease at that time. Although the disease has improved for now, it will be sick again because the Qi is concentrated in the abdomen.

    2. 帝曰：有病胸脇支滿者，妨於食，病至則先聞腥臊臭，出清液，先唾血，四支清，目眩，時時前後血，病名為何。何以得之。歧伯曰：病名血枯。此得之年少時，有所大脫血，若醉入房中，氣竭肝傷，故月事衰少不來也。帝曰：治之柰何，復以何術。歧伯曰：以四烏鰂骨一藘茹二物并合之，丸以雀卵，大如小豆，以五丸為後飯，飲以鮑魚汁，利腸中及傷肝也。 Emperor asked: There is a person with fullness and pain in the chest and sides, hindering them from eating. When the disease worsens, they first hear the smell of rancid meat and foul odors, exude clear fluids, spit blood, have cold limbs, become dizzy, and sometimes have blood in front and behind. What is this disease called? Why did they get it?

    Bo Yi replied: The disease is called Xue Ku (Blood Dryness). It occurs during youth when there is excessive bleeding, such as when one is drunk and enters a bedroom, exhausting the lung qi and damaging the liver. This results in menstruation becoming insufficient or absent.

    Emperor asked: How do we treat this? What methods should be used?

    Bo Yi replied: Take four black bones of crows, soak them with two medicinal materials, mix them into small pellets the size of a millet seed. Take five of these pellets as an after-dinner snack, and drink broth made from shrimp. This will regulate the intestines and heal damaged liver qi.

    1. 帝曰：病有少腹盛，上下左右皆有根，此為何病，可治不歧伯曰：病名曰伏梁。帝曰：伏梁何因而得之。歧伯曰：裹大膿血，居腸胃之外，不可治，治之，每切按之致死。帝曰：何以然。歧伯曰：此下則因陰必下膿血，上則迫胃脘生鬲，俠胃脘內癰。此久病也難治。居齊上為逆，居齊下為從，勿動亟奪，論在刺法中。帝曰：人有身體髀股䯒皆腫，環齊而痛，是為何病。歧伯曰：病名伏梁，此風根也。其氣溢於大腸而著於肓，肓之原在齊下，故環齊而痛也。不可動之，動之為水溺濇之病。 Emperor: This disease has a shallow root and manifests in all four directions. What is this disease, and can it be treated? Hui Bo replied: The name of the disease is Fu Ling. Emperor: How does one contract the Fu Ling disease? Hui Bo answered: It occurs from wrapping the intestines with blood, residing outside the stomach and intestines, and cannot be treated. If treated, pressing it will inevitably lead to death. Emperor: Why is that so? Hui Bo replied: Below, it is caused by yin deficiency causing intestinal bleeding; above, it is due to pressure on the diaphragm and spleen, causing ulcers in the diaphragm and spleen. This is a long-standing disease difficult to treat. As for someone living in Qi who is upstairs as a rebel and downstairs as a follower, do not act hastily or seize urgently; discuss it in the context of acupuncture law. Emperor: When people have hip and knee joint pain, their bodies are swollen and surrounded by pain, what disease is this? Hui Bo replied: The disease is called Fu Ling, and it is a wind-related illness. Its energy overflowed in the large intestine and settled on the tendons, and the roots of the tendons are below. Therefore, it surrounds the hip and causes pain. Do not move it, as moving it will cause water drowning disease.

    2. 帝曰：夫子數言熱中消中，不可服高梁芳草石藥，石藥發瘨，芳草發狂。夫熱中消中者皆富貴人也，今禁高梁，是不合其心，禁芳草石藥，是病不愈，願聞其說。歧伯曰：夫芳草之氣美，石藥之氣悍，二者其氣急疾堅勁，故非緩心和人，不可以服此二者。帝曰：不可以服此二者，何以然。歧伯曰：夫熱氣慓悍，藥氣亦然，二者相遇恐內傷脾。脾者土也而惡木，服此藥者，至甲乙日更論。 The Emperor said: Master Zhū says that, in treating fever, one should not use high-ranking medicinal herbs such as flowers, grasses, and stones. Stones produce toxins, while flowers induce madness. The fever patients are usually wealthy and powerful individuals. Prohibiting the use of high-ranking herbs is against their wishes; prohibiting the use of flower and stone medicines will not cure them. I would like to hear your opinion on this matter. Lord Bi said: The virtues of flower and stone medicines are potent and swift, but they are not suitable for those who need to calm their hearts and harmonize with others. The Emperor asked: Why are they not suitable? Lord Bi replied: Because the fever energy is fierce and uncontrollable, the energy of the medicines is also like this. Combining them may cause internal damage to the spleen. The spleen represents earth and dislikes wood. Those who take these medicines will have more discussions on the day of jia and yi.

    3. 帝曰：善。有病膺腫頸痛胸滿腹脹此為何病，何以得之。歧伯曰：名厥逆。帝曰：治之柰何。歧伯曰：灸之則瘖，石之則狂，須其氣并，乃可治也。帝曰：何以然。歧伯曰：陽氣重上，有餘於上，灸之則陽氣入陰，入則瘖，石之則陽氣虛，虛則狂，須其氣并而治之，可使全也。 The emperor asked, What is this illness that causes one to have the diseases of fever, swollen neck, throat pain, a full chest and distended abdomen? The Duke of Zhou replied, It is called 'Extreme Yin'. The emperor asked, How do I treat it? The Duke of Zhou said, Apply acupuncture to expel the illness, and apply moxibustion to regulate the body. When the energies are balanced, the patient can be treated completely.

    4. 帝曰：善。何以知懷子之且生也。歧伯曰：身有病而無邪脈也。 The emperor said: Good. How do I know there is a fetus and it is growing? Duke Zhou said: There is a physical condition without any sign of evil qi in the body.

    5. 帝曰：病熱而有所痛者，何也。歧伯曰：病熱者，陽脈也，以三陽之動也，人迎一盛少陽，二盛太陽，三盛陽明，入陰也，夫陽入於陰，故病在頭與腹，乃䐜脹而頭痛也。帝曰：善。 The Emperor asked: What about those who are sick with fever and pain? Hui Bao replied: The fever is due to the Yang Meridian, which is caused by the movement of three Yangs - one of Shao Yang, two of Tai Yang, and three of Yang Ming.
