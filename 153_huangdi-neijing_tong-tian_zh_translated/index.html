    通天

    1. 黃帝問於少師曰：余嘗聞人有陰陽，何謂陰人？何謂陽人？少師曰：天地之間，六合之內，不離於五，人亦應之，非徒一陰一陽而已也，而略言耳，口弗能遍明也。黃帝曰：願略聞其意，有賢人聖人，心能備而行之乎？少師曰：蓋有太陰之人，少陰之人，太陽之人，少陽之人，陰陽和平之人。凡五人者，其態不同，其筋骨氣血各不等。 Emperor Yellow asked the Master, I have heard that there are Yin and Yang in people. What does 'Yin person' mean? What does 'Yang person' mean? The Master said, Between heaven and earth, within the six cycles, do not deviate from the five [elements], and people should also follow this. It is not just about one Yin and one Yang; although it can be briefly mentioned, the mouth cannot fully explain it. Emperor Yellow asked, I wish to hear a brief understanding of your meaning. Are there wise sages who have an enlightened heart and can act accordingly? The Master said, There are indeed people of Great Yin, Lesser Yin, Great Yang, and Lesser Yang. All five people have different demeanors, and their tendons, bones, qi, and blood are not equal.

    2. 黃帝曰：其不等者，可得聞乎？少師曰：太陰之人，貪而不仁，下齊湛湛，好內而惡出，心和而不發，不務於時，動而後之，此太陰之人也。 Yellow Emperor said: Can you hear about those who are not equal? Master said: People of the Lesser Yin, greedy but not compassionate, lowly and shallow, preferring the inside but disliking the outside, their hearts harmonious but not enlightened, neglecting their duties in time, acting late, this is the kind of person of the Lesser Yin.

    3. 少陰之人，小貪而賊心，見人有亡，常若有得，好傷好害，見人有榮，乃反慍怒，心疾而無恩，此少陰之人也。 A person with insufficient yin, being small-minded and having a thief's heart, upon seeing another in danger of death, always feels as if they have gained something, taking pleasure in harming or hurting them. Upon seeing another in honor, they become envious and angry, their hearts racing but lacking compassion. Such a person is one with insufficient yin

    4. 太陽之人，居處於於，好言大事，無能而虛說，志發乎四野，舉措不顧是非，為事如常自用，事雖敗，而常無悔，此太陽之人也。 A person of the sun, dwelling in such a place, speaks eloquently about great matters, but is unable to practice what they preach. They are zealous and act without regard for right or wrong, handling their affairs as is proper, and even though their endeavors may fail, they never regret it; this is a person of the sun.

    5. 少陽之人，諟諦好自責，有小小官，則高自宜，好為外交，而不內附，此少陽之人也。 A person of Shao Yung, always feels guilty, is modest when having a small official position, often engages in external affairs but does not attach themselves to internal matters, this is a person of Shao Yung.

    6. 陰陽和平之人，居處安靜，無為懼懼，無為欣欣，婉然從物，或與不爭，與時變化，尊則謙謙，譚而不治，是謂至治。 A person of yin and yang harmony, resides in tranquility, without being frightened or pleased, without being attached to the material, sometimes not contesting, harmonizing with the times, being revered while humble, leaving traces but not fixing them, this is called ultimate governance.

    7. 古之善用鍼艾者，視人五態，乃治之。盛者寫之，虛者補之。 In ancient times, those skilled in using acupuncture and moxibustion would observe the five aspects of a person's appearance, and treat them accordingly. For those who are healthy, they would record their condition; for those who are weak, they would supplement their energy.

    8. 黃帝曰：治人之五態奈何？少師曰：太陰之人，多陰而無陽，其陰血濁，其衛氣澀，陰陽不和，緩筋而厚皮，不之疾寫，不能移之。 The Yellow Emperor asked: What about the five states of governing people? The Lesser Sage replied: Those governed by Yin, are excessively Yin without Yang. Their Yin blood is turbid and their defensive Qi is stagnant. With Yin and Yang not in harmony, they have weak muscles and thick skin, are slow to act, and cannot be moved

    9. 少陰之人，多陰少陽，小胃而大腸，六府不調，其陽明脈小，而太陽脈大，必審調之，其血易脫，其氣易敗也。 A person with less yin has an excess of yin and deficient yang, a small stomach and large intestine, disordered six palaces, the yang link pulse is weak while the great yang pulse is strong, must regulate it, the blood is easily lost, the qi is easily damaged.

    10. 太陽之人，多陽而少陰，必謹調之，無脫其陰，而寫其陽。陽重脫者易狂，陰陽皆脫者，暴死，不知人也。 The person of the sun is predominantly yang and lacks yin, so one must be cautious in their approach. It is not advisable to remove yin from them while focusing on their yang. Those who overemphasize yang can easily become insane, and if both yin and yang are removed, it will result in a violent death, unbeknownst to the person involved.

    11. 少陽之人，多陽少陰，經小而絡大，血在中而氣外，實陰而虛陽。獨寫其絡脈，則強氣脫而疾，中氣不足，病不起也。 A person with a deficiency of Shao Yang, has an excess of Tai Yin, the channel is short but the collateral is large, blood is in the center while qi is outside, it is real yin and empty yang. If one only writes about the collateral channel, then the qi will be strong and leave quickly, there will not be enough qi in the middle, and the person will become ill.

    12. 陰陽和平之人，其陰陽之氣和，血脈調，謹診其陰陽，視其邪正，安容儀，審有餘不足，盛則寫之，虛則補之，不盛不虛，以經取之，此所以調陰陽，別五態之人者也。 A person who is harmonious in yin and yang, their yin and yang energy will be balanced, and their blood vessels regulated. They carefully diagnose their yin and yang, observe their pathological conditions, handle them with appropriate measures, determine whether there is excess or deficiency, and write them down when they are abundant and supplement when they are empty. If there is neither abundance nor emptiness, take it from the classics. This is how to adjust yin and yang and differentiate between five states of people

    13. 黃帝曰：夫五態之人者，相與毋故，卒然新會，未知其行也，何以別之？少師答曰：眾人之屬，不知五態之人者，故五五二十五人，而五態之人不與焉。五態之人，尤不合於眾者也。黃帝曰：別五態之人，奈何？少師曰：太陰之人，其狀黮黮然黑色，念然下意，臨臨然長大，膕然未僂，此太陰之人也。 Yellow Emperor said: In the case of a person with five types, they do not know each other. When there is an unexpected encounter, they do not know each other's movements. How can they be distinguished? The disciple answered: Most people do not know someone with five types, so among twenty-five people, there are only those with five types. People with five types are especially not suitable for the crowd. Yellow Emperor asked: How to distinguish a person with five types? The disciple said: A person with yin is in a state of mysterious blackness, thinking deeply, approaching and standing tall, unchanging before decaying, this is someone with yin.

    14. 少陰之人，其狀清然竊然，固以陰賊，立而躁嶮，行而似伏，此少陰之人也。 A person of obscurity, their demeanor is clear and unexpected, they stand with agitation and hurry, move as if hiding, this is a person of obscurity.

    15. 太陽之人，其狀軒軒儲儲，反身折膕，此太陽之人也。 The man of the sun has a majestic and radiant aura, bending and folding, this is indeed the man of the sun.

    16. 少陽之人，其狀立則好仰，行則好搖，其兩臂兩肘，則常出於背，此少陽之人也。 A person of Shao Yang, their posture is good at elevating [their gaze], their movement is good at swaying, their two arms and two elbows are always extending from behind, this is a person of Shao Yang.

    17. 陰陽和平之人，其狀委委然，隨隨然，顒顒然，愉愉然，䁢䁢然，豆豆然，眾人皆曰君子，此陰陽和平之人也。 A person of yin and yang harmony, their demeanor is soft and yielding, gentle and fluid, pleasing and refined, subtle and delicate, and humble. All people call such a person a true gentleman, this is a person of yin and yang harmony.
