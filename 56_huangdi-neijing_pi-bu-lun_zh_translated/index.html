    皮部論

    1. 黃帝問曰：余聞皮有分部，脈有經紀，筋有結絡，骨有度量，其所生病各異，別其分部左右上下陰陽所在病之始終，願聞其道。歧伯對曰：欲知皮部以經脈為紀者，諸經皆然。陽明之陽，名曰害蜚，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆陽明之絡也，其色多青則痛，多黑則痺，黃赤則熱，多白則寒，五色皆見，則寒熱也，絡盛則入客於經，陽主外，陰主內。少陽之陽，名曰樞持，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆少陽之絡也，絡盛則入客於經，故在陽者主內，在陰者主出以滲於內，諸經皆然。太陽之陽，名曰關樞，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆太陽之絡也，絡盛則入客於經。少陰之陰，名曰樞儒，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆少陰之絡也，絡盛則入客於經，其入經也，從陽部注於經，其出者，從陰內注於骨。心主之陰，名曰害肩，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆心主之絡也，絡盛則入客於經。太陰之陰，名曰關蟄，上下同法，視其部中有浮絡者，皆太陰之絡也，絡盛則入客於經。凡十二經絡脈者皮之部也。 Emperor Yellow asked: I have heard that there are divisions of skin, channels and networks of the sinews, measured degrees of the bones, various diseases arising from them, distinguishing their divisions left and right, above and below, yin and yang aspects. I wish to learn about this method. Chi Po replied: To know about the division of the skin using channels and networks as reference is taught in all the classics. The Yang Ming's yang, named Hui Gui, has similar laws above and below, observe its part for any floating or swollen areas, these are all the floating networks of Yang Ming. If its color is predominantly blue, there is pain; if predominantly black, there is paralysis; if red or yellow, there is fever; if white, there is cold. With the five colors seen, this indicates cold and heat. When there is an abundance of floating networks, they enter the channels; yang governs outside while yin governs inside. The Shao Yang's yang, named Zhi Ri, has similar laws above and below, observe its part for any floating or swollen areas, these are all the floating networks of Shao Yang. When there is an abundance of floating networks, they enter the channels. Hence, in the case of the Yang aspect governing inside and the Yin aspect governing outside, it will be absorbed within. The Shao Yin's yin, named Zhi Ren, has similar laws above and below, observe its part for any floating or swollen areas, these are all the floating networks of Shao Yin. When there is an abundance of floating networks, they enter the channels. Their entry into the channels is from the Yang aspect and their exit is from the Yin aspect within the bones. The Heart Governor's yin, named Hui Gua, has similar laws above and below, observe its part for any floating or swollen areas, these are all the floating networks of the Heart Governor. When there is an abundance of floating networks, they enter the channels. The Tai Yin's yin, named Guan Ri, has similar laws above and below, observe its part for any floating or swollen areas, these are all the floating networks of Tai Yin. When there is an abundance of floating networks, they enter the channels. In summary, the twelve channels and network vessels are parts of the skin.

    2. 是故百病之始生也，必先於皮毛，邪中之則腠理開，開則入客於絡脈，留而不去，傳入於經，留而不去，傳入於府，廩於腸胃。邪之始入於皮也，泝然起毫毛，開腠理。其入於絡也，則絡脈盛色變。其入客於經也，則感虛乃陷下。其留於筋骨之間，寒多則筋攣骨痛，熱多則筋弛骨消，肉爍䐃破，毛直而敗。帝曰：夫子言皮之十二部，其生病皆何如。歧伯曰：皮者，脈之部也。邪客於皮則腠理開，開則邪入客於絡脈，絡脈滿則注於經脈，經脈滿則入舍於府藏也，故皮者有分部，不與，而生大病也。帝曰：善。 Therefore, illness begins with one hundred diseases and first manifests in the skin and hair. In the pathological stage, the channels are opened, leading to the penetration of pathogens into the sinew-meridians, where they lodge without leaving, spreading to the organs, lodging without leaving, and spreading to the palace (abdomen). Pathogens enter through the skin and hair, causing small lesions and hair loss. When they invade the meridians, the meridian channels become distended and change color. When pathogens reach the organs, the body's Qi becomes depleted and weakened. The Emperor asked Master Qi: If you can explain how illnesses affecting the skin's twelve divisions arise, what will they be like? The Duke of Shou replied: The skin is a division of the meridians. When pathogens invade the skin, the channels open up, and the pathogens then penetrate the sinew-meridians. When the meridian channels are full, they spread to the organ meridians, which in turn enter and lodge in the palace (abdomen). That is why there are twelve divisions of the skin, and when they are not balanced, illness can develop into a serious condition. The Emperor said: I understand.
