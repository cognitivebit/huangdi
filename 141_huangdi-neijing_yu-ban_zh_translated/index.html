    1. 黃帝曰：余以小鍼為細物也，夫子乃言上合之于天，下合之于地，中合之于人，余以為過鍼之意矣，願聞其故。歧伯曰：何物大于天乎？夫大于鍼者，惟五兵者焉，五兵者死之備也，非生之具。且夫人者，天地之鎮也，其不可不參乎？夫治民者，亦唯鍼焉。夫鍼之與五兵，其孰小乎？ Yellow Emperor said: I consider the small needle as insignificant, and Confucius says to harmonize it with heaven above, earth below, and humanity within. I find this a wrong interpretation of the needle's meaning, and I would like to hear its true intention. Tui Bo said: What is greater than heaven? The only thing greater than a needle is the five weapons, which are preparations for death, not for life. Moreover, human beings are the settling of heaven and earth; they should not be neglected. Treating people is also dependent on the needle. Which is more insignificant, the needle or the five weapons?

    2. 黃帝曰：病之生時，有喜怒不測，飲食不節，陰氣不足，陽氣有餘，營氣不行，乃發為癰疽。陰陽不通，兩熱相搏，乃化為濃，小鍼能取之乎？歧伯曰：聖人不能使化者為之，邪不可留也。故兩軍相當，旗幟相望，白刃陳於中野者，此非一日之謀也。能使其民令行，禁止士卒無白刃之難者，非一日之教也，須臾之得也。夫至使身被癰疽之病，膿血之聚者，不亦離道遠乎？夫癰疽之生，膿血之成也，不從天下，不從地出，積微之所生也，故聖人自治於未有形也，愚者遭其已成也。 Yellow Emperor said: When a disease occurs, there are feelings of joy and anger that cannot be measured, eating and drinking without restraint, insufficient yin qi, excess yang qi, qi not circulating smoothly, resulting in the formation of boils and carbuncles. If yin and yang do not communicate, the two heat struggle against each other, leading to a thickening. Can a small knife remove it? Duke of Zhou said: A sage cannot cause this transformation, and it is wrong. Therefore, the two armies face each other, flags face each other, sharp blades are arrayed in the middle ground; this is not a one-day plan. To make the people follow this, to prohibit soldiers from having no sharp blades; this is not a one-day teaching but a moment's achievement. When the body is afflicted with boils and carbuncles, how far from the right way? The generation of boils and carbuncles is the accumulation of turbid blood, not from under heaven or from the earth, and it arises from the subtle. Therefore, a sage regulates without having a form yet, while a fool falls victim to it after it has already formed

    3. 黃帝曰：其已形，不予遭，膿已成，不予見；為之奈何？歧伯曰：膿已成，十死一生，故聖人弗使已成，而明為良方，著之竹帛，使能者踵而傳之後世，無有終時者，為其不予遭也。 Yellow Emperor said: Once formed, I do not allow it to happen, and once fully grown, I do not look at it. What can be done? Hui Shi said: Once fully grown, one is ten times dead and one time alive; thus, sages do not allow it to grow fully, but make clear what is a good method, record it on bamboo or silk, and let those who understand pass it down to future generations, without having an end time, so as not to allow it to happen.

    4. 黃帝曰：其已有膿血而後遭乎？不導之以小鍼治乎？歧伯曰：以小治小者，其功小，以大治大者，多害，故其已成膿血者，其唯砭石鈹鋒之所取也。 Yellow Emperor said: Is it already festered and then met with misfortune? Should it not be treated with a small lancet? Duke of Shou replied: For treating small issues, the effect is small, and for treating large issues, there are more harms. Thus, when it has already festered, the only option is to use a leech and flint knife.

    5. 黃帝曰：多害者其不可全乎？歧伯曰：其在逆順焉。黃帝曰：願聞逆順。歧伯曰：以為傷者，其白眼青，黑眼小，是一逆也；內藥而嘔者，是二逆也；腹痛渴甚，是三逆也；肩項中不便，是四逆也；音嘶色脫，是五逆也。除此五者，為順矣。 Yellow Emperor said: Is it impossible to completely eliminate harm? Duke of Shao replied: It is in the area of reversal (or upside down). Yellow Emperor asked: I wish to hear about reversal and order. Duke of Shao replied: The one who causes injury, his whites are green and blacks are small, this is one kind of reversal; taking medicine internally and vomiting, this is two kinds of reversal; abdominal pain with thirst, this is three kinds of reversal; difficulty moving the shoulders and neck, this is four kinds of reversal; hoarse voice and pale skin, this is five kinds of reversal. Except for these five, it becomes order (or right side up).

    6. 黃帝曰：諸病皆有逆順，可得聞乎？歧伯曰：腹脹、身熱、脈大，是一逆也；腹鳴而滿，四肢清泄，其脈大，是二逆也；衄而不止，脈大，是三逆也；咳而溲血脫形，其脈小勁，是四逆也；咳脫形，身熱，脈小以疾，是謂五逆也。如是者，不過十五日而死矣。 Yellow Emperor said: All diseases have reversals and progressions. Can you hear it? Master of Deviations replied: Distended abdomen, fever, and large pulse are one kind of reversal; distended abdomen, resonating sound within, urine and feces expelled from all four limbs, with a large pulse, this is the second kind of reversal; bleeding but not stopping, with a large pulse, this is the third kind of reversal; coughing with spitting blood and losing form, with a small weak pulse, this is the fourth kind of reversal; coughing until losing form, fever, with a small and quick pulse, this is called the five kinds of reversals. Those who experience such will not live beyond fifteen days.

    7. 其腹大脹，四末清，脫形，泄甚，是一逆也；腹脹便血，其脈大，時絕，是二逆也；咳溲血，形肉脫，脈搏，是三逆也；嘔血，胸滿引背，脈小而疾，是四逆也；咳嘔，腹脹且飧泄，其脈絕，是五逆也。如是者，不及一時而死矣。工不察此者而刺之，是謂逆治。 A large abdomen protruding, the extremities weak and pale, with distention and frequent diarrhea; this is one reversal. Abdominal distention accompanied by bleeding, the pulse being large and then ceasing; this is two reversals. Coughing up blood, skin and flesh falling off, pulse palpitation; this is three reversals. Vomiting blood, fullness in the chest referred to the back, and the pulse small and rapid; this is four reversals. Coughing and vomiting, abdominal distention and frequent diarrhea, and then the pulse ceasing; this is five reversals. Those who suffer thus are not likely to survive more than one day. If a physician does not understand this condition and proceeds to acupuncture, he is guilty of reversal therapy.

    8. 黃帝曰：夫子之言鍼甚駿，以配天地，上數天文，下度地紀，內別五藏，外次六府，經脈二十八會，盡有周紀。能殺生人，不能起死者，子能反之乎？歧伯曰：能殺生人，不能起死者也。黃帝曰：余聞之，則為不仁，然願聞其道，弗行於人。歧伯曰：是明道也，其必然也，其如刀劍之可以殺人，如飲酒使人醉也，雖勿診，猶可知矣。 Yellow Emperor said: Master Zhou's words are sharp like a needle, used to harmonize heaven and earth, calculating the stars above and determining the calendar below, distinguishing between the five viscera inside and the six departments outside, connecting the twenty-eight will points of the meridians, and fully recording the times. He can kill living people but cannot raise the dead; can Master Zhou reverse this? Kuai Bo said: Yes, he can kill living people but cannot raise the dead. Yellow Emperor said: I have heard that it is not humane; I hope to hear its method and do not practice it on humans. Kuai Bo said: This is a clear teaching, and it must be so; just as knives and swords can kill people, and drinking can make people drunk, even without diagnosis, it is still known.

    9. 黃帝曰：願卒聞之。歧伯曰：人之所受氣者，谷也。谷之所注者，胃也。胃者，水谷氣血之海也。海之所行云氣者，天下也。胃之所出氣血者，經隧也。而隧者，五藏六府之大絡也，迎而奪之而已矣。 Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear about it. Duke of Shou said: The energy that people receive is grain. The place where the grain is stored is the stomach. The stomach is like an ocean of water, grain, Qi, and blood. The ocean's currents of cloud and Qi are like the entire world. The Qi and blood that come out from the stomach go through the meridians, which are like the great conduit connecting the five viscera and six fu organs, and once they have been seized and taken away, that is all.

    10. 黃帝曰：上下有數乎？歧伯曰：迎之五里，中道而止，五至而已，五往而藏之氣盡矣，故五五二十五，而竭其輸矣，此所謂奪其天氣者也，非能絕其命而傾其壽者也。黃帝曰：願卒聞之。歧伯曰：闚門而刺之者，死於家中；入門而刺之者，死於堂上。黃帝曰：善乎方，明哉道，請著之玉版，以為重寶，傳之後世，以為刺禁，令民勿敢犯也。 Yellow Emperor asked: Is there a measure for above and below? Hui Bo replied: Approach from five li away, stop at the central avenue, arrive at five [places] and complete it, go to them and hide their vital energy completely, so there are twenty-five fives, and they have exhausted their output. This is what is called depriving someone of their celestial vitality, not being able to cut off their life and shorten their longevity. Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear it again. Hui Bo said: If one pokes through the bolt hole, they will die inside the house; if one enters the door and pokes, they will die on the floor. Yellow Emperor said: This method is excellent and clear in its teaching. Let me engrave it on a jade plate and make it a valuable treasure, to be passed down for future generations. Use it as a restraining device, so that the people dare not dare to violate it.
