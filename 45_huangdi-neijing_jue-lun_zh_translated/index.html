    厥論

    1: 黃帝問曰：厥之寒熱者何也。歧伯對曰：陽氣衰於下，則為寒厥；陰氣衰於下，則為熱厥。帝曰：熱厥之為熱也，必起於足下者，何也。歧伯曰：陽氣起於足五指之表，陰脈者，集於足下而聚於足心，故陽氣勝，則足下熱也。帝曰：寒厥之為寒也，必從五指而上於膝者，何也。歧伯曰：陰氣起於五指之裏，集於膝下而聚於膝上，故陰氣勝，則從五指至膝上寒，其寒也，不從外，皆從內也。帝曰：寒厥何失而然也。歧伯曰：前陰者，宗筋之所聚，太陰陽明之所合也。春夏則陽氣多而陰氣少，秋冬則陰氣盛而陽氣衰，此人者質壯，以秋冬奪於所用，下氣上爭不能復，精氣溢下，邪氣因從之而上也；氣因於中，陽氣衰，不能滲營其經絡，陽氣日損，陰氣獨在，故手足為之寒也。帝曰：熱厥何如而然也。歧伯曰：酒入於胃，則絡脈滿而經脈虛；脾主為胃行其津液者也，陰氣虛，則陽氣入，陽氣入，則胃不和，胃不和，則精氣竭，精氣竭，則不營其四支也。此人必數醉若飽以入房，氣聚於脾中不得散，酒氣與穀氣相薄，熱盛於中，故熱徧於身內熱而溺赤也。夫酒氣盛而慓悍，腎氣有衰，陽氣獨勝，故手足為之熱也。帝曰：厥或令人腹滿，或令人暴不知人，或至半日遠至一日乃知人者，何也。歧伯曰：陰氣盛於上則下虛，下虛則腹脹滿；陽氣盛於上，則下氣重上而邪氣逆，逆則陽氣亂，陽氣亂則不知人也。 Emperor asked: What is the cause of cold and fever? Hua Tuo replied: When yang qi declines below, it leads to a cold condition; when yin qi declines below, it leads to a feverish condition. Emperor asked: If the fever condition is due to the foot, what is the reason for this? Hua Tuo said: Yang qi arises from the tips of the five fingers and yin qi gathers at the soles and accumulates in the heart of the foot; therefore, if yin qi prevails, there will be heat at the foot. Emperor asked: What is the cause of cold condition? Hua Tuo said: The yin qi arises from the tips of the five fingers and gathers at the knees and accumulates at the knee cap; hence, if yin qi prevails, there will be cold from the tips of the five fingers to the knees. Emperor asked: Why do cold and fever conditions result in failure? Hua Tuo said: The front yin is where the governing vessels gather and the Taiyang Yingming merge. In spring and summer, there is an abundance of yang qi and a lack of yin qi; in autumn and winter, yin qi flourishes while yang qi declines. This person possesses strength, but due to autumn and winter, it takes away what they need, causing the qi to struggle upwards and not be able to recover, leading to the depletion of essence and allowing pathogenic qi to arise from within. The qi is weakened, yang qi cannot nourish its channels and networks, yang qi gradually decreases, yin qi remains alone, and thus the hands and feet become cold. Emperor asked: What is the nature of fever condition? Hua Tuo said: When food enters the stomach, the network and transporting vessels are filled while the meridian vessels are empty; the spleen governs the stomach's transformation of fluids, yin qi is lacking, leading to yang qi intruding. Yang qi intruding leads to an unbalanced stomach, causing essence qi to be exhausted, essence qi being exhausted leads to failure of nourishing the four limbs, this person will drink excessively as if fully sated and go to bed, causing the qi to gather in the spleen without dispersing, the qi of alcohol and grain mingle, leading to fever within the body and appearing red and flushed. The qi of alcohol is abundant but causes the essence of the kidneys to decline, allowing yang qi to prevail, resulting in heat in the hands and feet. Emperor asked: Can cold and fever conditions cause someone to be bloated and not know who they are? Hua Tuo said: When yin qi prevails above, it leads to deficiency below, causing the abdomen to distend; when yang qi prevails above, the qi rises incorrectly, and pathogenic qi rebels, leading to confusion of yang qi, resulting in not knowing who they are.

    2: 帝曰：善。願聞六經脈之厥狀病能也。歧伯曰：巨陽之厥，則腫首頭重，足不能行，發為眴仆；陽明之厥，則癲疾欲走呼，腹滿不得臥面赤而熱，妄見而妄言；少陽之厥，則暴聾頰腫而熱，脇痛，䯒不可以運；太陰之厥，則腹滿䐜脹後不利不欲食，食則嘔不得臥；少陰之厥，則口乾溺赤，腹滿心痛；厥陰之厥，則少腹腫痛，腹脹涇溲不利，好臥屈膝，陰縮腫䯒內熱，盛則寫之，虛則補之，不盛不虛，以經取之。太陰厥逆，䯒急攣，心痛引腹，治主病者，少陰厥逆，虛滿嘔變，下泄清，治主病者；厥陰厥逆，攣腰痛，虛滿前閉譫言，治主病者；三陰俱逆，不得前後，使人手足寒，三日死。太陽厥逆，僵仆嘔血善衂，治主病者；少陽厥逆，機關不利，機關不利者，腰不可以行，項不可以顧，發腸癰不可治驚者死，陽明厥逆喘欬身熱，善驚衂嘔血。手太陰厥逆，虛滿而欬，善嘔沫治主病者；手心主少陰厥逆，心痛引喉身熱死不可治。手太陽厥逆，耳聾泣出，項不可以顧，腰不可以俛仰，治主病者；手陽明少陽厥逆，發喉痺，嗌腫痓，治主病者。 Emperor said: Good. I wish to hear about the six classic meridians and their pathological disorders. The Duke of Shao replied: When there is a blockage in the Jue Yang meridian, there is swelling of the head and neck, difficulty walking, stiffness of the limbs; when there is a blockage in the Yang Ming meridian, one has blurred vision and hearing, feels restless and calls out, abdominal distention with redness and fever, sees hallucinations and speaks nonsense; when there is a blockage in the Shao Yang meridian, one has deafness and facial swelling with redness and fever, pain in the flanks, jaundice that cannot be discharged; when there is a blockage in the Tai Yin meridian, the abdomen is distended and bloated, constipation, does not want to eat, turns red with fever, sees hallucinations and speaks nonsense, with the genitals swollen and internal heat, if full it should be drained, if empty it should be supplemented, if neither full nor empty, it should be treated according to the Classic; when there is a blockage in the Shao Yin meridian, the lower abdomen is distended and painful, with bloody stool and constipation, likes to lie down with knees bent, the genitals are swollen and have internal heat, if full it should be drained, if empty it should be supplemented, if neither full nor empty, it should be treated according to the Classic; when both Shao Yin meridians are inverted, one cannot go forward or backward, causing the hands and feet to feel cold, and death occurs within three days. When there is a blockage in the Tai Yang meridian, the patient is rigid with vomiting of blood, and it is the primary disorder to be treated; when there is a blockage in the Shao Yang meridian, the channels are not smooth, leading to difficulty walking, the neck cannot be turned, abdominal distention without treatment, shock and vomiting of blood lead to death, when there is a blockage in the Yang Ming meridian, one has shortness of breath, panting, fever with redness of the body, shock easily leads to vomiting of blood. When there is a blockage in the hand Tai Yin meridian, there are emptiness and panting, and it is suitable for treating the primary disorder; when there is a blockage in the hand Shao Yin meridian, there is pain in the heart and redness of the throat, leading to death that cannot be treated; when there is a blockage in the hand Tai Yang meridian, deafness and tears overflowing, the neck cannot be turned, it is suitable for treating the primary disorder; when there is a blockage in the hand Yang Ming and Shao Yang meridians, the throat is paralyzed, the tongue and gums are swollen with ulcers, it is suitable for treating the primary disorder.
