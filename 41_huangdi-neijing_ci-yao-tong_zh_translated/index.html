    刺腰痛

    1. 足太陽脈，令人腰痛，引項脊尻背如重狀；刺其郄中太陽正經出血，春無見血。少陽令人腰痛，如以鍼刺其皮中，循循然不可以俛仰，不可以顧。刺少陽成骨之端出血，成骨在膝外廉之骨獨起者，夏無見血。陽明令人腰痛，不可以顧顧如有見者，善悲；刺陽明於䯒前三痏，上下和之出血。秋無見血，足少陰令人腰痛，痛引脊內廉；刺少陰於內踝上二痏，春無見血，出血太多，不可復也。厥陰之脈，令人腰痛，腰中如張弓弩弦；刺厥陰之脈，在腨踵魚腹之外，循之累累然，乃刺之，其病令人善言，默默然不慧，刺之三痏。解脈令人腰痛，痛引肩，目䀮䀮然，時遺溲；刺解脈，在膝筋肉分閒郄外廉之橫脈出血，血變而止。解脈令人腰痛如引帶，常如折腰狀，善恐，刺解脈，在郄中結絡如黍米，刺之血射以黑，見赤血而已。同陰之脈，令人腰痛，痛如小錘居其中，怫然腫；刺同陰之脈，在外踝上絕骨之端，為三痏。陽維之脈，令人腰痛，痛上怫然腫，刺陽維之脈，脈與太陽合腨下間，去地一尺所。衡絡之脈，令人腰痛，不可以俛仰，仰則恐仆，得之舉重傷腰，衡絡絕，惡血歸之；刺之在郄陽筋之間，上郄數寸，衡居為二痏出血。會陰之脈，令人腰痛，痛上漯漯然汗出，汗乾令人欲飲，飲已欲走。刺直陽之脈，上三痏在蹻上郄下五寸橫居，視其盛者出血。飛陽之脈，令人腰痛，痛上拂拂然，甚則悲以恐；刺飛陽之脈，在內踝上五寸，少陰之前，與陰維之會。昌陽之脈，令人腰痛，痛引膺，目䀮䀮然，甚則反折，舌卷不能言；刺內筋為二痏，在內踝上大筋前，太陰後上踝二寸所。散脈令人腰痛而熱，熱甚生煩，腰下如有橫木居其中，甚則遺溲，刺散脈，在膝前骨肉分間，絡外廉束脈為三痏。肉里之脈，令人腰痛，不可以欬，欬則筋縮急，刺肉里之脈為二痏，在太陽之外，少陽絕骨之後。 Foot Too Yang channel, causing lower back pain that refers to the shoulder blades and makes one feel heavy; puncture the Shu-point of the Too Yang channel at the Yuan level, and there will be bleeding without seeing blood in spring. The Shao Yin causes lower back pain, similar to inserting an awl through the skin; when one leans forward or looks back, there is no relief. Puncture the Shao Yin at the Sinew Channel point on the inner side of the knee, and in spring there will be bleeding without seeing blood. The Yang Ming causes lower back pain, preventing one from looking back as if looking at something; puncture the Yang Ming at the Lung channel point of the first three Jing-well points, connect the blood and bleed. In autumn, the foot Too Yin channel causes lower back pain that refers to the inside of the lumbar region; puncture the Too Yin at the point two cun above the inner ankle bone, in spring there will be excessive bleeding, which cannot be stopped. The Kidney Yin channel causes lower back pain, like a tight bowstring; puncture the Kidney Yin channel, outside the Lower Sea of Qi and Shu points of the Bladder, accumulate them and then puncture, after which the disease will cause one to speak eloquently but with confusion. Puncture the Luo-connecting channel, causing lower back pain that refers to the shoulder, with blurred vision; puncture the Luo-connecting channel at the Bladder channel point on the crease of the knee, connect the blood and stop it from bleeding. The Luo-connecting channel causes lower back pain as if dragging something, always feeling like the waist is bent, causing fear; puncture the Luo-connecting channel, in the area of the sinew and muscle between the Shu points of the Governing Vessel and the Conception Vessel, connect the blood and stop it from bleeding. The same Yin channel causes lower back pain, like a small axe inside; puncture the Same Yin channel at the point above the outer ankle where the bone ends, for three cun. The Yang Wei channel causes lower back pain, with the pain above feeling tight and swollen; puncture the Yang Wei channel, between the Too Yang channel and the Bladder channel, one foot's distance away. The Conception Vessel channel causes lower back pain, preventing one from looking up as if afraid; puncture the Conception Vessel at the point of the sinew channel on the inner side of the knee, connect the blood and stop it from bleeding. The Willow (Flying) Yang channel causes lower back pain, with pain above feeling like brushing something; puncture the Flying Yang channel at the point five cun above the Shu points of the Too Yin channel and the Shao Yin channel. The Pure Yang channel of the Stomach causes lower back pain, referring to the chest and making one feel like bending over; puncture the Interior sinew channels for two cun, at the point in front of the inner knee where the Shao Yin meets the Yang Wei channel. Scattered (Qi) channel causes lower back pain with fever, and excessive heat causing irritability; with a feeling as if there is a horizontal board inside the lower back, and in severe cases, there will be excessive bleeding due to injury. Puncture the scattered (Qi) channel at the point between the sinew channels of the Stomach and Spleen, connect the blood and stop it from bleeding. The muscle channel causes lower back pain, preventing one from looking up; puncture the muscle channel for two cun, outside the Too Yang channel and the Shao Yin channel.

    2. 腰痛俠脊而痛至頭几几然，目䀮䀮欲僵仆；刺足太陽郄中出血，腰痛上寒，刺足太陽陽明；上熱，刺足厥陰。不可以俛仰，刺足少陽。中熱而喘，刺足少陰，刺郄中出血。腰痛上寒不可顧，刺足陽明；上熱，刺足太陰，中熱而喘，刺足少陰。大便難刺足少陰。少腹滿，刺足厥陰。如折不可以俛仰不可舉，刺足太陽。引脊內廉，刺足少陰。腰痛引少腹控䏚，不可以仰，刺腰尻交者，兩髁胂上。以月生死為痏數，發鍼立已，左取右，右取左。 Acute lower back pain with radiating pain to the head, vision blurring and wanting to stiffen; bleeding after needling at the Yongquan point, lower back pain becoming cold, needle at the Yongquan and Yangming points. Feverishness, needle at the Weiguan point. Not to be treated in a supine position, needle at the ShaoYang point. Short of breath while feeling feverish, needle at the ShaoYin point and Xuejin point. Difficulty passing feces, needle at the ShaoYin point. Bloated lower abdomen, needle at the Weiguan point. If unable to straighten due to lower back pain, needle at the Yangming point. Feverishness, needle at the Taiyin point. Short of breath while feeling feverish, needle at the ShaoYin point. Difficulty passing feces, needle at the ShaoYin point. Unable to straighten and not allowed to recline, needle at the Yangming point. Guide the Du channel internally and needle at the ShaoYin point. Lower back pain radiating to the lower abdomen, not allowed to recline, needle at the intersection of the Bladder and Governor Vessel channels, and both the Huaivo and Pangguangshu points. Count by the lunar calendar for treatment number, stand up after moxibustion, left hand takes right and vice versa.
