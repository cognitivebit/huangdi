    背腧

    1: 黃帝問于歧伯曰：願聞五藏之腧，出於背者。歧伯曰：背中大腧，在杼骨之端，肺腧在三焦之間，心腧在五焦之間，膈腧在七焦之間，肝腧在九焦之間，脾腧在十一焦之間，腎腧在十四焦之間。皆挾脊相去三寸所，則欲得而驗之，按其處，應在中而痛解，乃其輸也。灸之則可刺之則不可。氣盛則寫之，虛則補之。以火補者，毋吹其火，須自滅也；以火寫之，疾吹其火，傳其艾，須其火滅也。 Emperor Yellow asked Duke of Shou: I want to hear about the five viscera that emerge from the back. Duke of Shou said: The large intestine is in the middle of the back, between the juncture of the spine and the lung channel; the heart is in the middle of the five channels, between the lung and the large intestine channel; the stomach is in the middle of the seven channels, between the spleen and the heart channel; the liver is in the middle of the nine channels, between the gallbladder and the spleen channel; the spleen is in the middle of the eleven channels, between the stomach and the kidney channel; the kidney is in the middle of the fourteen channels, between the bladder and the liver channel. If you press them all at the place where they are tethered to the spine, about three inches away, and you want to test them, then press the location, and it should hurt and resolve in the middle, which indicates an outflow. Acupuncture is possible, but moxibustion is not. If there is fullness, use moxibustion; if there is emptiness, use supplementation. If you use fire to supplement, do not blow on the fire, it must extinguish itself; if you use fire to drain, blow the fire quickly and pass the aphis, make sure the fire goes out.
