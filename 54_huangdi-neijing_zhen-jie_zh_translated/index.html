    鍼解

    1. 黃帝問曰：願聞九鍼之解，虛實之道。歧伯對曰：刺虛則實之者，鍼下熱也，氣實乃熱也。滿而泄之者，鍼下寒也，氣虛乃寒也。菀陳則除之者，出惡血也。邪勝則虛之者，出鍼勿按。徐而疾則實者，徐出鍼而疾按之。疾而徐則虛者，疾出鍼而徐按之。言實與虛者，寒溫氣多少也。若無若有者，疾不可知也。察後與先者，知病先後也。為虛與實者，工勿失其法。若得若失者，離其法也。虛實之要，九鍼最妙者，為其各有所宜也。補寫之時者，與氣開闔相合也。九鍼之名，各不同形者，鍼窮其所當補寫也。 Yellow Emperor asked, I would like to hear the explanation of the nine needles, the truth of the Way. Guanpu answered, Sticking the real, it becomes virtual; the needle penetrates heat, and the qi is real. Filling and discharging it, the needle penetrates cold, and the qi is virtual. Dispersing and eliminating, expelling evil blood, remove the needle. When the pathogenic factor prevails in the virtual, do not press the needle. Gradually pressing the real, quickly press the needle; quickly pressing the virtual, gradually press the needle. Speaking of real and virtual, it is a matter of the amount of qi being hot or cold. If there is none or if there is, it is difficult to know how fast or slow. Discern the later and earlier, knowing the order of disease progression. For the real and virtual, do not fail to use the method. When obtaining or losing it, separate from the method. The essence of real and virtual, the supreme skill of the nine needles, each has its own way to apply. Supplementing and opening at the time, correspond with qi's opening and closing. The name of the nine needles, each having a different shape, exhausts the needles according to what should be supplemented and opened.

    2. 刺實須其虛者，留鍼陰氣隆至，乃去鍼也。刺虛須其實者，陽氣隆至，鍼下熱乃去鍼也。經氣已至，慎守勿失者，勿變更也。深淺在志者，知病之內外也。近遠如一者，深淺其候等也。如臨深淵者，不敢墯也。手如握虎者，欲其壯也。神無營於眾物者，靜志觀病人無左右視也。義無邪下者，欲端以正也。必正其神者，欲瞻病人目制其神，令氣易行也。 The needle must penetrate the virtual aspect, leaving the subtle energy to rise fully. When the needle penetrates the real aspect, the vital energy descends and the needle is removed. Be careful when the energy has arrived, and do not change or alter anything. Know the shallow and deep aspects of the disease, as well as its external and internal manifestations. Treat the near and far equally, and balance the timing of your approach. When faced with a difficult case, be cautious and hesitant. Hold the needle as if grasping a tiger, wishing for strength. Be free from distractions and maintain focus on the patient. Uphold righteousness without deviation, seeking to align the mind with the truth. By regulating the patient's spirit, you can observe their eyes and ensure that their energy flows freely.

    3. 所謂三里者，下膝三寸也。所謂跗之者，舉膝分易見也。巨虛者蹻足，䯒獨陷者。下廉者，陷下者也。 The phrase three li refers to a distance of three inches kneeling. The term zhi refers to the act of lifting the knee, making it easily visible. If something is juxu, it is like a huge emptiness and if someone is lun zhu, they are alone and sinking. When someone is in a state of shi, they are sinking downwards.

    4. 帝曰：余聞九鍼，上應天地四時陰陽，願聞其方，令可傳於後世以為常也。歧伯曰：夫一天二地三人四時五音六律七星八風九野，身形亦應之，鍼各有所宜，故曰九鍼。人皮應天，人肉應地，人脈應人，人筋應時，人聲應音，人陰陽合氣應律，人齒面目應星，人出入氣應風，人九竅三百六十五絡應野。故一鍼皮，二鍼肉，三鍼脈，四鍼筋，五鍼骨，六鍼調陰陽，七鍼益精，八鍼除風，九鍼通九竅，除三百六十五節氣，此之謂各有所主也。 The Emperor said: I have heard of the Nine Needles, which respond to heaven and earth, and the four seasons, yin and yang. I wish to hear their method so that it can be passed down to future generations as a constant principle. Duke Xing stated: The body has one heaven, two earths, three people, four seasons, five sounds, six laws, seven stars, eight winds, nine wildernesses. Each needle has its proper application, so they are called the Nine Needles. People's skin responds to heaven, their flesh to earth, their pulse to people, their sinews to time, their voice to sound, their yin and yang to regulating qi, their teeth and eyes to stars, their inhaling and exhaling of qi to winds, and their nine orifices and three hundred sixty-five channels respond to the wilderness. Therefore, one needle for skin, two needles for flesh, three needles for pulse, four needles for sinews, five needles for bones, six needles for regulating yin and yang, seven needles for nourishing essence, eight needles for dispersing wind, nine needles for connecting the nine orifices, and expelling the three hundred sixty-five disharmonious qi; these are each responsible for their own application.

    5. 人心意應八風，人氣應天，人髮齒耳目五聲，應五音六律，人陰陽脈血氣應地，人肝目應之九，九竅三百六十五。人一以觀動靜天二以候五色七星應之以候髮毋澤五音一以候宮商角徵羽六律有餘不足應之二地一以候高下有餘九野一節俞應之以候閉節三人變一分人候齒泄多血少十分角之變五分以候緩急六分不足三分寒關節第九分四時人寒溫燥濕四時一應之以候相反一四方各作解。 The human heart and mind follow the eight winds, energy follows heaven, human hair, teeth, ears, eyes, five sounds, follow five notes and six rhythms. Human yin and yang pulse, blood and qi follow earth. Human liver and eyes follow nine, nine orifices, 365. Human observation of movement and stillness in heaven, follow the changing color of the five stars. Human hair follows the five notes, one following the palace, market, trumpet, and imply wings. Six rhythms have some insufficient to follow. One follows high and low, nine wilderness, one knot. Human variation following one division, follows the candidate teeth leak blood more ten divisions of variation. Five variations follow observing delay, urgency, six variations do not suffice three variations cold joints number nine parts. In four seasons, people follow the changing heat and humidity. Four directions each make a solution.
