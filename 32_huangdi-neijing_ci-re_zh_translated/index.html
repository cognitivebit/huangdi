    刺熱

    1. 肝熱病者，小便先黃，腹痛多臥身熱，熱爭，則狂言及驚，脇滿痛，手足躁，不得安臥，庚辛甚，甲乙大汗，氣逆則庚辛死，刺足厥陰少陽，其逆則頭痛員員，脈引衝頭也。心熱病者，先不樂，數日乃熱，熱爭則卒心痛，煩悶善嘔，頭痛面赤無汗，壬癸甚，丙丁大汗，氣逆則壬癸死，刺手少陰太陽。脾熱病者，先頭重頰痛，煩心顏青，欲嘔身熱，熱爭則腰痛不可用俛仰，腹滿泄，兩頷痛，甲乙甚，戊己大汗，氣逆則甲乙死，刺足太陰陽明。肺熱病者，先淅然厥，起毫毛惡風寒，舌上黃身熱。熱爭，則喘欬，痛走胸膺背，不得大息，頭痛不堪，汗出而寒，丙丁甚，庚辛大汗，氣逆則丙丁死，刺手太陰陽明，出血如大豆，立已。腎熱病者，先腰痛䯒痠，苦渴數飲，身熱，熱爭，則項痛而強，䯒寒且痠，足下熱，不欲言，其逆，則項痛員員澹澹然，戊己甚，壬癸大汗，氣逆則戊己死，刺足少陰太陽，諸汗者，至其所勝日，汗出也。肝熱病者，左頰先赤，心熱病者，顏先赤，脾熱病者，鼻先赤，肺熱病者，右頰先赤，腎熱病者，頤先赤，病雖未發，見赤色者刺之，名曰治未病。熱病從部所起者，至期而已；其刺之反者，三周而已；重逆則死。諸當汗者，至其所勝日，汗大出也。諸治熱病，以飲之寒水，乃刺之，必寒衣之，居止寒處，身寒而止也。 Patients with hepatic fever have yellow urine first, abdominal pain with much lying down, body heat and restlessness leading to madness and strange speech, pain in the flank, restless hands and feet, and difficulty in resting. In the case of pisces, leo, scorpio and aquarius, there is great sweating. If qi rises, patients will die of pisces, leo, or scorpio. Use foot-tau (Jiao) needling to relieve the rising qi; the reference is to headache.

    Patients with heart fever initially feel uncomfortable, and after several days, they have a fever. Restlessness leads to sudden heart pain, agitation and frequent vomiting, headaches with red faces and no sweating. In the cases of taurus, dragon and monkey, there is great sweating. If qi rises, patients will die of taurus, dragon or monkey. Use hand-tau (Jiao) needling for the Conception channel and Yangming.

    Patients with spleen fever initially have headaches and pain around the mouth, are annoyed and feel like vomiting, have a feverish body, restlessness leads to lower back pain, inability to bend over or stand upright, abdominal fullness with involuntary release of flatus, and pain in both corners of the mouth. In the cases of aries, libra and sagittarius, there is great sweating. If qi rises, patients will die of aries, libra or sagittarius. Use foot-tau (Jiao) needling for the Yinwei channel and Yangming.

    Patients with lung fever initially have a sunken appearance, bad odor from their breath, and yellow bodies. Restlessness leads to shortness of breath, pain in the chest and back, inability to take deep breaths, headaches, sweating but with cold sweat, taurus and dragon have great sweating. If qi rises, patients will die of taurus or dragon. Use hand-tau (Jiao) needling for the Conception channel and Yangming; bleeding occurs like that from large beans, and once it has stopped, the patient can no longer stand.

    Patients with kidney fever initially have lower back pain, craving for fluids but drinking little, a feverish body, restlessness leads to neck pain and stiffness, cold and cramping in the region of the kidneys, hot feet and reluctance to speak. In the cases of virgo, ox and horse, there is great sweating. If qi rises, patients will die of virgo, ox or horse. Use foot-tau (Jiao) needling for the Shao yang channel and Yangming. When the treatment reaches its peak, sweating occurs.

    In cases of hepatic fever, the left cheek is initially red; in heart fever, the face is initially red; in spleen fever, the nose is initially red; in lung fever, the right cheek is initially red; in kidney fever, the chin is initially red. Even before the onset of symptoms, needling should be applied to these areas. This method of treatment is called treating before the disease appears. If the fever originates from a specific area and reaches its peak, the needling will have been completed within three cycles; if qi rises again, death will occur. When the sweating treatment reaches its peak, great sweating occurs.

    All patients with fevers should be treated with cold water to drink, and needling must be done in a cold environment. Patients should wear warm clothing and rest in cool places.

When the three below sink on the back, it is central.
