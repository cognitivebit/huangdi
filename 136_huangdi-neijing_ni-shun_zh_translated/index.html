    逆順

    1: 黃帝問于伯高曰：余聞氣有逆順，脈有盛衰，刺有大約，可得聞乎？伯高曰：氣之逆順者，所以應天地陰陽四時五行也；脈之盛衰者，所以候血氣之虛實有餘不足；刺之大約者，必明知病之可刺，與其未可刺，與其已不可刺也。 Emperor Yellow asked Confucius: I have heard that there is a change of Yin and Yang, the rise and fall of the pulse, and the large and small needles, can you hear about it? Confucius said: The change of Yin and Yang in Qi is related to the adaptation to heaven, earth, yin and yang, and the four seasons and five elements; the rise and fall of the pulse are related to the diagnosis of blood and qi, whether there is abundance or deficiency. The large and small needles refer to understanding the disease and knowing when to use acupuncture, distinguishing between when it can be used and when it cannot be used, and when it has already been too late for acupuncture.

    2: 黃帝曰：候之奈何？伯高曰：兵法曰無迎逢逢之氣，無擊堂堂之陣。刺法曰：無刺熇熇之熱，無刺漉漉之汗，無刺渾渾之脈，無刺病與脈相逆者。 Yellow Emperor asked: What about waiting? Duke of Zhou said: The art of war is to not meet the enemy head-on, and the art of needles is to not pierce with a burning heat, not pierce with a sweating dew, not pierce with a full pulse, and not pierce in those who have a sickness that contradicts their pulse.

    3: 黃帝曰：候其可刺奈何？伯高曰：上工，刺其未生者也；其次，刺其未盛者也；其次，刺其已衰者也。下工，刺其方襲者也；與其形之盛者也；與其病之與脈相逆者也。故曰：方其盛也，勿敢毀傷，刺其已衰，事必大昌。故曰：上工治未病，不治已病，此之謂也。 Yellow Emperor asked, What about puncturing at the right moment? Duke of Zhou replied, In major cases, it is appropriate to puncture before the growth; in secondary cases, after the growth but before the decline; and in tertiary cases, after the decline. In minor cases, puncture only when there are specific signs. Along with the signs of the constitution, do not dare to injure at the moment of abundance; if puncturing after decline, one can achieve great success. Thus, he said, At the time of abundance, do not dare to damage, and puncture after decline, and it will surely be a great achievement. Duke of Zhou added, In major cases, treating before the onset of disease, and not treating after the onset of disease, this is what I mean.
