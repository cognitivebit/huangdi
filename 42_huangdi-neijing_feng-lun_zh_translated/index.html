    風論

    1: 黃帝問曰：風之傷人也，或為寒熱，或為熱中，或為寒中，或為癘風，或為偏枯，或為風也；其病各異，其名不同，或內至五藏六府，不知其解，願聞其說。歧伯對曰：風氣藏於皮膚之閒，內不得通，外不得泄；風者，善行而數變，腠理開則洒然寒，閉則熱而悶，其寒也，則衰食飲，其熱也，則消肌肉，故使人怢慄而不能食，名曰寒熱。風氣與陽明入胃循脈而上至目內眥，其人肥則風氣不得外泄，則為熱中而目黃；人瘦，則外泄而寒，則為寒中而泣出。風氣與太陽俱入行諸脈俞，散於分肉之閒，與衛氣相干，其道不利，故使肌肉憤䐜而有瘍；衛氣有所凝而不行，故其肉有不仁也。癘者有榮氣熱胕，其氣不清，故使其鼻柱壞而色敗，皮膚瘍潰。風寒客於脈而不去，名曰癘風，或名曰寒熱。 Yellow Emperor asked: Wind can harm people, and it can be due to cold-heat, heat alone, cold alone, or wind stroke, or dryness, or just wind. The diseases are different and have different names. Sometimes it affects the five organs and six palaces, and one does not know how to treat it. I hope to hear your explanation.

    The Duke answered: Wind energy is stored in the skin's slackness, and it cannot be dispersed internally or externally. Wind is changeable and often causes chills and sweating. If it is cold, one loses strength and appetite; if it is hot, the muscles weaken, causing the person to tremble and unable to eat - this is called cold-heat.

    Wind energy and Yang Ming enter the stomach and spleen, ascending through the meridians to the eye's surface. If a person is fat, wind energy cannot be released externally, leading to heat in the eyes; if a person is thin, wind is released externally, causing cold in the eyes and tearing.

    Wind energy and Yang Qi both enter and circulate through the meridians, dispersing into the muscle's slackness and interfering with defensive Qi. If the pathway is not smooth, it causes muscle spasms and sores; if defensive Qi congeals and does not move, the muscles become unyielding.

    Strokes are caused by excessive heat and unclear Qi, damaging the nasal bridge and causing the skin to degenerate. Wind-cold lingers in the meridians and does not leave, known as wind-cold or cold-heat.

    2: 以春甲乙傷於風者為肝風；以夏丙丁傷於風者為心風；以以季夏戊己傷於邪者為脾風；秋庚辛中於邪者為肺風；以冬壬癸中於邪者為腎風。風中五藏六府之俞亦為藏府之風，各入其門戶所中，則為偏風。風氣循風府而上，則為腦風；風入係頭，則為目風眼寒；飲酒中風，則為漏風；入房汗出中風，則為內風；新沐中風則為首風；久風入中，則為腸風飱泄；外在腠理，則為泄風。故風者，百病之長也，至其變化，乃為他病也，無常方，然致有風氣也。 Those harmed by spring's wind are afflicted with liver wind; those harmed by summer's wind are afflicted with heart wind; those harmed in the period of midsummer, by metallic influences, are afflicted with spleen wind; in autumn, within the phase of adverse influences, those afflicted are said to have lung wind; and in winter, within the phase of adverse influences, those afflicted are said to have kidney wind. Wind affecting the five viscera and six bowels is also called viscera-bowel wind, each entering its gate, resulting in localized or offsetting wind. If wind Qi ascends through the wind palace, it is brain wind; if it enters the head, it is eye wind and eye cold; when drinking, it is leak wind; when inside a room and sweating, it is interior wind; after bathing, it is neck wind; if prolonged, it becomes intestinal wind with diarrhea; outside in the hairy regions, it is a wind of discharge. Thus, wind is the root of myriad diseases. When its transformations become varied and diverse, they may be called epidemic diseases, with no constant treatment, yet they all involve wind Qi.

    3: 帝曰：五藏風之形狀不同者何，願聞其診及其病能。歧伯曰：肺風之狀，多汗惡風，色皏然白，時欬短氣，晝日則差，暮則甚，診在眉上，其色白；心風之狀，多汗惡風，焦絕善怒嚇，赤色，病甚則言不可快，診在口，其色赤；肝風之狀，多汗惡風，善悲色微蒼，嗌乾善怒，時憎女子，診在目下，其色青；脾風之狀，多汗惡風，身體怠墯，四支不欲動，色薄微黃，不嗜食，診在鼻上，其色黃；腎風之狀，多汗惡風，面痝然浮腫，脊痛不能正立，其色炱，隱曲不利，診在肌上，其色黑，胃風之狀，頸多汗惡風，食飲不下，鬲塞不通，腹善滿，失衣則䐜脹，食寒則泄，診形瘦而腹大；首風之狀頭面多汗惡風當先風一日，則病甚頭痛不可，以出內至其風日，則病少愈；漏風之狀，或多汗，常不可單衣，食則汗出，甚則身汗，喘息惡風，衣常濡，口乾善渴，不能勞事；泄風之狀，多汗，汗出泄衣上，口中乾上漬，其風不能勞事，身體盡痛則寒。帝曰：善。 Emperor: What are the different manifestations of the Five Winds, and I wish to hear about their diagnosis and treatment. Taiyi said: The Lung wind manifestation has excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, with a pale and white complexion, coughing with short breath during the day, worsening at night, diagnose it on the forehead, and its color is white. The Heart wind manifestation has excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, becomes angry easily with a red complexion, speech becomes difficult when sick, diagnose it on the mouth, and its color is red. The Liver wind manifestation has excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, prone to sadness with a light blue complexion, becomes angry easily dislikes women, diagnose it in the eyes, and its color is green. The Spleen wind manifestation has excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, body weak and unwilling to move limbs, pale and yellowish complexion, dislikes food, diagnose it on the nose, and its color is yellow. Kidney wind manifestation has excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, facial swelling and abdominal distension, back pain unable to stand straight, its color is purple, curvature not beneficial, diagnose it on the flesh, and its color is black. Stomach wind manifestation has neck with excessive sweating and aversion to cold wind, difficulty swallowing food and drink, stomach full, unbuttoning clothes results in vomiting, cold leads to diarrhea, diagnose it with a thin appearance but abdomen swollen. Wind on the head and face manifestation has excess sweating and aversion to cold wind, with the first symptoms appearing in the head and face, if sick, headache is unbearable, with the progression of the disease, it becomes less severe. Leak wind manifestation may have excessive sweating, often unable to wear clothes, sweats when eating, worsens severely with a pale complexion, always damp clothes, mouth dry and thirsty, unable to do strenuous work. Discharge wind manifestation has excessive sweating, discharge of sweat and discharge on clothing, salty taste in the mouth, unable to do strenuous work, body hurts severely when cold. Emperor: Good.
