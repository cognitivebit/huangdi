    上古天真論

    1. 昔在黃帝，生而神靈，弱而能言，幼而徇齊，長而敦敏，成而登天。迺問於天師曰：余聞上古之人，春秋皆度百歲，而動作不衰；今時之人，年半百而動作皆衰者，時世異耶，人將失之耶？歧伯對曰：上古之人，其知道者，法於陰陽，和於術數，食飲有節，起居有常，不妄作勞，故能形與神俱，而盡終其天年，度百歲乃去。今時之人不然也，以酒為漿，以妄為常，醉以入房，以欲竭其精，以耗散其真，不知持滿，不時御神，務快其心，逆於生樂，起居無節，故半百而衰也。 In ancient times, during the Yellow Emperor's era, there was an individual born with exceptional spiritual prowess. Though weak at birth, he could speak fluently, as a young child he imitated Qi, growing up he showed great intelligence and wisdom, and in his later years, ascended to the level of a sage.

    He questioned the Master of Heaven, saying: I have heard that people from ancient times, during the Spring and Autumn period, lived to be over one hundred years old without decaying physically. However, people today, even if they live to be fifty years old, show signs of decay. Is this not a sign of the times? Are people soon to lose this ability?

    The Duke replied: In ancient times, those who understood the way of life, relied on the principles of Yin and Yang, harmonized their actions with the arts of numerology, maintained discipline in diet and dwelling, avoided unnecessary labor, and as a result were able to sustain both their bodies and spirits, reaching their full lifespan of one hundred years before passing. People today do not follow this path; they rely on wine as glue, indulge in frivolous behavior, become intoxicated in their chambers, exhaust their essence through lust, and dissipate their true nature through excessive activity, without understanding the importance of preserving their vital energy, failing to govern their spirits properly, and hastening their decline. This is why people today, even if they live to be fifty years old, show signs of decay.

    1. 夫上古聖人之教下也，皆謂之虛邪賊風，避之有時，恬惔虛无，真氣從之，精神內守，病安從來。是以志閑而少欲，心安而不懼，形勞而不倦，氣從以順，各從其欲，皆得所願。故美其食，任其服，樂其俗，高下不相慕，其民故曰朴。是以嗜欲不能勞其目，淫邪不能惑其心，愚智賢不肖不懼於物，故合於道。所以能年皆度百歲，而動作不衰者，以其德全不危也。 Since ancient sages' teaching, all referred to it as empty, evil, counterfeit wind, avoiding it at times, being tranquil and empty-minded, following the genuine energy, guarding the spirit within, health coming by itself. Therefore, cherishing desires but keeping one's willpower in check, having a peaceful mind without fear, exerting oneself physically but not becoming weary, following one's own inclinations, all getting what they wish. Thus, praising their food, allowing their medicine, enjoying their customs, not coveting high or low positions, the people of this country call it primitive. This is why desire cannot exhaust their eyesight, lust and evil cannot confuse their minds, ignorance of wisdom but sages do not fear things, so they follow the Dao. Therefore, able to live long, all reaching a hundred years, with movements not decaying, this is because their virtue is complete and not in danger also.

    2. 帝曰：人年老而無子者，材力盡邪，將天數然也。歧伯曰：女子七歲，腎氣盛，齒更髮長；二七而天癸至，任脈通，太衝脈盛，月事以時下，故有子；三七，腎氣平均，故真牙生而長極；四七，筋骨堅，髮長極，身體盛壯；五七，陽明脈衰，面始焦，髮始墮；六七，三陽脈衰於上，面皆焦，髮始白；七七，任脈虛，太衝脈衰少，天癸竭，地道不通，故形壞而無子也。丈夫八歲，腎氣實，髮長齒更；二八，腎氣盛，天癸至，精氣溢寫，陰陽和，故能有子；三八，腎氣平均，筋骨勁強，故真牙生而長極；四八，筋骨隆盛，肌肉滿壯；五八，腎氣衰，髮墮齒槁；六八，陽氣衰竭於上，面焦，髮鬢頒白；七八，肝氣衰，筋不能動，天癸竭，精少，腎藏衰，形體皆極；八八，則齒髮去，腎者主水，受五藏六府之精而藏之，故五藏盛，乃能寫。今五藏皆衰，筋骨解墮，天癸盡矣。故髮鬢白，身體重，行步不正，而無子耳。帝曰：有其年已老而有子者何也。歧伯曰：此其天壽過度，氣脈常通，而腎氣有餘也。此雖有子，男不過盡八八，女不過盡七七，而天地之精氣皆竭矣。帝曰：夫道者年皆百數，能有子乎。歧伯曰：夫道者能卻老而全形，身年雖壽，能生子也。 Emperor said: A person becomes old without offspring, and their vital energy has been exhausted, just as their time is about to end. Duke said: A girl is seven years old, her kidney energy is flourishing, her teeth change and hair grows long; at 27 when the heaven's stems are Jīn, the conduits are open, the Greater Yín and Lesser Yǐn vessels have abundant energy, and the menstruation occurs regularly, so there will be offspring. At 37, the kidney energy is balanced, so true teeth grow and become extremely long; at 47, the tendons and bones are strong, hair grows extremely long, and the body is vigorous; at 57, the Yang Míng vessels decline, the face becomes dull, and hair starts to fall out. At 67, the three Yang vessels decline in the upper part, the face becomes dull, and hair starts to turn white. At 77, the conduits are empty, the Greater Yín and Lesser Yǐn vessels decline somewhat, the heaven's stems have reached their limit, and the earthly paths are not open, so the body deteriorates and there is no offspring. A man at eight years old has kidney energy that is flourishing, his hair grows long and his teeth change; at 28, the kidney energy is abundant when the heaven's stems are Jīn, and the essence energy overflows, the Yin and Yang are harmonious, so there can be offspring. At 38, the kidney energy is balanced, the tendons and bones have strong power, so true teeth grow and become extremely long; at 48, the tendons and bones are flourishing, the muscles are full and vigorous; at 58, the kidney energy declines, hair falls out and teeth weaken; at 68, the Yang energy declines in the upper part, the face becomes dull, and hair turns white. At 78, the liver energy declines, tendons cannot move, the heaven's stems have reached their limit, and essence is scarce, the kidney vessels decline, so the body deteriorates completely and there is no offspring. Emperor asked: Why can a person still have offspring when they are old? Duke said: This is because their lifespan exceeded what was destined, and their conduits remain open, while their kidney energy has some remaining. Although there are offspring, boys do not exceed eight at eight years old, and girls do not exceed seven at seven years old, and the essence energy of heaven and earth has been exhausted. Emperor asked: If the Dao exists, can a person have offspring when they are one hundred? Duke said: The Dao enables one to age while still maintaining their body's integrity, even though their lifespan is one hundred, they can have offspring.

    3. 黃帝曰：余聞上古有真人者，提挈天地，把握陰陽，呼吸精氣，獨立守神，肌肉若一，故能壽敝天地，无有終時，此其道生。中古之時，有至人者，淳德全道，和於陰陽，調於四時，去世離俗，積精全神，游行天地之間，視聽八達之外，此蓋益其壽命而強者也，亦歸於真人。其次有聖人者，處天地之和，從八風之理，適嗜欲於世俗之間，无恚嗔之心，行不欲離於世，被服章，舉不欲觀於俗，外不勞形於事，內无思想之患，以恬愉為務，以自得為功，形體不敝，精神不散，亦可以百數。其次有賢人者，法則天地，象似日月，辯列星辰，逆從陰陽，分別四時，將從上古合同於道，亦可使益壽而有極時。 Yellow Emperor said: I have heard that in ancient times, there were true beings who held up Heaven and Earth, controlled Yin and Yang, breathed the divine qi, stood alone guarding the gods, their muscles as one, thus they could nourish and sustain Heaven and Earth, without an end, this is the beginning of their way. In ancient times, there were excellent people, pure virtue, complete the Way, harmonious with Yin and Yang, regulated by the four seasons, departed from worldly desires, accumulating qi and refining their spirit, roaming between Heaven and Earth, seeing and hearing beyond the eight limits, this is indeed to strengthen their life and longevity, also returning to true beings. Next are sages who dwell in harmony with Heaven and Earth, follow the principles of the eight winds, adapt their desires within worldly affairs, without resentment or anger in their hearts, they act without desiring to leave the world, wear but do not show off, lift but do not want to be seen in worldliness, externally not laboring their bodies in matters, internally without troubled thoughts, cherishing tranquility as their duty, and self-realization as their achievement, their body and spirit do not decay, and they can also extend their lives by hundreds. There are also wise people who follow the laws of Heaven and Earth, resembling the sun and moon, differentiating stars and constellations, following Yin and Yang, dividing the four seasons, joining from ancient times to combine with the Way, also able to strengthen their longevity and have an extreme limit.
