    刺要論

    1: 黃帝問曰：願聞刺要。歧伯對曰：病有浮沈，刺有淺深，各至其理，無過其道；過之則內傷，不及則生外壅，壅則邪從之。淺深不得，反為大賊，內動五藏後生大病。故曰：病有在毫毛腠理者，有在皮膚者，有在肌肉者，有在脈者，有在筋者，有在骨者，有在髓者。是故刺毫毛腠理，無傷皮，皮傷則內動肺，肺動則秋病溫瘧，泝泝然寒慄；刺皮，無傷肉，肉傷則內動脾，脾動則七十二日四季之月，病腹脹煩，不嗜食；刺肉，無傷脈，脈傷則內動心，心動則夏病心痛；刺脈，無傷筋，筋傷則內動肝，肝動則春病熱而筋弛；刺筋無傷骨，骨傷則內動腎，腎動則冬病脹腰痛；刺骨，無傷髓，髓傷則銷鑠，胻酸，體解㑊然不去矣。 The Yellow Emperor asked, I would like to hear about acupuncture. Tong Fahui replied, Disease has a floating and sinking sensation, and acupuncture has shallow and deep insertion. Each one should be at its proper level without exceeding its limits; otherwise, it will cause internal injury, not reaching the appropriate depth will cause external obstruction, and obstruction will lead to the invasion of evil qi. If shallow and deep cannot be controlled, it becomes a great danger, causing internal movement of the five zang organs and leading to serious disease. Thus, disease can be in the hair follicles, skin, muscles, vessels, tendons, and bones. Therefore, acupuncture the hair follicles without harming the skin; if the skin is harmed, it will cause internal movement of the lung, leading to autumn diseases of fever and chills. If you acupuncture the skin without harming the muscles, but the muscles are harmed, it will cause internal movement of the spleen, leading to diseases occurring every seventy-two days in all four seasons, abdominal distension and discomfort, and lack of appetite. Acupuncture the muscles without harming the vessels; if the vessels are harmed, it will cause internal movement of the heart, leading to summer diseases of heart pain. If you acupuncture the vessels without harming the tendons, but the tendons are harmed, it will cause internal movement of the liver, leading to spring diseases of fever and tendon weakness. Acupuncture the tendons without harming the bones; if the bones are harmed, it will cause internal movement of the kidney, leading to winter diseases of lower back pain. If you acupuncture the bones without harming the marrow, but the marrow is harmed, it will lead to waste and body aches that do not go away.
