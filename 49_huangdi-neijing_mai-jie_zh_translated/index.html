    1. 太陽所謂腫腰脽痛者，正月太陽寅，寅，太陽也，正月陽氣出在上，而陰氣盛，陽未得自次也，故腫腰脽痛也。病偏虛為跛者，正月陽氣凍解地氣而出也，所謂偏虛者冬寒頗有不足者，故偏虛為跛也。所謂強上引背者，陽氣大上而爭故強上也。所謂耳鳴者，陽氣萬物盛上而躍，故耳鳴也。所謂甚則狂巔疾者，陽盡在上，而陰氣從下，下虛上實，故狂巔疾也。所謂浮為聾者，皆在氣也。所謂入中為瘖者，陽盛已衰，故為瘖也。內奪而厥，則為瘖俳，此腎虛也。少陰不至者，厥也。 Regarding those with pain in the waist and loins due to the sun, it is the first month of the sun [cycle], and during the sun's cycle, when the sun [cycle] begins, solar qi is abundant above while yin qi is not yet self-manifesting; thus there is waist and loin pain. If the disease is vacuity and affects the foot, it is because solar qi has dissolved earth qi as it emerges in the first month, and vacuity diseases are more common in winter. Therefore, vacuity results in a limping gait. The case of strong upward-pulling and bending of the back is due to an excess of solar qi contending [with yin qi] above, resulting in strong upward-pulling and bending of the back. Tinnitus, meaning that solar qi is abundant in all things and leaping, results in tinnitus. The case of wild and raving restlessness is due to solar qi being fully present above while yin qi ascends from below; with emptiness below and fullness above, this results in wild and raving restlessness. Floating leading to deafness is all due to qi. Entering the center leads to stiffness, which occurs when solar qi has reached its peak and then declines, resulting in stiffness. Internal theft results in collapse, leading to stiffness; this is a deficiency of the kidney [organ]. Inadequate nourishment from the kidneys results in collapse, manifesting as stiffness and numbness; this is due to deficient yin.

    2. 少陽所謂心脇痛者，言少陽盛也，盛者心之所表也。九月陽氣盡而陰氣盛，故心脇痛也，所謂不可反側者，陰氣藏物也，物藏則不動，故不可反側也。所謂甚則躍者，九月萬物盡衰，草木畢落而墮，則氣去陽而之陰，氣盛而陽之下長，故謂躍。 Of those who are said to have heart and flank pain due to Shao Yang, this refers to the excess of Shao Yang. The excess is what the heart expresses. In September, yang qi is exhausted and yin qi is abundant, so there is heart and flank pain, which means that one cannot turn over. Yin qi governs storage, and when it stores something, it does not move, thus one cannot turn over. When it reaches the extreme and leaps, in September all things have waned and grass and trees have fallen, so the yang has dissipated and the yin has accumulated, causing it to grow below the yang, hence it is said to leap

    3. 陽明所謂洒洒振寒者，陽明者，午也，五月盛陽之陰也，陽盛而陰氣加之，故洒洒振寒也。所謂脛腫而股不收者，是五月盛陽之陰也，陽者衰於五月，而一陰氣上，與陽始爭，故脛腫而股不收也。所謂上喘而為水者，陰氣下而復上，上則邪客於藏府間，故為水也。所謂胸痛少氣者，水氣在藏府也，水者，陰氣也，陰氣在中，故胸痛少氣也。所謂甚則厥惡人與火，聞木音則惕然而驚者，陽氣與陰氣相薄，水火相惡，故惕然而驚也。所謂欲獨閉戶牖而處者，陰陽相薄也，陽盡而陰盛，故欲獨閉戶牖而居。所謂病至則欲乘高而歌，棄衣而走者，陰陽復爭，而外并於陽，故使之棄衣而走也。所謂客孫脈則頭痛鼻鼽腹腫者，陽明并於上，上者，則其孫絡太陰也，故頭痛鼻鼽腹腫也。 On the Yangming theory of chilling and warmth: Yangming refers to the refreshing and warming energy. Yangming is the energy in the middle hour (noon) of the fifth solar term, which is when the yang energy is at its peak. When the yang energy is strong and the yin energy arrives, it causes a refreshing and cooling sensation.

    On the condition of knee swelling but thighs not receding: This is also related to the yin energy in the fifth solar term when the yang energy starts to decline. As the yang energy wanes in the fifth solar term, the yin energy surges upwards and competes with the yang energy, causing knee swelling and thighs that do not recede.

    On the condition of shortness of breath and feeling of suffocation: The yin energy has descended but then rises again, causing disruption in the lower abdomen where vital organs are located, leading to shortness of breath and a feeling of suffocation.

    On the condition of chest pain and weakness: The yin energy is present in the lower abdomen, where vital organs are located, and hence the chest pain and weakness.

    On the phenomenon of being shocked and frightened by sudden loud noises: The yang and yin energies are out of balance, and water and fire are at odds with each other, causing one to be startled and frightened by sudden loud noises.

    On the condition of wanting to be alone and closing oneself off: The yin and yang energies are out of balance, and when the yang energy is depleted and the yin energy is in excess, it leads to a desire for solitude and closure.

    On the condition of wanting to sing and climb high during illness: The yin and yang energies are competing, with the external force leaning towards the yang energy, leading to the desire to sing and climb high during illness.

    On the phenomenon of Toung-Chi (client's grandson) pulse and headache, nasal congestion, and abdominal distension: The Yangming energy is present in the upper body, where the grandson pulse is located. The presence of the grandson pulse indicates an imbalance of yin and yang energies, leading to headache, nasal congestion, and abdominal distension.

    1. 太陰所謂病脹者，太陰子也，十一月萬物氣皆藏於中，故曰病脹；所謂上走心為噫者，陰盛而上走於陽明，陽明絡屬心，故曰上走心為噫也；所謂食則嘔者，物盛滿而上溢，故嘔也；所謂得後與氣則快然如衰者，十二月陰氣下衰，而陽氣且出，故曰得後與氣則快然如衰也。 Regarding the becoming full illness of the Yin: This refers to the Yin itself, as in the eleventh month, the Qi of all things is stored within it. Hence it is called becoming full. As for the so-called ascending heart complaint, when the Yin is abundant and rises upward towards Yang Ming, Yang Ming belongs to the heart, hence it is called ascending heart complaint

    2. 少陰所謂腰痛者，少陰者，腎也，十月萬物陽氣皆傷，故腰痛也；所謂嘔欬上氣喘者，陰氣在下，陽氣在上，諸陽氣浮，無所依從，故嘔欬上氣喘也；所謂色色不能久立，久坐起則目䀮䀮無所見者，萬物陰陽不定未有主也。秋氣始至，微霜始下，而方殺萬物，陰陽內奪故目䀮䀮無所見也；所謂少氣善怒者，陽氣不治，陽氣不治，則陽氣不得出，肝氣當治而未得，故善怒，善怒者，名曰煎厥；所謂恐如人將捕之者，秋氣萬物未有畢去，陰氣少，陽氣入，陰陽相薄，故恐也；所謂惡聞食臭者，胃無氣，故惡聞食臭也；所謂面黑如地色者，秋氣內奪，故變於色也；所謂欬則有血者，陽脈傷也，陽氣未盛於上而脈滿，滿則欬，故血見於鼻也。 Regarding those with a deficiency of the Kidney Yang and experiencing lower back pain, the Kidneys are the root of this. In the tenth lunar month, all creatures' Yang Qi is injured, hence the lower back pain. As for those who experience nausea and shortness of breath upon rising, the Yin Qi is below and the Yang Qi is above, with the Yang Qi floating without anything to support it, resulting in nausea and shortness of breath. Regarding those who cannot maintain their posture due to a deficiency of Yin and Yang, no one has ever governed the myriad creatures' Yin and Yang. When autumn energy arrives and the first frost falls, it kills all creatures, with the Yin and Yang of all creatures not yet stabilized and without a governor, causing them to lose their posture and not be able to see anything upon standing up. Regarding those who become easily angered due to a deficiency of Qi, the Yang Qi is disordered; since the Yang Qi is not discharged, the Liver Qi cannot regulate, leading to easy anger, which is known as Shan Jue. Regarding those who are afraid like someone about to be captured, the autumn energy has not yet completed its disappearance, with Yin deficient and Yang rising; Yin and Yang are insufficiently balanced, causing fear. Regarding those who dislike hearing sound and smell odor, the Stomach lacks Qi, so they dislike hearing sounds and smelling odors. Regarding those whose faces become black like the color of soil, the autumn energy has invaded internally, causing a change in their appearance. Regarding those who cough with blood when they exhale, it is damage to the Yang Meridian, with the Yang Qi not yet flourishing above and the pulse full; when full, there is coughing, and so blood appears at the nostrils.

    3. 厥陰所謂㿉疝婦人少腹腫者，厥陰者，辰也，三月陽中之陰邪在中，故曰㿉疝少腹腫也。所謂腰脊痛不可以俛仰者，三月一振榮華萬物，一俛而不仰也。所謂㿉癃疝膚脹者，曰陰亦盛而脈脹不通，故曰㿉癃疝也。所謂甚則嗌乾熱中者，陰陽相薄而熱，故嗌乾也。 Regarding the so-called pubic swelling of women with less abdomen, it is the Yin (kidneys) that is involved, and the Three Yin Evil in the middle of the third lunar month. Therefore, it is called pubic swelling and slight abdominal distension. As for the so-called lower back pain that cannot be cured by bending forward, the third lunar month is a time of one shaking honor and glory for all things, bending forward but not bending backward. Regarding the so-called pubic rash, it means that the Yin (kidneys) is also abundant but the pulse is not well circulated, hence it is called pubic rash. As for the case of extreme heat and dryness in the urethra, the Yin and Yang are weak and there is heat, which causes the urethra to dry up.
