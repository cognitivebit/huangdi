    逆調論

    1: 黃帝問曰：人身非常溫也，非常熱也，為之熱而煩滿者何也。歧伯對曰：陰氣少而陽氣勝，故熱而煩滿也。 Emperor Yellow asked, When a person is not very hot or very warm, and yet is troubled by the heat, what is the reason for this? The Grand Assistant replied, It is because there is a deficiency of Yin energy and an excess of Yang energy, which causes the heat and trouble.

    2: 帝曰：人身非衣寒也，中非有寒氣也，寒從中生者何。歧伯曰：是人多痺氣也，陽氣少，陰氣多，故身寒如從水中出。 The emperor said: A person's body is not cold because of clothing, nor is it cold because of an internal cold energy. Where does the cold come from? Hui-Bo replied: This is due to a person having too much Qi and insufficient Yang Qi, with excessive Yin Qi. Therefore, the body feels cold as if emerging from water.

    3: 帝曰：人有四支熱，逢逄風寒，如灸如火者，何也。歧伯曰：是人者，陰氣虛，陽氣盛，四支者陽也，兩陽相得而陰氣虛少，少水不能滅盛火，而陽獨治，獨治者不能生長也，獨勝而止耳，逢風而如灸如火者，是人當肉爍也。 The Emperor asked: People have four phases of heat. When encountering wind and cold, like needling and fire, what is this? Hui Bao replied: This person has deficient yin energy and excessive yang energy. The four phases refer to yang. When the two yangs come together, the yin energy is less abundant, and there is not enough water to extinguish the abundant fire. Yang acts alone, but alone it cannot grow or thrive. It stops when victorious, and when encountering wind, it feels like needling and fire. This person must be on the verge of consumption.

    4: 帝曰：人有身寒，湯火不能熱，厚衣不能溫，然不凍慄，是為何病。歧伯曰：是人者，素腎氣勝，以水為事，太陽氣衰，腎脂枯不長，一水不能勝兩火，腎者水也，而生於骨，腎不生，則髓不能滿，故寒甚至骨也。所以不能凍慄者，肝一陽也，心二陽也，腎孤藏也，一水不能勝二火，故不能凍慄，病名曰骨痺，是人當攣節也。 The Emperor asked: People have a cold body, the fire cannot be warm, and thick clothing cannot be warm. Yet they do not freeze with shivering; what disease is this? Huang Di answered: In humans, the kidney essence is strong, dealing with water affairs, the Yang essence declines, the kidney marrow is dry and does not grow, one water cannot win over two fires. The kidney is water, and it is born in the bone; if the kidney does not exist, then the marrow cannot be full, causing great cold to the bone. Therefore, because they do not freeze with shivering, it is due to the one Yang of the liver and the two Yangs of the heart, and the lonely storage of the kidney. One water cannot win over two fires, so they do not freeze with shivering. This disease is called bone rigidity; it is a condition for humans.

    5: 帝曰：人之肉苛者，雖近衣絮，猶尚苛也，是謂何疾。歧伯曰：榮氣虛，衛氣實也，榮氣虛則不仁，衛氣虛則不用，榮衛俱虛，則不仁且不用，肉如故也，人身與志不相有，曰死。 The emperor said: People's flesh is weak, even if they are close to clothing, it is still weak. What is the hurry about? Duke Huan said: Honor and shame are empty, vitality is real; when honor and shame are empty, there is no benevolence; when vitality is empty, there is no use; when both honor and vitality are empty, there is no benevolence and no use. The flesh is like before, the person's body and their will do not correspond, and they say it is death

    6: 帝曰：人有逆氣不得臥而息有音者，有不得臥而息無音者，有起居如故而息有音者，有得臥行而喘者，有不得臥不能行而喘者，有不得臥臥而喘者，皆何藏使然，願聞其故。歧伯曰：不得臥而息有音者，是陽明之逆也，足三陽者下行，今逆而上行，故息有音也。陽明者，胃脈也，胃者六府之海，其氣亦下行，陽明逆不得從其道，故不得臥也。下經曰：胃不和則臥不安，此之謂也。夫起居如故而息有音者，此肺之絡脈逆也，絡脈不得隨經上下，故留經而不行，絡脈之病人也微，故起居如故而息有音也。夫不得臥臥則喘者，是水氣之客也，夫水者循津液而流也，腎者水藏，主津液，主臥與喘也。帝曰：善。 The emperor said: People have reverse qi that cannot lie down and breathe with sound, have those who cannot lie down and breathe without sound, have those who rise and walk as usual but still breathe with sound, have those who can sleep and move but are panting, have those who cannot lie down and cannot move but are panting, and those who cannot lie down and pant even when lying down. What are the reasons for all of these, and I wish to hear their explanations. The Duke of Shou said: Those who cannot lie down and breathe with sound are due to the reverse of yangming, which controls the lower three yang meridians that are now flowing in the opposite direction, thus causing the breathing sounds. Yangming refers to the stomach channel, the stomach is the sea of the six ministers, and its qi also flows downward. When yangming reverses and cannot follow its path, it cannot lie down. The Classic of Internal Medicine says: If the stomach is not harmonious, one will not be able to rest comfortably, referring to this situation. Those who rise and walk as usual but breathe with sound are due to the reverse of lung meridian channels. Lung channel Qi cannot follow the path of the meridians, causing qi to accumulate but not move, making those who have a slight disturbance in their lung channel breathe with sound while rising and walking as usual. Those who pant even when lying down are due to an intruding water qi. Water follows the channels of rivers and fluids, and the kidney stores water, controls fluids, and is responsible for sleeping and panting. Emperor said: Good
