    海論

    1. 黃帝問于歧伯曰：余聞刺法於夫子，夫子之所言，不離於營衛血氣。夫十二經脈者，內屬於府藏，外絡於肢節，夫子乃合之於四海乎。歧伯答曰：人亦有四海，十二經水。經水者，皆注于海，海有東西南北，命曰四海。黃帝曰：以人應之奈何？歧伯曰：人有髓海，有血海，有氣海，有水谷之海，凡此四者，以應四海也。 Emperor Yellow asked Master Hei Bo: I have heard that the needling method is used by Confucius, and everything he says does not depart from military and defensive vitality. The twelve channels and networks are internally related to the ministries and externally connected to the limbs and joints; Confucius unites them at the four corners. Master Hei Bo answered: Human beings also have four corners and twelve waters (channels). The water channels all flow into the sea, with east, west, south, and north as the four corners. Emperor Yellow said: How should people respond to this? Master Hei Bo replied: People have a marrow sea, a blood sea, a qi sea, and a food grain sea; these four respond to the four corners.

    2. 黃帝曰：遠乎哉，夫子之合人天地四海也，願聞應之奈何？歧伯答曰：必先明知陰陽表裏滎輸所在，四海定矣。 Yellow Emperor said: It is far away, how can the master's merging of people, heaven and earth, and four seas be heard? Hui Bao answered: One must first clearly understand the location of yin and yang, front and back, surplus and deficiency, and then it will be defined.

    3. 黃帝曰：定之奈何？歧伯曰：胃者水穀之海，其輸上在氣街，下至三里；衝脈者，為十二經之海，其輸上在於大杼，下出於巨虛之上下廉；膻中者，為氣之海，其輸上在於柱骨之上下，前在於人迎，腦為髓之海，其輸上在於其蓋，下在風府。 Yellow Emperor asked: How about it? Kuang Bo replied: The stomach is the sea of water grains. Its connection is at Qi Street, going down to three li; the Conception Vessel is the sea of the twelve meridians. Its connection is at the Dan Tian, and comes out from Juyu's upper and lower Tan; the Bamboo Grove is the sea of Qi. Its connection is at the top and bottom of Zhong Bai, in front at the Ren Yan; the brain is the sea of marrow. Its connection is at its lid, and comes out at Feng Fu.

    4. 黃帝曰：凡此四海者，何利何害？何生何敗？歧伯曰：得順者生，得逆者敗；知調者利，不知調者害。 Yellow Emperor said: What are the benefits and harm of these four seas? What is the life and death of them? The Duke of Zhou replied: Those who follow the proper order will live, those who go against it will perish; knowing the right time brings profit, not knowing the right time brings loss.

    5. 黃帝曰：四海之逆順奈何？歧伯曰：氣海有餘者，氣滿胸中，悗息面赤；氣海不足，則氣少不足以言。血海有餘，則常想其身大，怫然不知其所病；血海不足，亦常想其身小，狹然不知其所病。水谷之海有餘，則腹滿；水谷之海不足，則飢不受谷食。髓海有餘，則輕勁多力，自過其度；髓海不足，則腦轉耳鳴，脛痠眩冒，目無所見，懈怠安臥。 Yellow Emperor asked: What is the difference between deficiency and excess in the Four Seas (i.e., the four major organs of the body)? Qi Master replied: When there is an abundance of Qi, it fills the chest, and one gasps for breath with a flushed face; when Qi is insufficient, there is not enough to speak. If there is an abundance of blood, one constantly imagines their body as large and clumsy, without knowing what is wrong; if there is not enough blood, one constantly imagines their body as small and narrow, also unaware of what is wrong. With regard to the sea of grain and water, when there is an abundance, one's abdomen is full; when there is a deficiency, there is hunger that cannot be satisfied by food. If there is an abundance of essence, there are many vigorous energies and abundant strength, exceeding their limits; when there is a deficiency, the mind spins, the ears ring, the limbs tremble and dizziness comes over one's vision, lying languidly without knowing what to do.

    6. 黃帝曰：余已聞逆順，調之奈何？歧伯曰：審守其俞，而調其虛實，無犯其害，順者得復，逆者必敗。黃帝曰：善。 Yellow Emperor said: I have heard about Yin and Yang, how can they be regulated? Duke of Zhou said: Examine the function of Yin and Yang, and regulate their substance without violating their nature. Those who follow will prosper, and those who oppose will perish. Yellow Emperor said: Good.
