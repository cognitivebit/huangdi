    順氣一日分為四時

    1. 黃帝曰：夫百病之所始生者，必起於燥溫寒暑風雨陰陽喜怒飲食居處，氣合而有形，得藏而有名，余知其然也。夫百病者，多以旦慧晝安，夕加夜甚，何也？歧伯曰：四時之氣使然。 Yellow Emperor said: The source of the hundred diseases is definitely due to dryness, heat, cold, hot and cold winds, rain, yin and yang, joy and anger, drinking, eating, and dwelling. The qi gathers and takes shape, and hides and has a name, I know it is true. Yellow Emperor asked: The hundred diseases are often more intelligent in the morning and peaceful at night, and increase in the evening and become worse at night, what is the reason for this? Qi Bo said: This is due to the energy of the four seasons.

    2. 黃帝曰：願聞四時之氣。歧伯曰：春生，夏長，秋收，冬藏，是氣之常也，人亦應之，以一日分為四時，朝則為春，日中為夏，日入為秋，夜半為冬。朝則人氣始生，病氣衰，故旦慧；日中人氣長，長則勝邪，故安；夕則人氣始衰，邪氣始生，故加；夜半人氣入藏，邪氣獨居於身，故甚也。 Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear about the changes of the four seasons. Kuang-pen replied: Spring brings forth, summer prolongs, autumn harvests, winter stores – this is the natural cycle of energy. People should follow these cycles. One day is divided into four times: morning is spring, noon is summer, afternoon is autumn, and midnight is winter. In the morning, people's vitality begins to emerge, and pathogenic factors wane, thus making it an auspicious time. At noon, people's vitality grows stronger, which helps overcome evil influences. In the evening, people's vitality starts to decline, and pathogenic factors arise, so it is a dangerous period. At midnight, people's vitality is stored and pathogenic factors dominate the body, making it a severe time.

    3. 黃帝曰：有時有反者何也？歧伯曰：是不應四時之氣，藏獨主其病者，是必以藏氣之所不勝時者甚，以其所勝時者起也。黃帝曰：治之奈何？歧伯曰：順天之時，而病可與期。順者為工，逆者為麤。 Yellow Emperor said: What causes someone to have a counter-seasonal disease? Guanpu said: It is due to not responding to the qi of the four seasons, and using self-isolation to treat their illness, so one must use the time when their qi cannot prevail to suppress it, and use the time when it can prevail to rise. Yellow Emperor said: How should it be treated? Guanpu said: Follow the rhythm of heaven and earth, and the disease can be predicted. Following is a skill, whereas opposing is a loss.

    4. 黃帝曰：善，余聞刺有五變，以主五輸。願聞其數。歧伯曰：人有五藏，五藏有五變。五變有五輸，故五五二十五輸，以應五時。 The Yellow Emperor said, Good, I have heard that there are five changes of the needle and it governs five losses. I wish to hear about their number. Qi Bo replied, Human beings have five vital organs, and the five vital organs have five changes. The five changes have five losses, so there are twenty-five losses in total, which correspond to the five times of the day.

    5. 黃帝曰：願聞五變。歧伯曰：肝為牡藏，其色青，其時春，其音角，其味酸，其日甲乙；心為牡藏，其色赤，其時夏，其日丙丁，其音徵，其味苦；脾為牝藏，其色黃，其時長夏，其日戊己，其音宮，其味甘；肺為牝藏，其色白，其音商，其時徵，其日庚辛，其味辛；腎為牝藏，其色黑，其時冬，其日壬癸，其音羽，其味鹹。是為五變。 Yellow Emperor said, I wish to hear about the Five Phases. Duke of Shou replied, The Liver is the Yin source. It has a green color, its season is spring, its sound is Xiang, its taste is sour, its days are Jia and Yi. The Heart is the Yin source. It has a red color, its season is summer, its days are Bing and Ding, its sound is Zhī, its taste is bitter. The Spleen is the Yang source. Its season is late summer, its days are Wu and Ji, its sound is Gong, its taste is sweet. The Lungs are the Yang source. It has a white color, its season is autumn, its days are Geng and Xin, its sound is Shan, its taste is pungent. The Kidneys are the Yin source. Its season is winter, its days are Ren and Gui, its sound is Yǔ, its taste is salty. These are the Five Phases

    6. 黃帝曰：以主五輸奈何？藏主冬，冬刺井；色主春，春刺滎；時主夏，夏刺輸；音主長夏，長夏刺經；味主秋，秋刺合。是謂五變，以主五輸。 Yellow Emperor said, Why have the five seasons not submitted? In the midst of winter, one stabs the well; in the midst of spring, one stabs the Bamboo Grove; in the midst of summer, one stabs the floods; during the season of Long Summer, one stabs the Meridians; and in the midst of autumn, one stabs the Gathering. Such are the Five Cycles, through which the five seasons submit.

    7. 黃帝曰：諸原安和，以致五輸。歧伯曰：原獨不應五時，以經合之，以應其數，故六六三十六輸。 Yellow Emperor said: All under Heaven are in peace and harmony, which resulted in five losses. Duke of Zhou said: If there were no original chaos, but the world was regulated according to the Book of Changes, then it would correspond with its numbers, resulting in six six thirty-six losses.

    8. 黃帝曰：何謂藏主冬，時主夏，音主長夏，味主秋，色主春。願聞其故。歧伯曰：病在藏者，取之井；病變於色者，取之滎；病時間時甚者，取之輸；病變於音者，取之經；經滿而血者，病在胃；及以飲食不節得病者，取之於合，故命曰味主合。是謂五變也。 Yellow Emperor said: What do you mean by the Master of Storage in winter, the Master of Time in summer, the Master of Long Summer, the Master of Taste in autumn, and the Master of Color in spring? I wish to hear about their origins. Duke of Shou replied: Disease resides in the storage, draw from the well; disease changes with color, draw from the flood; disease of timing is quite critical, draw from the channel; when the channel is full and there is blood, the disease is in the stomach; when diseases are caused by improper diet, draw from the Union, so it is called the Master of Taste of the Union. These are the Five Changes.
