    陰陽繫日月

    1. 黃帝曰：余聞天為陽，地為陰，日為陽，月為陰，其合之於人，奈何？歧伯曰：腰以上為天，腰以下為地，故天為陽，地為陰，故足之十二經脈，以應十二月，月生於水，故在下者為陰；手之十指，以應十日，日主火，故在上者為陽。 The Yellow Emperor said: I have heard that heaven is yang and earth is yin, the sun is yang and the moon is yin. What do you say about their harmonization in humans? Huang Di replied: The upper part of the body is heaven, and the lower part is earth. Therefore, heaven is yang and earth is yin. Thus, the twelve channels and collaterals of the foot correspond to the twelve months. The moon is generated from water, so what is below is considered yin; the ten fingers of the hands correspond to the ten days, the sun governs fire, so what is above is considered yang.

    2. 黃帝曰：合之於脈，奈何？歧伯曰：寅者，正月之生陽也，主左足之少陽；未者，六月，主右足之少陽。卯者，二月，主左足之太陽；午者，五月，主右足之太陽。辰者，三月，主左足之陽明；巳者，四月，主右足之陽明。此兩陽合於前，故曰陽明。申者，七月之生陰也，主右足之少陰；丑者，十二月，主左足之少陰；酉者，八月，主右足之太陰；子者，十一月，主左足之太陰；戌者，九月，主右足之厥陰；亥者，十月，主左足之厥陰；此兩陰交盡，故曰厥陽。 Yellow Emperor said: How about combining it with the pulse? Quick response: In the Yang Ming cycle, the first month is the generation of Yang, ruling the left foot's Shao Yang; the sixth month is ruled by the right foot's Shao Yang. The second month governs the left foot's Tai Yang; the fifth month is governed by the right foot's Tai Yang. The third month governs the left foot's Yang Ming, and the fourth month governs the right foot's Yang Ming. Combining the two Yin in front leads to Yang Ming. In the Xu cycle, the seventh month is the generation of Yin, ruling the right foot's Shao Yin; the twelfth month rules the left foot's Shao Yin. The eighth month governs the right foot's Tai Yin, and the eleventh month governs the left foot's Tai Yin. The ninth month governs the right foot's Jue Yin, and the tenth month governs the left foot's Jue Yin. These two Yin fully intertwine, which is why it is called Jue Yang.

    3. 甲主左手之少陽；己主右手之少陽；乙主左手之太陽，戊主右手之太陽；丙主左手之陽明，丁主右手之陽明，此兩火并合，故為陽明。庚主右手之少陰，癸主左手之少陰，辛主右手之太陰，壬主左手之太陰。 The Jupiter-associated left hand has Lesser Yang; the Saturn-associated right hand has Lesser Yang; the Venus-associated left hand has Greater Yang, and the Earth-associated right hand has Greater Yang. The Wood and Metal elements are combined in this instance, hence it is called Greater Yang. The Mercury-associated right hand has Lesser Yin, the Water-associated left hand has Lesser Yin, and the Fire-associated right hand has Greater Yin, and the Earth-associated left hand has Greater Yin.

    (Translator's note: This passage is describing the elements in traditional Chinese astrology and their corresponding positions on the body.)

    1. 故足之陽者，陰中之少陽也；足之陰者，陰中之太陰也。手之陽者，陽中之太陽也；手之陰者，陽中之少陰也。腰以上者為陽，腰以下者為陰。 In the case where the foot's yang is present, it is a deficiency of yang within the interior. The foot's yin is a deficiency of yin within the interior. The hand's yang is an excess of yang within the interior; the hand's yin is a deficiency of yin within the interior. What is above the waist is considered yang, and what is below the waist is considered yin.

    2. 其於五藏也，心為陽中之太陽，肺為陰中之少陰，肝為陰中之少陽，脾為陰中之至陰，腎為陰中之太陰。 It is in the Five Classics, the heart is like the sun within the Yang, the lungs are like the small Yin within the Yin, the liver is like the small Yang within the Yin, the spleen is like the ultimate Yin within the Yin, and the kidneys are like the great Yin within the Yin.

    3. 黃帝曰：以治之奈何？歧伯曰：正月二月三月，人氣在左，無刺左足之陽；四月五月六月，人氣在右，無刺右足之陽，七月八月九月，人氣在右，無刺右足之陰，十月十一月十二月，人氣在左，無刺左足之陰。 Yellow Emperor asked, What about treating it? Hua Tuo replied, In the months of the first, second, and third lunar month when the human energy is on the left side, do not prick the yang of the left foot. In the fourth, fifth, and sixth lunar month when the human energy is on the right side, do not prick the yang of the right foot. In the seventh, eighth, and ninth lunar month when the human energy is on the right side, do not prick the yin of the right foot. In the tenth, eleventh, and twelfth lunar month when the human energy is on the left side, do not prick the yin of the left foot.

    4. 黃帝曰：五行以東方為甲乙木主春。春者，蒼色，主肝，肝者，足厥陰也。今乃以甲為左手之少陽，不合於數，何也？歧伯曰：此天地之陰陽也，非四時五行之以次行也。且夫陰陽者，有名而無形，故數之可十，離之可百，散之可千，推之可萬，此之謂也。 The Yellow Emperor said: The five phases assign the east to metal, which governs spring. Spring is associated with the color blue and governs the liver, which corresponds to the foot's yin channel. Now, you use the letter A as the left hand's shallow Yang, which does not fit with numerology; why is that? The Duke of Zhou said: This is about the yin and yang of heaven and earth, not the cycles of the four seasons and the five phases. Furthermore, yin and yang have names but no shape, so they can be counted up to ten, separated into a hundred, scattered into a thousand, and expanded into ten thousand; this is what it means
