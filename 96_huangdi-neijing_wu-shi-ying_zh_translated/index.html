    五十營

    1: 黃帝曰：余願聞五十營奈何？歧伯答曰：天周二十八宿，宿三十六分；人氣行一周，千八分，日行二十八宿。人經脈上下左右前後二十八脈，周身十六丈二尺，以應二十八宿。 Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear about the five zang organs. Duke of Shao replied: There are 28 mansions in the heavenly cycle, and each mansion consists of 36 degrees. When human qi circulates one cycle, it covers a distance of 108 degrees (1,000 miles) per day, traversing 28 mansions. There are 28 channels/vessels on the body, above and below, left and right, front and back. The human body's meridians encircle it with a length of 16 feet and 2 inches to correspond with the 28 mansions.

    2: 漏水下百刻，以分晝夜。故人一呼脈再動，氣行三寸，一吸，脈亦再動，氣行三寸，呼吸定息，氣行六寸；十息，氣行六尺，日行二分。二百七十息，氣行十六丈二尺，氣行交通於中，一周於身，下水二刻，日行二十五分。五百四十息，氣行再周於身，下水四刻，日行四十分。二千七百息，氣行十周於身，下水二十刻，日行五宿二十分。一萬三千五百息，氣行五十營於身，水下百刻，日行二十八宿，漏水皆盡脈終矣。 A drop of water misses each hour, separating day and night. A person is called when the pulse moves again; the breath flows three inches, a breath moves the pulse again, the breath flows three inches. The inhalation and exhalation are measured, the breath flows six inches per cycle. Ten cycles, the breath flows six feet, one walks two steps. Two hundred and seventy cycles, the breath flows sixteen yards and six inches, breathing is evenly distributed throughout the body, sinking water twice an hour, walking twenty-five minutes per day. Five hundred and forty cycles, breathing is evenly distributed throughout the body, sinking water four times an hour, walking forty minutes per day. Two thousand seven hundred cycles, breathing is evenly distributed throughout the body, sinking water twenty times an hour, walking fifty stays per day. One hundred and thirty-five hundred cycles, breathing is evenly distributed throughout the body, sinking water a hundred times an hour, walking twenty-eight stays per day, every drop of water lost is measured by the pulse's end.

    3: 所謂交通者，並行一數也。故五十營備，得盡天地之壽矣，凡行八百一十丈也。 As for those who travel, there are few who can match their speed. Therefore, with fifty tents prepared, they can complete the journey of eight hundred and ten paces in heaven and earth.
