    外揣

    1: 黃帝曰：余聞九鍼九篇，余親受其調，頗得其意。夫九鍼者，始於一而終於九，然未得其要道也。夫九鍼者，小之則無內，大之則無外，深不可為下，高不可為蓋，恍惚無竅，流溢無極，佘知其合於天道人事四時之變也，然余願雜之毫毛，渾束為一，可乎？歧伯曰：明乎哉問也，非獨鍼道焉，夫治國亦然。 Yellow Emperor said: I have heard of the Nine Needles and Nine Sheets, and I have received its essence. As for the Nine Needles, it starts from one and ends at nine, but I have not yet attained its essential way. The Nine Needles is something that is neither small nor large, has no inside or outside, cannot be described in terms of depth or height, and is like a blurry vision with no holes and an overflowing torrent without limits. Recognizing that it is applicable to the transformations of heavenly principles, human events, and the four seasons, I wish for my understanding to be as fine as a hair's breadth and bundled into one, can this be done? Hui Shi said: This question is clear indeed, and it is not only about the Nine Needles' way. As for governing a country, it is also the same.

    2: 黃帝曰：余願聞鍼道，非國事也。歧伯曰：夫治國者，夫惟道焉，非道，何可小大深淺，雜合而為一乎。 Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear about the method of acupuncture, which is not a matter of state affairs. Duke of Shou said: Governing a country is nothing but the Way, without the Way, what can be small or large, deep or shallow, and mixed together as one?

    3: 黃帝曰：願卒聞之。歧伯曰：日與月焉，水與鏡焉，鼓與響焉。夫日月之明，不失其影，水鏡之察，不失其形，鼓響之應，不後其聲，動搖則應和，盡得其情。 Yellow Emperor said: I wish to hear about it. Huzi replied: Like the sun and moon, water and a mirror, and drums and their echoes. The brightness of the sun and moon does not lose its shadow, the attentiveness of water and a mirror does not lose its shape, and the response of drums and their echoes do not lag behind their sound. When moving or shaking, it responds in harmony and accurately captures the mood.

    4: 黃帝曰：窘乎哉！昭昭之明不可蔽，其不可蔽，不失陰陽也。合而察之，切而驗之，見而得之，若清水明鏡之不失其形也。五音不彰，五色不明，五藏波蕩，若是則內外相襲，若鼓之應桴，響之應聲，影之似形。故遠者，司外揣內，近者，司內揣外，是謂陰陽之極，天地之蓋，請藏之靈蘭之室，弗敢使泄也。 Yellow Emperor said: Ah, how difficult! The clarity of the Shao-yan is impossible to conceal; its inability to be concealed does not mean it fails to distinguish yin and yang. When combined, examined, scrutinized, and seen, it is like the unfailing shape of a clear water mirror. Five sounds are not prominent, five colors are not clear, five viscera are turbid, if so, then inner and outer will harm each other, just as drum responds to gong, resonates with sound, shadow resembles form. Therefore, those far away should investigate inside while watching outside, and those nearby should investigate outside while watching inside, this is what we call the extreme of yin and yang, the seal of heaven and earth, and the dwelling place of the secret treasure, which I dare not reveal.
