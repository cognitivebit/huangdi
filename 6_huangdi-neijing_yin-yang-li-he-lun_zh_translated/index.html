    陰陽離合論

    1. 黃帝問曰：余聞天為陽，地為陰，日為陽，月為陰，大小月三百六十日成一歲，人亦應之。今三陰三陽，不應陰陽，其故何也。歧伯對曰：陰陽者，數之可十，推之可百，數之可千，推之可萬，萬之大不可勝數，然其要一也。天覆地載，萬物方生，未出地者，命曰陰處，名曰陰中之陰；則出地者，命曰陰中之陽。陽予之正，陰為之主。故生因春，長因夏，收因秋，藏因冬，失常則天地四塞。陰陽之變，其在人者，亦數之可數。 Emperor Yellow asked: I have heard that heaven is yang, earth is yin, the sun is yang and the moon is yin. A month has 360 days, completing a year, and humans should also follow this. Now there are three yins and three yangs not following the yin and yang, what is the reason for this? The Grand Officer replied: Yin and Yang can be counted to ten, extended to a hundred, counted to a thousand, and extended to ten thousand, but its root is one. Heaven covers the earth, and myriad things are born; those not yet out of the earth have the command of yin and the name of the hidden yin within the yin; once out of the earth, they have the command of the yin within the yang. Yang gives birth to it, and yin governs it. Therefore, life arises in spring, grows in summer, harvests in autumn, stores in winter, and losing this order leads to chaos in heaven and earth. The changes of yin and yang are also something that can be counted in humans.

    2. 帝曰：願聞三陰三陽之離合也。歧伯曰：聖人南面而立，前曰廣明，後曰太衝，太衝之地，名曰少陰，少陰之上，名曰太陽，太陽根起於至陰，結於命門，名曰陰中之陽。中身而上，名曰廣明，廣明之下，名曰太陰，太陰之前，名曰陽明，陽明根起於厲兌，名曰陰中之陽。厥陰之表，名曰少陽，少陽根起於竅陰，名曰陰中之少陽。是故三陽之離合也，太陽為開，陽明為闔，少陽為樞。三經者，不得相失也，搏而勿浮，命曰一陽。 The Emperor said: I wish to hear about the separation and combination of the three Yin and three Yang. Kuai Bo said: A sage stands facing south; in front it is Broad Brightness, behind it is Great Excess, the land of Great Excess is called Lesser Yin, the area above Lesser Yin is called Great Yang, the root of Great Yang arises from the Extreme Yin, binds at the Command Gate, and is called the Yang within Yin. Rising inwardly and above it is called Broad Brightness; below Broad Brightness it is called Greater Yin, the front of Greater Yin is called Yang Bright, the root of Yang Bright arises from severe cleaving and is called the Yang within Yin. The surface of Excess Yin is called Lesser Yang, the root of Lesser Yang arises from the Orifice Yin and is called the Yang within Lesser Yang. Therefore, the separation and combination of the three Yangs are as follows: Great Yang opens, Yang Bright closes, and Lesser Yang serves as the pivot. The Three Classics must not be lost; struggle without floating, and call it the one Yang.

    3. 帝曰：願聞三陰。歧伯曰：外者為陽，內者為陰，然則中為陰，其衝在下，名曰太陰，太陰根起於隱白，名曰陰中之陰。太陰之後，名曰少陰，少陰根起於涌泉，名曰陰中之少陰。少陰之前，名曰厥陰，厥陰根起於大敦，陰之絕陽，名曰陰之絕陰。是故三陰之離合也，太陰為開，厥陰為闔，少陰為樞。三經者，不得相失也。搏而勿沈，名曰一陰。陰陽𩅞𩅞，積傳為一周，氣裏形表而為相成也。 Emperor said: I wish to hear about the Three Yin. Guo Bo said: The outside is Yang and the inside is Yin, so the center is Yin, its surge is below, named as Taiyin, the root of Taiyin originates from Xuanbi, named as Yinxuantaiyin. The offspring of Taiyin, named as Shaoyin, Shaoyin's root originates from Shuopan, named as Yinxuanshaoyin. Shaoyin's front is named as Jiyin, Jiyin's root originates from Dadun, the termination of Yin and the beginning of Yang, named as Yin's termination of Yin. Therefore, the separation and combination of the Three Yin, Taiyin opens, Jiyin closes, Shaoyin is the pivot. The three classics must not be lost. Struggling yet not sinking, named as one Yin. Yin and Yang are inherited as one cycle, Qi inside and outside forms and becomes complete.
