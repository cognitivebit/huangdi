    湯液醪醴論

    1: 黃帝問曰：為五穀湯液及醪醴，柰何。歧伯對曰：必以稻米，炊之稻薪，稻米者完，稻薪者堅。帝曰：何以然。歧伯曰：此得天地之和，高下之宜，故能至完，伐取得時，故能至堅也。帝曰：上古聖人作湯液醪醴，為而不用，何也。歧伯曰：自古聖人之作湯液醪醴者，以為備耳，夫上古作湯液，故為而弗服也。中古之世，道德稍衰，邪氣時至，服之萬全。帝曰：今之世不必已何也。歧伯曰：當今之世，必齊毒藥攻其中，鑱石鍼艾治其外也。 Emperor asked: Why is the broth of the five grains thick and the wine potent? The Duke replied: It must be made from rice and wheat, cooked with rice straw, and both are complete. Emperor asked: How come they are so complete? The Duke said: This comes from the harmony of Heaven and Earth, and the appropriateness of high and low. Thus, it can achieve completeness, and when the time is ripe, it can achieve strength. Emperor asked: Why did ancient sages make this broth and wine but not use them? The Duke replied: Since ancient times, the sage made this broth and wine as a preventive measure. In the ancient times, however, they did not use it because it was made. In later times when the virtues declined and evil Qi prevailed, using it would save all under heaven. Emperor asked: What about our times now? The Duke replied: Nowadays, we have poisonous drugs that attack within, and jade stones, needles, and moxa treat from outside.

    2: 帝曰：形弊血盡而功不立者何。歧伯曰：神不使也。帝曰：何謂神不使。歧伯曰：鍼石道也。精神不進，志意不治，故病不可愈。今精壞神去，榮衛不可復收。何者，嗜欲無窮，而憂患不止，精氣弛壞，榮泣衛除，故神去之而病不愈也。 The emperor asked: What of one whose form is ruined and blood drained, yet achievements do not stand? Duke Huan replied: It is due to the lack of divine will. The emperor asked: What do you mean by lack of divine will? Duke Huan replied: It is the path of needling stones (referring to acupuncture). When the spirit and mind are not cultivated, intentions are not controlled, resulting in illness that cannot be cured. Now, with depletion and loss of spirit, the honored officials cannot be reinstated. This pertains to insatiable desire without end, accompanied by continuous worries, leading to a gradual decline of vital energy, and causing the loss of honor and defense, resulting in illness that cannot be cured

    3: 帝曰：夫病之始生也，極微極精，必先入結於皮膚。今良工皆稱曰：病成名曰逆，則鍼石不能治，良藥不能及也。今良工皆得其法，守其數，親戚兄弟遠近音聲日聞於耳，五色日見於目，而病不愈者，亦何暇不早乎。歧伯曰：病為本，工為標，標本不得，邪氣不服，此之謂也。 The emperor said: A disease begins when it is extremely subtle and refined, and must first enter and settle on the skin. Nowadays, even good doctors all say that if a disease becomes famous as reversed, acupuncture and moxibustion cannot cure it, and good medicine cannot reach it. Good doctors all know the method, keep track of the number, and relatives and friends, whether far or near, hear about it through word of mouth, and see the five colors with their eyes every day. If the disease does not heal, what time would there be to not act early? Hui Bo said: Disease is the root, and diagnosis is the standard; if the standard cannot be established from the root, then the pathogenic qi will not dissipate, which is what is meant by this.

    4: 帝曰：其有不從毫毛而生，五藏陽以竭也，津液充郭，其魄獨居，孤精於內，氣耗於外，形不可與衣相保，此四極急而動中，是氣拒於內，而形施於外，治之柰何。歧伯曰：平治於權衡，去宛陳莝，微動四極，溫衣，繆刺其處，以復其形。開鬼門，潔淨府，精以時服，五陽已布，踈滌五藏，故精自生，形自盛，骨肉相保，巨氣乃平。帝曰：善。 Emperor said: What does not arise from a single hair follicle can be brought about by the essence of the five organs and the Yang. The fluids fill the cavities, its spirit resides alone, the solitary consciousness dwells within, the vital energy is expended externally, the shape cannot be preserved with clothes, at this point, the four extremes are urgent in motion. This causes the Qi to repel from within, and manifest the form from without, how can it be treated? The Duke of Zhou replied: Regulate the balance between Yin and Yang, eliminate the deviation of Zhou, make subtle movements for the four extremes, warm the clothes, puncture the acupoints, thereby restoring its shape. Open the Ghost Gate, purify the Palace, refine the essence at the appropriate time, when the five Yangs have been established, tread and massage the five organs, thus the spirit will spontaneously emerge, the form will naturally be ample, the flesh and bones will be preserved, and the immense Qi will be pacified. Emperor said: Good.
