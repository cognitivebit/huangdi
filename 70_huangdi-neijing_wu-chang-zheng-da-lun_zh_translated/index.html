    1: 黃帝問曰：太虛寥廓，五運迴薄，衰盛不同，損益相從，願聞平氣何如而名，何如而紀也。歧伯對曰：昭乎哉問也。木曰敷和，火曰升明，土曰備化，金曰審平，水曰靜順。帝曰：其不及，奈何。歧伯曰：木曰委和，火曰伏明，土曰卑監，金曰從革，水曰涸流。帝曰：太過何謂。歧伯曰：木曰發生，火曰赫曦，上曰敦阜，金曰堅成，水曰流衍。帝曰：三氣之紀，願聞其候。歧伯曰：悉乎哉問也。敷和之紀，木德周行，陽舒陰布，五化宣平，其氣端，其性隨，其用曲直，其化生榮，其類草木，其政發散，其候溫和，其令風，其藏肝，肝其畏清，其主目，其穀麻，其果李，其實核，其應春，其蟲毛，其畜犬，其色蒼，其養筋，其病裏急支滿，其味酸，其音角，其物中堅，其數八。升明之紀，正陽而治，德施周普，五化均衡，其氣高，其性速，其用燔灼，其化蕃茂，其類火，其政明曜，其候炎暑，其令熱，其藏心，心其畏寒，其主舌，其穀麥，其果杏，其實絡，其應夏，其蟲羽，其畜馬，其色赤，其養血，其病瞤瘛，其味苦，其音徵，其物脈，其數七。備化之紀，氣協天休，德流四政，五化齊脩，其氣平，其性順，其用高下，其化豐滿，其類土，其政安靜，其候溽蒸，其令濕，其藏脾，脾其畏風，其主口，其穀稷，其果棗，其實肉，其應長夏，其蟲倮，其畜牛，其色黃，其養肉，其病否，其味甘，其音宮，其物膚，其數五。審平之紀，收而不爭，殺而無犯，五化宣明，其氣潔，其性剛，其用散落，其化堅斂，其類金，其政勁肅，其候清切，其令燥，其藏肺，肺其畏熱，其主鼻，其穀稻，其果桃，其實殼，其應秋，其蟲介，其畜難，其色白，其養皮毛，其病欬，其味辛，其音商，其物外堅，其數九。靜順之紀，藏而勿害，治而善下，五化咸整，其氣明，其性下，其用沃衍，其化凝堅，其類水，其政流演，其候凝肅，其令寒，其藏腎，腎其畏濕，其主二陰，其穀豆，其果栗，其實濡，其應冬，其蟲鱗，其畜彘，其色黑，其養骨髓，其病厥，其味鹹，其音羽，其物濡，其數六。故生而勿殺，長而勿罰，化而勿制，收而勿害，藏而勿抑，是謂平氣。 Emporer asked: The Great Emptiness is vast and obscure, the Five Phases return with decrease. Youth and old age are different, profits and losses follow each other. I wish to hear how to maintain harmony and be named, how to record it also.

    Huangdi replied: How excellent your question! Wood says, Peaceful Harmony, Fire says, Rising Brightness, Earth says, Preparation for Change, Metal says, Judicious Balance, Water says, Quiet Obedience.

    Emperor asked: Is there something that is not as good as this?

    Huangdi replied: Wood says, Enriching Harmony, Fire says, Beaming Brightness, above it says, Gentle and Yielding, Metal says, Tenacious Success, Water says, Diverging Flow.

    Emperor asked: What do you mean by excessive?

    Huangdi replied: Wood says, Generated Abundance, Fire says, Vivid Brightness, above it says, Gentle and Yielding, Metal says, Resolute Success, Water says, Fluid Flow.

    Emperor asked: I wish to hear about the records of the Three Phases.

    Huangdi replied: How excellent your question! The record of Peaceful Harmony includes: Wood follows its moral law, yang becomes spacious and yin is distributed, the Five Phases are announced in harmony, its Qi is pure, its nature is yielding, its uses are curved and straight, its transformation produces honor, its types are grasses and trees, its government is dispersive, its season is warm and humid, its decree is wind, it controls the liver, its grain is hemp, its fruit is the pear, its seeds are the core, its season is spring, its insects have fur, its domestic animals are dogs, its color is blue, its nourishes sinews, its diseases are sharp and fullness in the organs, its taste is sour, its sound is sharp, its things are hard, its number is eight.

    The record of Rising Brightness includes: Regulating yang and governing, moral law follows its cycle, the Five Phases are balanced, its Qi is high, its nature is swift, its uses are fiery and incinerating, its transformation produces luxuriant growth, its types are fire, its government is bright, its season is summer heat, its decree is warm, it controls the heart, the heart fears cold, its grain is wheat, its fruit is the apricot, its seeds are the rind, its season is summer, its insects have feathers, its domestic animals are horses, its color is red, its nourishes blood, its diseases are trembling and numbness, its taste is bitter, its sound is indicative, its things are vessels, its number is seven.

    The record of Preparation for Change includes: Qi coexists with Heaven's rest, moral law flows through the four governments, the Five Phases are harmoniously produced, its Qi is balanced, its nature is obedient, its uses are high and low, its transformation produces abundance, its types are earth, its government is peaceful and still, its season is moisture and steam, its decree is dampness, it controls the spleen, the spleen fears wind, its grain is millet, its fruit is the peach, its seeds are flesh, its season is late summer, its insects are flies, its domestic animals are oxen, its color is yellow, its nourishes muscles, its diseases are weakness, its taste is sweet, its sound is palace, its things are skin, its number is five.

    The record of Judicious Balance includes: Receiving and not competing, killing but without transgression, the Five Phases are announced in clarity, its Qi is pure, its nature is rigid, its uses are scattered and falling, its transformation is steadfast support, its types are metal, its government is calm and solemn, its season is clear division, its decree is aridity, it controls the lungs, the lungs fear heat, its grain is rice, its fruit is the peach, its seeds are shells, its season is autumn, its insects have wings, its domestic animals are difficult to handle, its color is white, its nourishes skin and hair, its diseases are weakness, its taste is salty, its sound is commerce, its things are outside hardness, its number is nine.

    The record of Quiet Obedience includes: Hiding but not harming, regulating but beneficial, the Five Phases are recorded in harmony, its Qi is clear, its nature is lower, its uses are nourishing and spreading, its transformation is solidified support, its types are water, its government is flowing performance, its season is freezing and gathering, its decree is coldness, it controls the kidneys, the kidneys fear moisture, its grain is beans, its fruit is the chestnut, its seeds are moist, its season is winter, its insects have scales, its domestic animals are difficult to handle, its color is black, its nourishes bones and marrow, its diseases are weakness, its taste is salty, its sound is feather, its things are wetness, its number is six. Therefore, in being born do not kill, in growing do not punish, in transforming do not control, in receiving do not harm, in hiding do not suppress, this is how to maintain harmony and be named, how to record it also.

    The Record of Origin (发生之纪) describes Jǐ (启), the beginning of heaven and earth, where yin and yang are balanced and the political order is harmonious. There is a peaceful flow of qi energy in all things, and the virtues are manifested. Yang Qi ascends, while yin Qi descends, creating a balance between the two energies that govern the universe. The political order is firm but not rigid, and there is no confusion or disorder. The land flourishes, and all creatures thrive in harmony. The colors of the fruits are bright and vibrant, their flavors pure and sweet, and the seasons are mild and pleasant. The Record of the Classics (赫曦之纪) describes Yángpíng (荣茂), a period when yin energy is concentrated within and Yang Qi shines externally. There is a balance between the two energies, but with a greater emphasis on Yang energy. The political order is strong and just, but not oppressive, and the qi energy of all creatures flows smoothly. The colors of fruits are rich and varied, their flavors spicy and salty, and the seasons are hot and sunny. The Record of Changes (敦阜之纪) describes Hòudìng (厚德), an era characterized by a deep understanding of yin and yang energies. There is a balance between the two energies, but with more emphasis on the virtues and moral values that govern society. The political order is just and fair, and there is no confusion or disorder. All creatures thrive in harmony, and their colors are muted and subdued, with flavors that are mild and bland. The Record of Concealment (流衍之纪) describes Shěn (封藏), an era when yin energy is dominant and the political order is more oppressive and rigid. There is a greater emphasis on controlling people's actions and restricting their freedom. All creatures are subdued, and their colors are dark and somber, with flavors that are bitter or salty. The seasons are cold and harsh, and the qi energy of all creatures is stagnant and unhealthy.

    The above translations are from the I Ching, which is a classic Chinese text that dates back to around 1000 BCE. It is used for divination and guidance in decision-making and life choices. The text describes different periods or eras characterized by different balances of yin and yang energies, with each era having its own unique characteristics in terms of the political order, qi energy flow, virtues, and the colors, flavors, and seasons.

    4: 帝曰：天不足西北，左寒而右涼，地不滿東南，右熱而左溫，其故何也。歧伯曰：陰陽之氣，高下之理，太少之異也。東南方，陽也，陽者其精降於下，故右熱而左溫。西北方，陰也，陰者其精奉於上，故左寒而右涼。是以地有高下，氣有溫涼，高者氣寒，下者氣熱。故適寒涼者脹之，溫熱者瘡，下之則脹已，汗之則瘡已，此湊理開閉之常，太少之異耳。帝曰：其於壽夭何如。歧伯曰：陰精所奉其人壽，陽精所降其人夭。帝曰：善。其病也，治之柰何。歧伯曰：西北之氣散而寒之，東南之氣收而溫之，所謂同病異治也。故曰：氣寒氣涼，治以寒涼，行水漬之。氣溫氣熱，治以溫熱，強其內守。必同其氣，可使平也，假者反之。 The Emperor asked: Why are the heavenly principles of yin and yang not balanced in the west and north, with cold on the left and heat on the right, and on earth, the south and east being hot while the north and west are cold? The Duke of Zhou replied: This is due to the different qualities of the energy of yin and yang. In the south and east, it is yang energy, which causes its essence to descend below, resulting in heat on the right and warmth on the left. In the west and north, it is yin energy, which causes its essence to pay homage above, resulting in cold on the left and coolness on the right. Therefore, there are high and low energies, with warmth and coolness. When something adapts to cold and heat, the body's cavities expand, and when there is warmth and heat, it produces boils; once the cavities have expanded and the boils have formed, this is the natural opening and closing of the body's pores, a manifestation of too little energy. The Emperor asked: How does this relate to life and death? The Duke of Zhou replied: The essence of yin causes people to live longer, and the descending essence of yang causes people to die earlier. The Emperor asked: Good. What should be done to treat the disease? The Duke of Zhou said: The energy of the west is dispersed and cold, while the energy of the south is concentrated and warm. This is what is meant by treating similar diseases differently. Therefore, for energy that is cold and energy that is warm, treat with coolness and heat respectively, and regulate water astringency. For energy that is warm and energy that is hot, strengthen the interior guard. If you can harmonize their energies, it will be beneficial, but if you misjudge this, the opposite will occur.
