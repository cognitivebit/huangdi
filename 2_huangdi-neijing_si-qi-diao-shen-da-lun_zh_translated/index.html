    四氣調神大論

    1. 春三月，此謂發陳，天地俱生，萬物以榮，夜臥早起，廣步於庭，被髮緩形，以使志生，生而勿殺，予而勿奪，賞而勿罰，此春氣之應養生之道也。逆之則傷肝，夏為寒變，奉長者少。夏三月，此謂蕃秀，天地氣交，萬物華實，夜臥早起，無厭於日，使志無怒，使華英成秀，使氣得泄，若所愛在外，此夏氣之應養長之道也。逆之則傷心，秋為痎瘧，奉收者少，冬至重病。秋三月，此謂容平，天氣以急，地氣以明，早臥早起，與雞俱興，使志安寧，以緩秋刑，收斂神氣，使秋氣平，無外其志，使肺氣清，此秋氣之應養收之道也，逆之則傷肺，冬為飱泄，奉藏者少。冬三月，此謂閉藏，水冰地坼，無擾乎陽，早臥晚起，必待日光，使志若伏若匿，若有私意，若已有得，去寒就溫，無泄皮膚使氣亟奪，此冬氣之應養藏之道也。逆之則傷腎，春為痿厥，奉生者少。 In the third month of spring, this refers to sending forth Chen. Heaven and Earth are all alive, and all things flourish through honor. Sleep early and rise early, walk with leisure in the courtyard, with hair and form softened, so as to nurture ambition. Do not kill when born, do not rob what is given, and do not punish when honoring. This is the way of nourishing the qi of spring for generating life. Reverse this, and it will harm the liver. Summer in the third month refers to Nan Xiu. Heaven and Earth's qi interact, all things blossom with ripe fruit. Sleep early and rise early, not tired of the sun, nurture ambition without anger, help the flowers bloom beautifully, let the qi be released like a cherished object outside. This is the way of nourishing the qi of summer for lengthening life. Reverse this, and it will harm the heart. Summer for diseases like cholera, those who follow will be fewer. In the third month of autumn, this refers to Tongping. Heaven's qi is urgent, and Earth's qi is clear. Sleep early and rise early, along with the rooster waking up, nurture a calm mind, moderate the harshness of autumn, recover and replenish the spirit, maintain the qi of autumn in balance, without external ambition, let the lung qi be clean, this is the way of nourishing the qi of autumn for collecting. Reverse this, and it will harm the lungs. Winter for exhaustion, those who store will be fewer. In the third month of winter, this refers to closing storage. The ice water and ruined land, do not disturb the Yang, sleep early and wake up late, waiting for sunlight, nurture ambition like hiding or concealing, if there are private intentions, if there is already something obtained, let go of the cold and approach warmth, do not release the qi to cause the skin to dry out and steal the qi. This is the way of nourishing the qi of winter for storing. Reverse this, and it will harm the kidneys. Spring for weakness and limpness, those who are born will be fewer.

    2. 天氣，清淨光明者也，藏德不止，故不下也。天明則日月不明，邪害空竅，陽氣者閉塞，地氣者冒明，雲霧不精，則上應白露不下。交通不表，萬物命故不施，不施則名木多死。惡氣不發，風雨不節，白露不下，則菀稾不榮。賊風數至，暴雨數起，天地四時不相保，與道相失，則未央絕滅。唯聖人從之，故身無奇病，萬物不失，生氣不竭。逆春氣，則少陽不生，肝氣內變。逆夏氣，則太陽不長，心氣內洞。逆秋氣，則太陰不收，肺氣焦滿。逆冬氣，則少陰不藏，腎氣獨沈。夫四時陰陽者，萬物之根本也。所以聖人春夏養陽，秋冬養陰，以從其根，故與萬物沈浮於生長之門。逆其根，則伐其本，壞其真矣。 The atmosphere is clear and bright, containing hidden virtues, therefore it does not descend. When the sun rises, the moon and stars are no longer visible; evil influences enter through the pores, the yang qi is blocked, and the earthly energy is manifest. The mists are not clear, then dew will not descend. There is confusion in traffic, and the lives of all things are not protected; without protection, there will be many dead trees. Evil qi does not disperse, winds and rains are not moderate, dew does not descend, and the millet will not grow well. The bandits' wind has arrived frequently, violent rains have occurred often, the heavens and earth do not protect their seasons, and they have lost the way of the Dao; then, the Central State will perish completely. Only the sage follows this and therefore has no strange diseases, all things are not lost, and the qi does not become exhausted. Reversing the spring qi, then the shining yang will not be born, and the liver's qi will vary internally. Reversing the summer qi, then the great yang will not grow, and the heart's qi will be hidden internally. Reversing the autumn qi, then the great yin will not shrink, and the lung's qi will become bitter and full. Reversing the winter qi, then the small yin will not hide, and the kidney's qi will sink alone. The four seasons' yin and yang are the roots of all things. Therefore, the sage nourishes yang in spring and summer, and nourishes yin in autumn and winter, following their roots, so he can harmonize with all things, sinking and floating at the door of their generation and growth. Reversing their roots is to cut off their source and destroy their true nature.

    3. 故陰陽四時者，萬物之終始也，死生之本也，逆之則災害生，從之則苛疾不起，是謂得道。道者，聖人行之，愚者佩之。從陰陽則生，逆之則死，從之則治，逆之則亂。反順為逆，是謂內格。是故聖人不治已病，治未病，不治已亂，治未亂，此之謂也。夫病已成而後藥之，亂已成而後治之，譬猶渴而穿井，鬭而鑄錐，不亦晚乎。 One who knows the yin and yang of the four seasons is the beginning and end of all things, the root of life and death. Reversing it brings calamity, following it brings harsh punishment that does not arise, so it is called attaining the Way. The Way is what sages practice, while fools wear it on their person. Following yin and yang brings life, reversing it brings death, following it brings order, reversing it brings chaos. Reversed is called inner potentiality. Therefore, sages do not treat after a disease has already formed, nor treat before one has yet to form; they do not bring order after chaos has already formed, nor treat before chaos has yet to form. This is what we mean. To cure after the illness has become established is like drawing water when thirsty, or forging a spear while under attack--is it not too late?
