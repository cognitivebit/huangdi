    五閱五使

    1. 黃帝問於歧伯曰：余聞刺有五官五閱，以觀五氣。五氣者，五藏之使也，五時之副也。願聞其五使當安出？歧伯曰：五官者，五藏之閱也。黃帝曰：願聞其所出，令可為常。歧伯曰：脈出於氣口，色見於明堂，五色更出，以應五時，各如其常，經氣入藏，必當治理。 The Yellow Emperor asked Yan Yuan, I have heard that there are five organs and five orifices for observing the five energies. The five energies are the functions of the five viscera and the assistants of the five phases. I wish to know where their five functions exit?

    Yan Yuan replied, The five organs are the means by which we observe the five viscera.

    The Yellow Emperor said, I wish to know where they exit, so that it can be made constant.

    Yan Yuan replied, The pulse comes out from the mouth of Qi and the color is seen in the hall of clarity. The five colors also come out, adapting to the five phases according to their constancy. As long as the Qi enters the viscera, it will surely regulate.

    1. 帝曰：善。五色獨決於明堂乎？歧伯曰：五官已辨，闕庭必張，乃立明堂，明堂廣大，蕃蔽見外，方壁高基，引垂居外，五色乃治，平搏廣大，壽中百歲，見此者，刺之必已，如是之人者，血氣有餘，肌肉堅致，故可苦以鍼。 The emperor asked: Good. Are the five colors decided independently in the bright hall? Minister Qi replied: The five senses have been distinguished, and the doors of the court will be closed, after which a bright hall is established. The bright hall is spacious, with walls extending to the borderlands, high and solid foundations, hanging drapes outside, and the five colors are arranged. The battlefield is vast, with people living for hundreds of years after seeing it. Those who see this will be pierced, and those of such a nature have abundant blood and qi, strong muscles that are firm and solid. Therefore, they can be subjected to torture with an awl

    2. 黃帝曰：願聞五官。歧伯曰：鼻者，肺之官也；目者，肝之官也；口唇者，脾之官也；舌者，心之官也；耳者，腎之官也。 Yellow Emperor said, I wish to hear about the five organs. Huan Bo replied: The nose belongs to the lung organ; the eyes belong to the liver organ; the mouth and lips belong to the spleen organ; the tongue belongs to the heart organ; and the ears belong to the kidney organ.

    3. 黃帝曰：以官何候？歧伯曰：以候五藏。故肺病者，喘息鼻張；肝病者，眥青；脾病者，唇黃；心病者，舌捲短，顴赤；腎病者，顴與顏黑。 Yellow Emperor asked: How can one tell if someone is an official? Boyi replied: One can determine it by the five zang organs. Therefore, a person with lung disease will have shortness of breath, sniffling nose; a person with liver disease will have yellow eyes; a person with spleen disease will have yellow lips; a person with heart disease will have short and swollen tongue, red cheeks; a person with kidney disease will have blackish cheeks and face.

    4. 黃帝曰：五脈安出，五色安見，其常色殆者如何？歧伯曰：五官不辨，闕庭不張，小其明堂，蕃蔽不見，又埤其牆，牆下無基，垂角去外。如是者，雖平常殆，況加疾哉。 Yellow Emperor said: When the five pulses are stable and the colors are calm, how about its constant color? The Duke of Zhou said: The five senses do not recognize it, the gateway of the court does not close, the brightness is small, the frontier areas cannot see it, and they also fill in the moat, with no foundation under the wall, and the corners are hanging outwards. If so, even if it is stable, how much more so when it becomes urgent.

    5. 黃帝曰：五色之見於明堂，以觀五藏之氣，左右高下，各有形乎？歧伯曰：藏府之在中也，各以次舍，左右上下，各如其度也。 Yellow Emperor said: The five colors are seen in the Bright Hall. Observe the five viscera's energy, up and down, each with a form? Duke of Zhou replied: The Depot Palace is within, each according to their respective proportions, up and down, according to their degrees.
