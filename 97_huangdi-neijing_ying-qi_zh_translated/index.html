    營氣

    1. 黃帝曰：營氣之道，內穀為寶。穀入于胃，乃傳之肺，流溢於中，布散於外，精專者，行於經隧，常營無已，終而復始，是謂天地之紀。故氣從太陰出注手陽明，上行注足陽明，下行至跗上，注大指間，與太陰合；上行抵髀，從脾注心中；循手少陰，出腋下臂，注小指，合手太陽；上行乘腋，出䪼內，注目內眥，上巔，下項，合足太陽；循脊，下尻，下行注小指之端，循足心，注足少陰；上行注腎，從腎注心外，散於胸中；循心主脈，出腋，下臂，出兩筋之間，入掌中，出中指之端，還注小指次指之端，合手少陽；上行注膻中，散於三焦，從三焦注膽，出脅，注足少陽；下行至跗上，復從跗注大指間，合足厥陰，上行至肝，從肝上注肺，上循喉嚨，入頏顙之竅，究於畜門。其支別者，上額，循巔，下項中，循脊入骶，是督脈也；絡陰器，上過毛中，入臍中，上循腹裏，入缺盆，下注肺中，復出太陰。此營氣之所行也，逆順之常也。 Yellow Emperor said: The practice of Qi, the grains are treasure. The grain enters the stomach and is transmitted by the lungs, overflowing in the middle and scattering externally. The refined one, follows the meridian, constantly practices Qi without end, returning to the beginning, this is called the record of heaven and earth. Therefore, Qi from the Conception vessel comes out and registers the Governor vessel, ascending and registering the foot Yangming, descending to the heel and registering at the intersection of the large intestine, merging with the Conception vessel; ascending striking the pubis, from the Spleen registering the heart center; following the Governor vessel, exiting the inframammary, registering the inner corner of the eye, up to the apex, down the sacrum, descending and registering the end of the small intestine, following the Huiyin, registering at the foot Shuyang; ascending riding the inguinal ligament, exiting the armpit, exiting between the biceps, entering the palm center, exiting the middle finger tip, returning to the small intestine and the end of the ring finger, merging with the Governor vessel. Ascending registering the Ren (Kidney), from the Kidney registering the exterior of the Heart, dispersing in the chest; following the Heart mother vessel, exiting the armpit, descending the arm, exiting between the biceps, entering the palm center, exiting the end of the middle finger, returning to the small intestine and the end of the ring finger, merging with the Governor vessel. Ascending registering the Governing vessel, dispersing in the three Jiao, from the three Jiao registering the Liver, ascending and registering the Lung, looping the throat, entering the nostril hole, concluding at the lactation gate. Its diverging branches, on the forehead, looping the vertex, down the sacrum, descending and registering the end of the small intestine, following the Governing vessel enters the coccyx, this is the Governor vessel; enveloping the Yin organs, ascending through the hair, entering the uterus, ascending looping the abdominal cavity, entering the sacrum, down registering the Lung, returning to the Conception vessel. This is the practice of Qi's course, reversal and progression are constant.
