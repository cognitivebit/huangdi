    標本病傳論

    1: 黃帝問曰：病有標本，刺有逆從，柰何。歧伯對曰：凡刺之方，必別陰陽，前後相應，逆從得施，標本相移。故曰：有其在標而求之於標，有其在本而求之於本，有其在本而求之於標，有其在標而求之於本，故治有取標而得者，有取本而得者，有逆取而得者，有從取而得者。故知逆與從，正行無問，知標本者，萬舉萬當，不知標本，是謂妄行。 Yellow Emperor asked: What are the principles of acupuncture? The Grand Master replied: Regarding any method of acupuncture, one must differentiate between yin and yang. Front and back correspond to each other, and following the norms leads to success. Thus it is said: In treating according to the standard and obtaining results from the standard, in treating according to the model and obtaining results from the model, in treating according to the locality and obtaining results from the standard, in treating according to the standard and obtaining results from the locality, there are those who succeed in obtaining results by following the standard, by following the locality, by taking what is inverse, and by taking what follows. Knowing inverse and following is essential for proper treatment; knowing the models and standards is essential for all actions and decisions; not knowing the models and standards leads to unwarranted action.

    2: 夫陰陽逆從，標本之為道也，小而大，言一而知百病之害，少而多，淺而博，可以言一而知百也。以淺而知深，察近而知遠，言標與本，易而勿及，治反為逆，治得為從。先病而後逆者治其本，先逆而後病者治其本，先寒而後生病者治其本，先病而後生寒者治其本，先熱而後生病者治其本，先熱而後生中滿者治其標，先病而後泄者治其本，先泄而後生他病者治其本，必且調之，乃治其他病，先病而後先中滿者治其標，先中滿而後煩心者治其本。人有客氣有同氣，小大不利治其標，小大利治其本。病發而有餘，本而標之，先治其本，後治其標。病發而不足標而本之，先治其標後治其本。謹察間甚，以意調之，閒者并行，甚者獨行，先小大不利而後生病者治其本。 Regarding yin and yang, the basic model is what the way is. Small yet all-encompassing, speaking of one thing can know a hundred diseases' harm, insufficient yet abundant, shallow yet extensive, it can be spoken of to know a hundred. Using the shallow to know the deep, observing close to know distant, speaking tags and roots, easy but not reaching, treating reversal as following, achieving it as following, first treating the disease and then the inverse, first treating the inverse and then the disease, first treating the cold and then causing disease, first treating the disease and then causing cold, first treating the heat and then causing disease, first treating the heat and then causing middle danger, first treating the disease and then expelling, first expelling and then causing other diseases, must certainly regulate it to treat other diseases. First treating the disease and then first middle danger, first treating middle danger and then disturbing the mind, treating the root. People have external and internal qi, small and large not suitable for treating the tags, small and large suitable for treating the roots. Diseases occur with residue, local and tag treatment, first treat the root, then treat the tag. Diseases occur without sufficient tag and root, first treat the tag, then treat the root. Carefully observe the intervals, adjust it by intention, idle parallel, profound alone, first small and large not suitable for causing disease, then causing disease, treat the root.

    3: 夫病傳者，心病先心痛，一日而欬，三日脇支痛，五日閉塞不通，身痛體重，三日不已，死，冬夜半，夏日中。肺病喘欬，三日而脇支滿痛，一日身重體痛，五日而脹，十日不已，死，冬日入，夏日出。肝病頭目眩脇支滿，三日體重身痛，五日而脹，三日腰脊少腹痛脛痠，三日不已，死，冬日入，夏早食。脾病身痛體重，一日而脹，二日少腹腰脊痛脛痠，三日背𦛗筋痛小便閉，十日不已，死，冬人定，夏晏食。腎病少腹腰脊痛䯒痠，三日背𦛗筋痛小便閉，三日腹脹，三日兩脇支痛，三日不已，死，冬大晨，夏晏晡。胃病脹滿，五日少腹腰脊痛䯒痠，三日背𦛗筋痛小便閉，五日身體重，六日不已，死，冬夜半後，夏日昳。膀胱病小便閉，五日少腹脹腰脊痛䯒痠，一日腹脹，一日身體痛，二日不已，死，冬雞鳴，夏下晡。諸病以次是相傳，如是者，皆有死期。不可刺。閒一藏止，及至三四藏者，乃可刺也。 The text describes the symptoms and progression of various diseases, followed by a caution against acupuncture. I'll translate it into English as follows:

    For those who suffer from illness, the heart is first affected with palpitations, followed by difficulty in swallowing, after one day; pain on the sides and abdomen after three days; and complete blockage after five days. The body feels heavy, and the symptoms persist for three days before death, which may occur during winter nights or summer days.

    Lung disease is characterized by wheezing, followed by severe pain on the sides and abdomen after three days; difficulty in breathing after five days; and continued symptoms for ten days before death, which may occur during winter or summer.

    Liver disease manifests with dizziness and pain on the sides, becoming progressively worse over three days, and is followed by a bloated abdomen, then pain in the hips, lower back, and inner thighs after five days; pain in the waist, groin, and knees after three days; and persistent symptoms that may lead to death during winter or summer.

    Spleen disease causes body and limb pain, leading to bloating after one day; abdominal pain and lower back pain after two days; severe pain in the muscles around the hips and difficulty urinating after three days; and continued symptoms for ten days before death, which may occur during winter or summer.

    Kidney disease is characterized by pain in the lower abdomen, lower back, and loins, followed by bloating and pain on both sides of the waist after three days; pain in the flanks after three days; persistent symptoms that may lead to death during winter mornings or summer afternoons.

    Stomach disease causes bloating, followed by abdominal pain and lower back pain after five days; severe pain in the muscles around the hips and difficulty urinating after five days; and a feeling of heaviness throughout the body after six days, leading to death during winter nights or summer days.

    Bladder disease results in difficulty urinating, followed by abdominal pain, lower back pain, and chills after five days; abdominal bloating after one day; and body pain that persists for two days before death, which may occur during winter cockcrow or summer afternoons.

    In summary, the different stages of diseases are characterized by distinct symptoms, and those who reach these stages have a fixed time to live. Acupuncture should not be performed in such cases. However, after an interval of one to four days, acupuncture may be applied for those who do not show any signs of recovery.
