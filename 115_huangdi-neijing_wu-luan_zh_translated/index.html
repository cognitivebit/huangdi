    五亂

    1. 黃帝曰：經脈十二者，別為五行，分為四時，何失而亂？何得而治？歧伯曰：五行有序，四時有分，相順則治，相逆則亂。 The Yellow Emperor said: The twelve channels are divided into five phases and classified into four seasons. How can they become disordered? How can they be regulated? The Duke of Shou replied: When the phases are in order, and the seasons have their respective divisions, following this order leads to regulation, while going against it results in disorder

    2. 黃帝曰：何謂相順？歧伯曰：經脈十二者，以應十二月。十二月者，分為四時。四時者，春秋冬夏，其氣各異，營衛相隨，陰陽已知，清濁不相干，如是則順之而治。 Yellow Emperor said: What does it mean to follow the rhythm? The Duke of Zhou said: The twelve channels correspond to the twelve months. The twelve months are divided into four seasons. The four seasons have different qi, and the defense and nourishment adjust according to them. Yin and Yang are understood, and clean and unclean do not interfere with each other. If so, follow it and govern.

    3. 黃帝曰：何為逆而亂，歧伯曰：清氣在陰，濁氣在陽，營氣順脈，衛氣逆行，清濁相干，亂於胸中，是謂大悗。故氣亂於心，則煩心密嘿，俛首靜伏；亂於肺，則俛仰喘喝，接手以呼；亂於腸胃，是為霍亂；亂於臂脛，則為四厥；亂於頭，則為厥逆，頭重眩仆。 Yellow Emperor said: What is the cause of chaos? Master Qi Bo replied: The clear Qi is in the Yin, and the turbid Qi is in the Yang. Regulating Qi follows the pulse, defending Qi moves against the grain. Clear and turbid are related, causing chaos within. This is called great distress. Therefore, when Qi is chaotic in the heart, the mind becomes restless and anxious, crouching with a hunched back; when it is chaotic in the lungs, one experiences shortness of breath and gasping, grasping for air; when it is chaotic in the stomach and intestines, it is called huo chaos; when it is chaotic in the arms and legs, it results in the four paralyses; when it is chaotic in the head, one experiences dizziness and weakness of the head.

    4. 黃帝曰：五亂者，刺之有道乎？歧伯曰：有道以來，有道以去，審知其道，是謂身寶。黃帝曰：善。願聞其道。歧伯曰：氣在於心者，取之手少陰心主之俞；氣在於肺者，取之手太陰滎，足少陰俞，氣在於腸胃者，取之足太陰陽明，不下者，取之三里，氣在於頭者，取之天柱大杼，不知，取足太陽滎俞；氣在於臂足，取之先去血脈，後取其陽明少陽之滎輸。 Yellow Emperor said: What is the way of five chaoses? Can one pierce it with needles? Huan Bo said: The way that has come and gone can be known. This is called the treasure within oneself. Yellow Emperor said: Good! I wish to hear its way. Huan Bo said: The qi is in the heart, take hold of the hand's Shao Yin ruler; the qi is in the lungs, take hold of the hand's Tai Yin flood and foot's Shao Yin ruler; the qi is in the intestines and stomach, take hold of the foot's Tai Yin Yang Ming, not going down, take hold of the three miles; the qi is in the head, take hold of the Tian Zhu Da Quan; if unknown, take hold of the foot's Tai Yang ruler. The qi is in the arms and legs, first remove the blood vessels, then take hold of the Yang Ming Shao Yuan output.

    5. 黃帝曰：補寫奈何？歧伯曰：徐入徐出，謂之導氣。補寫無形，謂之同精。是非有餘不足也，亂氣之相逆也。黃帝曰：允乎哉道，明乎哉論，請著之玉版，命曰治亂也。 Yellow Emperor said: How about supplementation and revision? Kuang Bo said: Enter and exit slowly, referring to guiding the Qi. Supplementation and revision have no form, referring to being refined. If there is insufficiency or excess, it will result in the reversal of the Qi. Yellow Emperor said: How marvelous is this way, how clear is this discussion! I shall inscribe it on jade and call it Regulating Chaos.
