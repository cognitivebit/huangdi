    腸胃

    1. 黃帝問於伯高曰：余願聞六府傳谷者，腸胃之大小長短，受谷之多少奈何？伯高曰：請盡言之，谷所從出入淺深遠近長短之度：唇至齒長九分，口廣二寸半；齒以後至會厭，深三寸半，大容五合；舌重十兩，長七寸，廣二寸半；咽門重十兩，廣一寸半。至胃長一尺六寸，胃紆曲屈，伸之，長二尺六寸，大一尺五寸，徑五寸，大容三斗五升。小腸後附脊，左環回日迭積，其注于回腸者，外附於臍上。回運環十六曲，大二寸半，徑八分分之少半，長三丈三尺。回腸當臍左環，回周葉積而下，回運還反十六曲，大四寸，徑一寸寸之少半，長二丈一尺。廣腸傳脊，以受回腸，左環葉脊上下，辟大八寸，徑二寸寸之大半，長二尺八寸。腸胃所入至所出，長六丈四寸四分，回曲環反，三十二曲也。 Emperor Yellow asked Bo Gao: I wish to hear the classic of the Six Canons. How do the intestines and stomach differ in size, length, and capacity? Bo Gao replied: Please proceed with your explanation. The extent of the intestines and stomach in terms of shallow, deep, near or far, long or short is as follows: the lips reach to the teeth and are nine parts long; the mouth is two and a half inches wide; the teeth lead to the heel, which is three and a half inches deep; it is five fists in size; the tongue weighs ten pounds and is seven feet long, two and a half inches wide; the throat weighs ten pounds and is one and a half inches wide; the stomach is one foot six inches long, curved, stretched out, reaching two feet six inches long, three feet five inches in size, five inches in width, and has a capacity of three bushels and five pints. The small intestine adheres to the spine, coils to the left, day after day accumulating, and is connected to the large intestine at the coil. The large intestine is connected to the right side of the abdomen. The coiled intestine is one foot six inches in length, two feet three feet in diameter, slightly less than half an inch wide, and three feet three inches in length. The coiled intestine passes through the right side of the abdomen, coils around the large intestine, and returns to its original state after 16 turns, measuring four inches, slightly less than a third of an inch wide, and two feet one inch in length. The expanded intestine extends from the spine, takes in the coiled intestine, left-coiling at the spine above and below, occupying eight inches, with a width of two inches; the large and small intestines enter and exit the body, measuring six feet four inches in total length, curving and coiling 32 times.
