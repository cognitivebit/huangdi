    陰陽類論

    1. 孟春始至，黃帝燕坐，臨觀八極，正八風之氣，而問雷公曰：陰陽之類，經脈之道，五中所主，何藏最貴。雷公對曰：春，甲乙青，中主肝，治七十二日，是脈之主時，臣以其藏最貴。帝曰：卻念上下經陰陽從容，子所言貴，最其下也。雷公致齋七日，旦復侍坐。帝曰：三陽為經，二陽為維，一陽為游部，此知五藏終始。三陽為表，二陰為裏，一陰至絕作朔晦，卻具合以正其理。雷公曰：受業未能明。帝曰：所謂三陽者，太陽為經，三陽脈至手太陰，弦浮而不沈，決以度，察以心，合之陰陽之論。所謂二陽者，陽明也，至手太陰，弦而沈急不鼓，炅至以病皆死。一陽者，少陽也，至手太陰，上連人迎弦急懸不絕，此少陽之病也，專陰則死。三陰者，六經之所主也，交於太陰，伏鼓不浮，上空志心。二陰至肺，其氣歸膀胱，外連脾胃。一陰獨至，經絕氣浮不鼓，釣而滑。此六脈者，乍陰乍陽，交屬相并，繆通五藏，合於陰陽，先至為主，後至為客。 Mengchun first arrived, the Yellow Emperor Yin sat, facing the observation of Ba Jí, regulating the Qi of the eight winds, and asked Master Lao Gong: The patterns of yin and yang, the meridians' ways, the five centers' masters, what is most precious in concealment? Lao Gong answered: Spring, jia and bing green, governs the liver, treats the seventy-two days, it is the time of the meridian, so I regard its concealment as most precious. The Emperor said: I suddenly think of the upper and lower journeys of yin and yang, what you said is valuable, most respected below. Lao Gong kowtowed for seven days, then served again at dawn. The Emperor said: Three yangs are the channel, two yangs are the mantle, one yang is the wandering department, this knows the five orifices beginning and end. Three yangs are the skin, two yin are the lungs, deep and urgent not resonating, when it reaches, all diseases will die. One yang is the shallow yang, at the hand Taiyang, rising and not sinking, deciding by measure, understanding by heart, combining yin and yang's reasoning. Two yangs are the Yang Ming, at the hand Taiyang, resonating and sinking urgent not resonating, when it reaches, all diseases will die. One yang is the Shao Yang, at the hand Taiyang, rising and connecting people's greeting string urgent not cut off, this is Shao Yang's disease, then there is interior. Three yin are the master of the six meridians, unite in the Taiyang, hide the drum without floating, aiming at the heart spirit. Two yins arrive at the lungs, their qi returns to the bladder, outer connecting spleen and stomach. One yin alone arrives, the meridian's qi floats without resonating, hangs and slides. These six meridians, alternately yin and yang, intertwine and overlap, link the five orifices, combine in yin and yang, begin as master, end as guest.

    2. 雷公曰：臣悉盡意，受傳經脈，頌得從容之道，以合從容，不知陰陽，不知雌雄。帝曰：三陽為父，二陽為衛，一陽為紀。三陰為母，二陰為雌，一陰為獨使。二陽一陰，陽明主病，不勝一陰，耎而動，九竅皆沈。三陽一陰，太陽脈勝，一陰不能止，內亂五藏，外為驚駭。二陰二陽，病在肺，少陰脈沈，勝肺傷脾，外傷四支。二陰二陽皆交至，病在腎，罵詈妄行，巔疾為狂。二陰一陽，病出於腎，陰氣客遊於心，脘下空竅，堤閉塞不通，四支別離。一陰一陽代絕，此陰氣至心，上下無常，出入不知，喉咽乾燥，病在土脾。二陽三陰，至陰皆在，陰不過陽，陽氣不能止陰，陰陽並絕，浮為血瘕，沈為膿胕。陰陽皆壯，下至陰陽，上合昭昭，下合冥冥，診決死生之期，遂合歲首。 Lei Gong said: I fully understand the intention, receive the transmissions of the classics and the pulse, praise the Way of serenity, harmonize with serenity, not knowing yin and yang. The Emperor said: Three Yang is the father, two Yang is defense, one Yang is record. Three Yin is the mother, two Yin is yin, one Yin is independent officer. Two Yang and one Yin, Yang Ming governs disease, unable to overcome one Yin, trembling and moving, all nine orifices sink. Three Yang and one Yin, Tai Yang pulse prevails, one Yin cannot stop, internal chaos of the five organs, external is fear. Two Yin two Yang, disease in the lungs, small Yin pulse sinking, superior lung hurts liver, external harm the limbs. Two Yin two Yang all meet at, disease in the kidneys, shouting, madness. Two Yin one Yang, disease from the kidneys, yin qi wandering in the heart, hollowness in the lower abdomen, dam blocked and closed, limbs scattered. One Yin one Yang substitution, this yin qi arrives at the heart, up and down is uncertain, coming and going does not know, throat dryness disease. Two Yang three Yin, all yin is present, yin not beyond yang, yang qi unable to stop yin, yin and yang are absorbed, floating as blood spots, sinking as pus. Yin and yang are both strong, below at yin and yang, above is clear, below is dark, diagnose life and death period, thus harmonize New Year's Day.

    3. 雷公曰：請問短期。黃帝不應。雷公復問。黃帝曰：在經論中。雷公曰：請聞短期。黃帝曰：冬三月之病，病合於陽者，至春正月脈有死徵，皆歸出春。冬三月之病，在理已盡，草與柳葉皆殺，春陰陽皆絕，期在孟春。春三月之病，曰陽殺，陰陽皆絕，期在草乾。夏三月之病，至陰不過十日，陰陽交，期在溓水。秋三月之病，三陽俱起，不治自已。陰陽交合者，立不能坐，坐不能起。三陽獨至，期在石水。二陰獨至，期在盛水。 Master Lái asked, What is the short term? The Emperor said no. Master Lái asked again, In the classic texts, what is the short term? The Emperor said, The illness of the three months of winter, which affects the Yang aspect, will show signs of death in the month of spring when the pulse indicates danger; these will all recover by the beginning of spring. In the case of the three months of winter's illness, the principles are complete, and the grass and willows will all be killed, while in spring the Yin and Yang will both be cut off, and the prognosis is in the vernal equinox. The illness of the three months of spring, known as the Yang killing, will have both the Yin and Yang cut off, and the prognosis will be at the withering stage. The illness of the three months of summer, if the Yin does not last beyond ten days, will have the Yin and Yang balanced, and the prognosis will be at the flooding stage. The illness of the three months of autumn, when all three Yangs rise but cannot be treated, will have a prognosis by itself. If the Yin and Yang are combined and cannot sit down, they cannot get up; if three Yangs arise alone, the prognosis is at the stone-water stage. If two Yins arise alone, the prognosis is at the flood stage.
