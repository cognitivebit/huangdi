    賊風

    1. 黃帝曰：夫子言賊風邪氣之傷人也，令人病焉，今有其不離屏蔽，不出室穴之中，卒然病者，非不離賊風邪氣，其故何也？歧伯曰：此皆嘗有所傷於濕氣，藏於血脈之中，分肉之間，久留而不去。若有所墮墜，惡血在內而不去，卒然喜怒不節，飲食不適，寒溫不時，腠理閉而不通。其開而遇風寒，則血氣凝結，與故邪相襲，則為寒痺。其有熱則汗出，汗出則受風，雖不遇賊風邪氣，必有因加而發焉。 Yellow Emperor said: Master said that the thief of wind and heat harms people, making them sick. Now, one cannot leave the screen and does not come out from the cave-like rooms; what if someone suddenly becomes ill? Is it not due to the thief of wind and heat? Huzi said: It is all because there has been some damage caused by dampness, hidden in the blood vessels, dividing the flesh, and lingering without disappearing. If one falls or collapses, the evil blood does not go away, and one suddenly becomes angry and frustrated, eats inappropriately, does not adjust to hot or cold temperatures, and the channels close up without circulation. Once they open and encounter cold winds, the blood and qi congeal and clash with the old pathogens, which results in cold paralysis. If there is fever, sweat will come out, sweating will catch a cold, even though it does not encounter the thief of wind and heat, it must have a reason for causing it.

    2. 黃帝曰：今夫子之所言者，皆病人之所自知也。其毋所遇邪氣，又毋怵惕之所志，卒然而病者，其故何也？唯有因鬼神之事乎？歧伯曰：此亦有故邪留而未發，因而志有所惡，及有所慕，血氣內亂，兩氣相搏。其所從來者微，視之不見，聽而不聞，故似鬼神。 Yellow Emperor said: Whatever Confucius speaks of today, it is all something that a sick person knows themselves. Those who have not encountered deviant qi and do not understand the intentions of the sick person, suddenly fall ill - what is the reason for this? The only possibility is being influenced by ghosts and spirits? Zhou Bo said: There is also an underlying cause that has remained hidden and has not yet manifested. As a result, one's intentions have something they dislike, and something they admire. Blood and qi become chaotic inside, and the two qis struggle against each other. Its origin is subtle, and it cannot be seen when looking at it, nor can it be heard when listening to it, so it seems like ghosts and spirits.

    3. 黃帝曰：其祝而已者，其故何也？歧伯曰：先巫者，因知百病之勝，先知其病之所從生者，可祝而已也。 Yellow Emperor said: What is the reason for your offering? Kuafu replied: The senior diviner knows that of the hundred diseases, knowing where they originate from can prevent them. Therefore, I have offered this advice.
