    氣穴論

    1: 黃帝問曰：余聞氣穴三百六十五，以應一歲，未知其所，願卒聞之。歧伯稽首再拜對曰：窘乎哉問也。其非聖帝，孰能窮其道焉，因請溢意盡言其處。帝捧手逡巡而卻曰：夫子之開余道也，目未見其處，耳未聞其數，而目以明，耳以聰矣。歧伯曰：此所謂聖人易語，良馬易御也。帝曰：余非聖人之易語也，世言真數開人意，今余所訪問者真數，發蒙解惑，未足以論也。然余願聞夫子溢志盡言其處，令解其意，請藏之金匱，不敢復出。歧伯再拜而起曰：臣請言之，背與心相控而痛，所治天突與十椎及上紀，上紀者，胃脘也，下紀者，關元也。背胸邪繫陰陽左右。如此其病前後痛濇，胸脇痛而不得息不得臥，上氣短氣偏痛，脈滿起斜出尻脈，絡胸脇支心貫鬲，上肩加天突，斜下肩交十椎下。藏俞五十穴，府俞七十二穴，熱俞五十九穴，水俞五十七穴，頭上五行行五，五五二十五穴，中𦛗兩傍各五，凡十穴，大椎上兩傍各一，凡二穴，目瞳子浮白二穴，兩脾厭分中二穴，犢鼻二穴，耳中多所聞二穴，眉本二穴，完骨二穴，頂中央一穴，枕骨二穴，上關二穴，大迎二穴，下關二穴，天柱二穴，巨虛上下廉四穴，曲牙二穴，天突一穴，天府二穴，天牖二穴，扶突二穴，天䆫二穴，肩解二穴，關元一穴，委陽二穴，肩貞二穴，瘖門一穴，齊一穴，胸俞十二穴，背俞二穴，膺俞十二穴，分肉二穴，踝上橫二穴，陰陽蹻四穴，水俞在諸分，熱俞在氣穴，寒熱俞在兩骸厭中二穴，大禁二十五，在天府下五寸，凡三百六十五穴，鍼之所由行也。 Emperor asked, saying, I have heard that there are 365 acupuncture points to respond to one year. I am not aware of their locations. I would like to hear about them once again. Tang of Zhou bowed, paid respects and replied, What a difficult question! It is not that the sacred emperor cannot fathom it. So I humbly ask to expound in detail on their locations. The Emperor held out his hand and circumambulated saying, Your opening of my path; I have not seen its location, nor heard of its number, yet through you I am enlightened, and my ears are informed. Tang of Zhou said, This is what is called the easy language of a sage and the ease of controlling a good horse. The Emperor said, My easy language of a sage has not yet opened people's minds. Today, the truth I sought from you, the truth has enlightened me and dispelled my confusion. It is insufficient to discuss it. However, the Emperor requested Tang of Zhou to expound in detail on their locations, letting him understand their meanings, and asked him to conceal it in a golden casket, never to be revealed again. Tang of Zhou bowed and replied, Your subject explains it, my heart is pained and controlled, treating Heaven's lower aperture with Ten Windows and Upper Aperture. The Upper Aperture is the diaphragm, while the Lower Aperture is the Gate of Life. Back-muscle and Yang channels interweave to the left and right. If the disease is at the front, there will be pain in the back; if there is pain in the chest and diaphragm, one cannot breathe or lie down, with shortness of breath and pain on one side, the pulse will be full and slanting out of the guàn mèi. There are 57 water points, and 1 head point for each of the Five Lines. In total, there are 365 acupuncture points. These are the pathways to treat diseases.

    2: 帝曰：余已知氣穴之處，遊鍼之居，願聞孫絡谿谷，亦有所應乎。歧伯曰：孫絡三百六十五穴會，亦以應一歲，以溢奇邪，以通榮衛，榮衛稽留，衛散榮溢，氣竭血著，外為發熱，內為少氣，疾寫無怠，以通榮衛，見而寫之，無問所會。帝曰：善。 Emperor: I have already learned the locations of the meridians, and practiced acupuncture at their points. Is there anything that Sun Ssu-ma has done in relation to the valley of Guan? Huan Bo: Sun Ssu-ma gathered 365 meridian points, which he applied for a year, excessively treating oddities, passing through the glorious defense, the defense dispersing gloriously, exhausting qi and blood, leading to exterior fever and interior deficiency of qi. It is urgent without rest, passing through the glorious defense, seeing it and writing about it without asking where he practiced. Emperor: Good

    3: 願聞谿谷之會也。歧伯曰：肉之大會為谷，肉之小會為谿，肉分之閒，谿谷之會，以行榮衛，以會大氣。邪溢氣壅，脈熱肉敗榮衛不行，必將為膿內銷骨髓，外破大膕，留於節湊，必將為敗。積寒留舍，榮衛不居，卷肉縮筋，肋肘不得伸，內為骨痺，外為不仁，命曰不足，大寒留於谿谷也。谿谷三百六十五穴會，亦應一歲。其小痺淫溢，循脈往來，微鍼所及，與法相同。 I wish to hear about the meeting of the willows and valleys. The oblique says: the great assembly of meat refers to a valley, the small assembly of meat refers to a willow grove, the division of meat is leisurely, and the meeting of willow valleys, through activity and honor guards, it is large qi. Excess qi blocks, the pulse is hot, meat fails, and honor guards do not function, and it must become pus inside, break out outside, leaving at the joints, must be ruined. Accumulating coldness and staying in the shed, honor guards not residing, rolled flesh shrinks sinews, rib joints not being able to extend, inside is bone sequelae, outside is lack of benevolence, command says insufficient, great cold accumulates at willow valleys. The meeting of the willow valleys has 365 acupoints gathering, and should also be once a year. Its small sequelae lewdness excessively blocks, following the pulse going back and forth, micro-needling at the appropriate place, and following the law.

    4: 帝乃辟左右而起再拜曰：今日發蒙解惑，藏之金匱，不敢復出，乃藏之金蘭之室，署曰氣穴所在。歧伯曰：孫絡之脈別經者，其血盛而當寫者，亦三百六十五脈，並注於絡，傳注十二絡脈，非獨十四絡脈也，內解寫於中者十脈。 The Emperor said, I have created the left and right and now stand up to offer my reverence again. Today I bestow wisdom and dispel confusion, sealing them within the Golden Cabinet and dare not reveal them again. I shall hide them in the room of the Jade Lady and name it the Place Where Qi Acupoints Are Located. The Duke said, The Unique Channel's branch channel is different from the Su Wen. There are a total of 365 channels, all recorded on the Unique Channel. It transmits the twelve channel vessels, but not just the fourteen channel vessels. Internal explanations are written in the middle of the text.
