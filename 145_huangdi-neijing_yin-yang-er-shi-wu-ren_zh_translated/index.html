    陰陽二十五人

    1. 黃帝曰：余聞陰陽之人何如？伯高曰：天地之間，六合之內，不離於五，人亦應之。故五五二十五人之政，而陰陽之人不與焉。其態又不合於眾者五，余已知之矣。願聞二十五人之形，血氣之所生，別而以候，從外知內，何如？歧伯曰：悉乎哉問也，此先師之秘也，雖伯高猶不能明之也。黃帝避席遵循而卻曰：余聞之得其人弗教，是謂重失，得而泄之，天將厭之，余願得而明之，金櫃藏之，不敢揚之。歧伯曰：先立五形金木水火土，別其五色，異其五形之人，而二十五人具矣。黃帝曰：願卒聞之。歧伯曰：慎之慎之，臣請言之。 Yellow Emperor said: What of those skilled in Yin and Yang? Bo Gao replied: Within the interval between Heaven and Earth, within the Six Cycles, there is no place without the Five. Man should also follow this pattern. Thus, among the Twenty-Five Hegemons, there are those skilled in Yin and Yang who are not part of it; their conduct does not conform to that of the Five. I already know this. I would like to hear about the form of the Twenty-Five Hegemons, the place where blood and qi are generated, separating them as omens and knowing the inside from the outside. How is this done?

    Huangdi (Yellow Emperor) avoided the seat and followed along while saying: I have heard that those who possess this person do not teach it, which is a great loss. Obtaining it and then disclosing it, the Heavens will be weary of it. I wish to understand it and keep it hidden in my heart, not daring to reveal it.

    Huangdi (Yellow Emperor) said: Please allow me to hear about this one more time. Huangdi (Yellow Emperor) replied: Please proceed with caution. My lord, may I dare speak of it?

    First establish the Five Forms of Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth, distinguish their five colors, and differentiate the forms of the people among the Twenty-Five Hegemons, and they will be complete. Yellow Emperor said: Please allow me to hear this one more time. Huangdi (Yellow Emperor) replied: Proceed with great care. My lord, may I dare speak about it?

    First establish the Five Forms of Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth, distinguish their five colors, and differentiate the forms of the people among the Twenty-Five Hegemons, and they will be complete. Yellow Emperor said: Please allow me to hear this one more time.

    1. 木形之人，比于上角似於蒼帝，其為人蒼色，小頭，長面大肩背直身小，手足好。有才，勞心少力多憂，勞於事，能春夏不能秋冬感而病生。足厥陰，佗佗然，大角之人比於左足少陽，少陽之上遺遺然。左角之人比於右足少陽，少陽之下隨隨然。鈦角之人，比於右足少陽，少陽之上推推然。判角之人比於左足少陽，少陽之下枯枯然。 The man with wooden limbs was like the Azure Emperor in his left corner, having an azure complexion, a small head, a long face, broad shoulders, and straight back, and his hands and feet were well-formed. He had talent but worked hard and was weak and often worried. When he engaged in tasks, he could not withstand the heat of spring and summer but could not bear the chill of autumn and winter, resulting in illness. His left foot had excess yin, dragging along carelessly, while his large horned man was like the left foot's slightly yang, lingering carelessly. The right-horned man was like the right foot's slightly yang, following along carelessly. The ox-horned man was like the right foot's slightly yang, gently pushing along. The goat-horned man was like the left foot's slightly yang, withering away carelessly.

    2. 火形之人，比於上徵，似於赤帝。其為人赤色廣䏖脫面小頭，好肩背，髀腹小手足，行安地疾心，行搖肩背肉滿。有氣輕財少信多慮，見事明好顏，急心不壽暴死。能春夏不能秋冬，秋冬感而病生，手少陰核核然。質徵之人，比於左手太陽，太陽之上，肌肌然，少徵之人比於右手太陽，太陽之下慆慆然，右徵之人比於右手太陽，太陽之上鮫鮫然。質判之人，比於左手太陽，太陽之下支支頤頤然。 The person with fire marks is like the upper summoning and resembles the Red Emperor. This person has a red skin broad face, narrow head, is good at shoulders and back, hips and abdomen, small hands and feet, moves securely on land, and moves nimbly while shaking shoulders and back with flesh filling. They have qi that is light, wealthy, and has little belief but much thought, see events clearly with a beautiful countenance, are anxious about the heart without having longevity and suffer violent death. They can adapt to spring and summer but not autumn and winter. In autumn and winter, they become cold and sick, their hands have yin cores that are like grains of sand. The person seeking quality is like the left hand Yang, on Yang's upper part, muscle-like, the person seeking little is like the right hand Yang, on Yang's lower part, dazed-like, and the person seeking on the right hand Yang, on Yang's upper part, fish-like. The person judging quality is like the left hand Yang, on Yang's lower part supportive and trembling-like

    3. 土形之人，比於上宮，似於上古黃帝，其為人黃色圓面、大頭、美肩背、大腹、美股脛、小手足、多肉、上下相稱行安地，舉足浮。安心，好利人不喜權勢，善附人也。能秋冬不能春夏，春夏感而病生，足太陰，敦敦然。大宮之人比於左足陽明，陽明之上婉婉然。加宮之人，比於左足陽明，陽明之下坎坎然。少宮之人，比於右足陽明，陽明之上，樞樞然。左宮之人，比於右足陽明，陽明之下，兀兀然。 People of the Earth, compared to those in the Upper Palace, resemble the ancient Yellow Emperor. They are characterized by a yellow human form, round face, large head, beautiful shoulders and back, large abdomen, beautiful thighs and calves, small hands and feet, abundant flesh, and are stable and secure when standing or moving. They dislike powerful people, are good at attaching themselves to others, and can withstand autumn and winter but not spring and summer. In spring and summer, they are affected and fall ill, having a weak Taiyin condition, appearing feeble. The Upper Palace people resemble the left foot Yangming, with delicate movements. The Middle Palace people resemble the left foot Yangming, with fluctuating movements. The Lower Palace people resemble the right foot Yangming, with stable movements. The Right Palace people resemble the right foot Yangming, with fluctuating movements

    4. 金形之人比於上商，似於白帝，其為人方面白色、小頭、小肩背小腹、小手足如骨發踵外，骨輕。身清廉，急心靜悍，善為吏，能秋冬，不能春夏，春夏感而病生。手太陰敦敦然，釱商之人比於左手陽明，陽明之上，廉廉然。右商之人，比於左手陽明，陽明之下脫脫然。左商之人比於右手陽明，陽明之上監監然。少商之人，比於右手陽明，陽明之下，嚴嚴然。 The man of metal compares to the Sage Emperor Shun, and resembles the Emperor of White. He is fair-skinned, small-headed, small-shouldered, and has a small waist and small hands and feet like bones protruding at the heels. His bones are light. He is clean and upright, with a keen heart and quick to act, skilled in serving as an official, proficient in autumn and winter but unable to handle spring and summer, feeling unwell in spring and summer. His left hand resembles Taoyin, with Taoyin's man resembling Yangming above, clear and clean. The right hand resembles Shang, with Shang resembling Yangming below, slipping away and clean. The left hand resembles the left hand of Yangming, standing above in a watchful manner. The Shaohao man resembles the right hand of Yangming, Yangming below, strict and stern.

    5. 水形之人，比於上羽，似於黑帝，其為人，黑色面不平，大頭廉頤，小肩大腹動手足，發行搖身下尻長，背延延然。不敬畏善欺紹人，戮死。能秋冬不能春夏，春夏感而病生。足少陰汗汗然。大羽之人，比於右足太陽，太陽之上，頰頰然。少羽之人，比於左足太陽，太陽之下潔潔然。眾之為人，比於右足太陽，太陽之下潔潔然。桎之為人，比於左足太陽，太陽之上安安然。是故五形之人二十五變者，眾之所以相欺者是也。 People shaped like water are compared to Yu (rain), resembling the Black Emperor. Their skin is black and not flat, with a large head and chubby cheeks, small shoulders and a big belly, moving their hands and feet nimbly, swaying their hips as they walk, and their backs extended. They are not afraid of showing disrespect or being deceitful towards the Shu people and will kill them. Those who can endure autumn and winter but cannot handle spring and summer become sick from the heat. Their sweaty feet have lots of yin sweat. People shaped like great wings are compared to the right foot Yang, on top of the Yang. Little wing-shaped people are compared to the left foot Yang, beneath it in a clean manner. The majority of people are compared to the right foot Yang, beneath it in a clean manner. And the reed-shaped people are compared to the left foot Yang, above it in peace. Therefore, the five shapes of people have 25 variations, and this is what the crowd uses to deceive each other.

    6. 黃帝曰：得其形，不得其色何如？歧伯曰：形勝色，色勝形者，至其勝時年加，感則病行，失則憂矣。形色相得者，富貴大樂。黃帝曰：其形色相勝之時，年加可知乎？歧伯曰：凡年忌下上之人，大忌常加七歲，十六歲、二十五歲、三十四歲、四十三歲、五十二歲、六十一歲皆人之大忌，不可不自安也，感則病行，失則憂矣，當此之時，無為姦事，是謂年忌。 Yellow Emperor asked: What if one acquires the form but not the complexion? How about that? Boyi replied: The form is superior to the complexion, and the complexion is superior to the form at its peak time. When years add on, one feels ill and suffers from diseases. If they are both obtained, great happiness and honor result. Yellow Emperor asked: At the time when the form and complexion are both superior, can one know when the years add on? Boyi replied: Anyone who is above seventy years old, the major taboo is to add seven years every ten years, sixteen years, twenty-five years, thirty-four years, forty-three years, fifty-two years, and sixty-one years. These are major taboos that one must not neglect oneself, feel ill and suffer from diseases. At such a time, without doing anything improper, this is what we call the taboo of adding years.

    7. 黃帝曰：夫子之言脈之上下，血氣之候似知形氣，奈何？歧伯曰：足陽明之上血氣盛則髯美長，血少氣多則髯短，故氣少血多則髯少，血氣皆少則無髯。兩吻多畫，足陽明之下血氣盛則下毛美長至胸，血多氣少則下毛美短至臍，行則善高舉足，足趾少肉足善寒，血少氣多則肉而善瘃，血氣皆少則無毛有則稀、枯悴，善痿厥，足痺。 Yellow Emperor said: The Master's words relate to the flow of blood and qi above and below. How does this pertain to the formation and appearance of hair? Boyang replied: Abundant blood and qi in the foot Jueyin channel lead to long and luxuriant eyebrows, while deficient blood and abundant qi cause short eyebrows. Hence, if qi is deficient and blood is abundant, the eyebrows will be sparse; if both are deficient, there will be no eyebrows. The upper lips are filled with lines, and in the foot Jueyin channel below, abundant blood and qi lead to long and luxuriant pubic hair that reaches the chest, while an excess of blood and deficient qi results in short pubic hair that is close to the genitals, leading to good gait and high-stepping feet. The soles of the feet have less flesh, which makes them suitable for cold climates. A deficiency of blood and abundance of qi will result in fleshy growths and suitability for sweating. If both are deficient, there may be hair but it will be sparse and dry, leading to weakness, numbness of the feet, and a tendency towards paralysis.

    8. 足少陽之上，氣血盛則通髯美長，血多氣少則通髯美短，血少氣多則少髯，血氣皆少則無鬚，感於寒濕則善痺。骨痛爪枯也。足少陽之下，血氣盛則脛毛美長，外踝肥；血多氣少則脛毛美短，外踝皮堅而厚，血少氣多則胻毛少，外踝皮薄而軟，血氣皆少則無毛，外踝瘦無肉。 Above the foot yang channel, when qi and blood are abundant, the beard is long and beautiful, and the mustache is long. When there is more blood and less qi, the beard is short, but the mustache is still beautiful. If there is a deficiency of both qi and blood, then there will be no beard. If one is affected by cold and dampness, it is beneficial for the genitals. Bone pain and brittle nails are also indicative. Below the foot yang channel, when qi and blood are abundant, the hair on the calves is beautiful and long, and the outer ankles are plump. When there is more blood but less qi, the hair on the calves is short, the skin of the outer ankles is tough and thick. If there is a deficiency of qi and blood, the hip hair is sparse, the skin of the outer ankles is thin and soft, there will be no hair, and the outer ankles are emaciated with little flesh

    9. 足太陽之上，血氣盛則美眉，眉有毫毛血多氣少則惡眉，面多少理，血少氣多則面多肉，血氣和則美色，足太陽之下，血氣盛則跟肉滿，踵堅，氣少血多則瘦，跟空，血氣皆少則善轉筋，踵下痛。 Above the Jueyin (足太陽) channel, when blood and qi are abundant, there will be beautiful eyebrows; if there is more blood but less qi, the eyebrows will be bad; face with some reason, more blood and less qi result in a meaty face. When blood and qi are balanced, there will be a beautiful complexion. Below the Jueyin channel, when blood and qi are abundant, there is full calf flesh and firm heels. If there is less qi and more blood, one becomes thin, with hollow calves and heels. Blood and qi deficient leads to flexibility of the tendons, and pain in the soles of the feet.

    10. 手陽明之上，血氣盛則髭美。血少氣多則髭惡，血氣皆少則無髭。手陽明之下血氣盛則腋下毛美，手魚肉以溫，氣血皆少則手瘦以寒。 Above the Hand Shao Yang, when blood and qi are abundant, the eyebrows are beautiful. If there is a deficiency of blood and an excess of qi, the eyebrows will be unsightly; if both blood and qi are deficient, there will be no eyebrows. Below the Hand Shao Yang, when blood and qi are abundant, the armpit hair is beautiful; the hand is warm, and there is no deficiency of either blood or qi.

    11. 手少陰之上，血氣盛則眉美以長，耳色美，血氣皆少則耳焦惡色。手少陽之下，血氣盛則手卷多肉以溫，血氣皆少則寒以瘦，氣少血多則瘦以多脈。 When there is a lack of yin on the upper hand, and qi is abundant, the eyebrows are beautiful and long, and the ear color is attractive. When there is a deficiency of yang below the hand, and the qi is abundant, the hands will have many tendons and be warm; when there is a lack of yin and yang and the qi is deficient, one will become thin due to coldness and have many pulse points.

    12. 手太陽之上，血氣盛則有多鬚，面多肉以平，血氣皆少則面瘦惡色。手太陽之下，血氣盛則掌肉充滿，血氣皆少則掌瘦以寒。 Above the Hand Sun (Meridian), when Qi is abundant, there are abundant whiskers, and the face is full and round; when Qi is deficient, the face is thin and has a bad complexion. Below the Hand Sun Meridian, when Qi is abundant, the palm muscles are full; when Qi is deficient, the palm is weak and cold.

    13. 黃帝曰：二十五人者，刺之有約乎？歧伯曰：美眉者，足太陽之脈，氣血多，惡眉者，血氣少，其肥而澤者，血氣有餘，肥而不澤者，氣有餘，血不足，瘦而無澤者，氣血俱不足，審察其形氣有餘不足而調之，可以知逆順矣。 Yellow Emperor said: Are there any patterns in the twenty-five men? Duke of Shao replied: Those with good brows, they have an abundance of Qi and blood from the foot Yin (Conception Vessel) channel. Those with bad brows, they have a deficiency of Qi and blood. If someone is fat and not bright, there is an excess of Qi but a deficiency of blood. If someone is thin and not bright, both Qi and blood are deficient. By examining their forms and determining if there is an excess or deficiency, one can discern if they are favorable or unfavorable.

    14. 黃帝曰：刺其諸陰陽奈何？歧伯曰：按其寸口人迎，以調陰陽，切循其經絡之凝澀，結而不通者，此於身皆為痛痺，甚則不行，故凝澀，凝澀者，致氣以溫之血和乃止。其結絡者，脈結血不和，決之乃行，故曰：氣有餘於上者，導而下之，氣不足於上者，推而休之，其稽留不至者，因而迎之，必明於經隧，乃能持之，寒與熱爭者，導而行之，其宛陳血不結者，則而予之，必先明知二十五人則血氣之所在，左右上下，刺約畢也。 Yellow Emperor asked: How to pierce the Jueyin? Duke of Shou replied: Press the Conception and Governor points, regulate Yin and Yang, follow the channels and collaterals, tie those that do not connect, in the body all these are painful and stiff, sometimes they cannot move, therefore follow the channels and collaterals, follow the channels and collaterals to regulate the Qi, warm the blood, and bring harmony. For those who are tied, there is excess Qi above, guide it downwards; if the Qi is insufficient above, push it gently; when they hesitate and do not reach the appropriate point, approach it; you must master the path of the channels, so as to be able to hold them. When cold and heat contend, guide them along the path; for those who have no knotted blood, give them medicine; you must first understand the location of the blood and Qi in the twenty-five men, left right upper and lower, pierce and complete.
