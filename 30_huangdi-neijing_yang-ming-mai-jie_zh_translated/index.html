    陽明脈解

    1: 黃帝問曰：足陽明之脈，病，惡人與火，聞木音則惕然而驚，鐘鼓不為動，聞木音而驚，何也，願聞其故。歧伯對曰：陽明者胃脈也，胃者，土也，故聞木音而驚者，土惡木也。帝曰：善。其惡火，何也。歧伯曰：陽明主肉，其脈血氣盛，邪客之則熱，熱甚則惡火。帝曰：其惡人何也。歧伯曰：陽明厥則喘而惋，惋則惡人。帝曰：或喘而死者，或喘而生者，何也。歧伯曰：厥逆連藏則死，連經則生。 Yellow Emperor asked: The Yangming pulse, when sick, is bad for people and fire. Hearing wood sound, feeling startled and frightened, bells and drums do not move, hearing wood sound while frightened, why is that? I want to hear the reason. Huangdi answered: Yangming is the Stomach meridian, the Stomach is Earth, so hearing the wood sound and being frightened is because Earth is bad for Wood. Emperor said: What is bad fire? Huangdi said: The Yangming governs the flesh, with the meridian blood and qi abundant, if a demonic pathogen enters then it will be hot, and if it becomes too hot, it is bad for fire. Emperor said: What is bad people? Huangdi said: When Yangming is deficient, there is panting and gasping, and gasping is bad for people. Emperor asked: If someone pants and dies or pants and lives, why is that? Huangdi answered: If the deficiency and excess are combined, then it will die, and if they are coordinated, then it will live.

    2: 帝曰：善。病甚則棄衣而走，登高而歌，或至不食數日，踰垣上屋，所上之處，皆非其素所能也，病反能者何也。歧伯曰：四支者，諸陽之本也，陽盛則四支實，實則能登高也。帝曰：其棄衣而走者，何也。歧伯曰：熱盛於身，故棄衣欲走也。帝曰：其妄言罵詈不避親踈而歌者，何也。歧伯曰：陽盛則使人妄言罵詈不避親踈，而不欲食，不欲食，故妄走也。 The Emperor said: Good. When the illness is severe, one abandons clothing and runs, climbs high and sings, or may not eat for several days, jumping over walls to enter a house - all of which are beyond one's previous abilities. How can someone who is ill be capable of such actions? Master Qi said: The four limbs are the origin of Yang energy. When Yang is strong, the four limbs are real, and thus able to climb high. The Emperor said: What about those who abandon clothing and run? Master Qi said: The heat is intense within the body, causing one to abandon clothing and want to run. The Emperor said: What about those who speak nonsense, curse, do not avoid their parents, and sing while doing so? Master Qi said: When Yang energy is strong, it causes a person to speak nonsense, curse, and not avoid their parents, and thus not want to eat, not want to eat, and therefore walk aimlessly.
