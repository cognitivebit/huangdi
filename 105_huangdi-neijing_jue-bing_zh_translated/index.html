    厥病

    1: 厥頭痛，面若腫起而煩心，取之足陽明太陰。厥頭痛，頭脈痛，心悲，善泣，視頭動脈反盛者，刺盡去血，後調足厥陰。 Headache, with the face feeling swollen and disturbing the mind, take sufficient Yang Ming and Yin Bai. Headache, headache, heartache, crying well, looking at the pulsating head blood vessel that is full, pierce to let blood and then apply moxa to the Kidney Yin.

    2: 厥頭痛，貞貞頭重而痛，寫頭上五行，行五，先取手少陰，後取足少陰。厥頭痛，意善忘，按之不得，取頭面左右動脈，後取足太陰。 Headache, headache with heaviness and pain, write the five elements on the forehead, perform the five, first take the hand shao yin, then take the foot shao yin. For headache, mind good and forgetting, pressing it does not alleviate, take the head's left and right dynamic vessels, then take the foot tai yin.

    3: 厥頭痛，項先痛，腰脊為應，先取天柱，後取足太陽。厥頭痛，頭痛甚，耳前後脈湧有熱，寫出其血，後取足少陽。 The text describes a condition and the corresponding acupuncture points to alleviate the symptoms. Here is the translation:

    Headache and neck pain, back pain, first take the Heaven's Pillar and then take the Bladder's Brightness. Headache severe, with hot pulse at both temples, release the blood by writing, then take the Minor Yin.

    Note that writing here refers to a specific acupuncture technique, not literally writing with a pen or pencil. The text also mentions some acupoints by their traditional names in Chinese medicine: 天柱 (Heaven's Pillar), 足太陽 (Bladder's Brightness), and 足少阳 (Minor Yin).

    4: 真頭痛，頭痛甚，腦盡痛，手足寒至節，死不治。 Extreme headache, headache severe, brain pain completely, hands and feet cold to the bone, cannot be cured until death.

    5: 頭痛不可取於腧者，有所擊墮，惡血在於內，若肉傷，痛未已，可則刺，不可遠取也。頭痛不可刺者，大痺為惡，日作者，可令少愈，不可已。頭半寒痛，先取手少陽陽明，後取足少陽陽明。 For headache at the temples that cannot be relieved by pressing, there is a fall and bad blood inside. If there is muscle injury and the pain has not yet stopped, then one can needle it, but do not take it too far away. For headaches that should not be needled, severe headache is bad, caused by a daytime evil spirit, which can make it slightly better, but do not let it persist. For half-cold headache, first take the hand Shaoyang Jingming, then take the foot Shaoyang Jingming.

    6: 厥心痛，與背相控，善瘈，如從後觸其心，傴僂者，腎心痛也，先取京骨、崑崙，發狂不已，取然谷。厥心痛，腹脹胸滿，心尤痛甚，胃心痛也，取之大都、大白。 The patient's heart aches, leaning against the back. It hurts on the right side, as if one is poked in the heart from behind. The patient is dizzy and restless, taking herbal medicine to no avail. Then, take rhubarb root. The patient's heart aches severely, with abdomen distended and chest full. The heart pain is particularly severe, due to stomach discomfort. Take ginseng and liquorice.

    7: 厥心痛，痛如以錐鍼刺其心，心痛甚者，脾心痛也，取之然谷、太谿。 Extreme pain in the heart, as if a needle were piercing it. If the heart pain is severe, it is due to a disharmony between the spleen and heart. Treat with rhizoma atractylodis macrococcae (dang shen) and rhizoma blemia (tong caulis).

    8: 厥心痛，色蒼蒼如死狀，終日不得太息，肝心痛也，取之行間、太衝。 The heart aches, the color turns pale like death, unable to take a deep breath all day, it is a pain in the liver and gallbladder, taking medicine while walking or sitting.

    9: 厥心痛，臥若徒居，心痛間，動作，痛益甚，色不變，肺心痛也，取之魚際、太淵。 The heart aches, lying as if constantly dwelling, the pain in the heart increases with movement, the color remains unchanged, it is pain between the lungs and heart, taking from the lower back/kidneys.

    10: 真心痛，手足清至節，心痛甚，日發夕死，夕發旦死。心痛不可刺者，中有盛聚，不可取於腧。 Deep grief, hands and feet thoroughly washed, heart grieves greatly, daytime sending forth, evening dying, evening sending forth, dawn dying. The pain of the heart cannot be pierced; there is a great accumulation in the middle, and it cannot be obtained from the liver.

    11: 腸中有蟲瘕及蛟蛕，皆不可取以小鍼；心腸痛，憹作痛，腫聚，往來上下行，痛有休止，腹熱喜渴涎出者，是蛟蛕也。以手聚按而堅持之，無令得移，以大鍼刺之，久持之，蟲不動，乃出鍼也。㤣腹憹痛，形中上者。 Inside the intestines, there are parasites and Leeches. They cannot be removed with a small lancet. When the abdomen and heart hurt, they cause pain that comes and goes, swelling that gathers and disperses, moving up and down. The pain has periods of relief, and the abdomen is hot, thirsty, and produces saliva. This is the case with Leeches. Holding the area with one hand and pressing firmly will prevent movement. Continuously pierce it with a large lancet until the parasite does not move and then comes out. 2. Pain in the abdomen and chest

    12: 耳聾無聞，取耳中；耳鳴，取耳前動脈；耳痛不可刺者，耳中有膿，若有乾聆聊，耳無聞也；耳聾取手小指次指爪甲上與肉交者，先取手，後取足；耳鳴取手中指爪甲上，左取右，右取左，先取手，後取足。 Deafness without hearing, take from the ear; tinnitus, take from the front of the carotid artery; painful ears that cannot be lanced, there is pus in the ear, if there is dry yellow ears, it is deafness; taking the ear with the fingertips of the small finger and the second finger, first take the hand and then take the foot; tinnitus, take from the finger joints of the hands, left take right, right take left, first take the hand and then take the foot.

    13: 足髀不可舉，側而取之，在樞合中，以員利鍼，大鍼不可刺。病注下血，取曲泉。 The thigh cannot be lifted; it must be taken from the side. In the joint, use a small needle with an advantageous handle. The large needle should not be inserted. Blood is drained below the disease site, and take curved water.

    14: 風痺淫礫，病不可已者，足如履冰，時如入湯中，股脛淫礫，煩心頭痛，時嘔時悗，眩已汗出，久則目眩，悲以喜恐，短氣，不樂，不出三年死也。 The person with a venereal disease, if it is not treatable, walks as if treading on ice and feels as though submerged in hot water. The genitals are inflamed like a pustule, causing headache, nausea, and occasional vomiting. Dizziness leads to sweating, which in turn causes the eyes to become dizzy, eventually leading to blindness. Overcome by sadness and fear, they experience shortness of breath, cannot find joy, and may die within three years.
