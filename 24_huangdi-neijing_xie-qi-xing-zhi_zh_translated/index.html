    血氣形志

    1. 夫人之常數，太陽常多血少氣，少陽常少血多氣，陽明常多氣多血，少陰常少血多氣，厥陰常多血少氣，太陰常多氣少血，此天之常數。足太陽與少陰為表裏，少陽與厥陰為表裏，陽明與太陰為表裏，是為足陰陽也。手太陽與少陰為表裏，少陽與心主為表裏，陽明與太陰為表裏，是為手之陰陽也。今知手足陰陽所苦，凡治病必先去其血，乃去其所苦，伺之所欲，然後寫有餘，補不足。 The constant nature of women, the sun is often bloody and less qi; lesser yang is less bloody and more qi, jueyin is often deficient in blood but more in qi, baxian is often deficient in blood but abundant in qi, and the kidneys are always deficient in blood but rich in qi. The foot zang and fu organs represent yin and yang externally; the zang of lesser yang and the fu of the kidney are external; the zang of yangming and the fu of the spleen are external; the hand zang and fu organs represent yin and yang externally; the zang of lesser yang and the fu of the heart are external; the zang of jueyin and the fu of the lungs are external. Now, knowing that the hands and feet are treated for their respective yin and yang deficiencies, in treating diseases one must first remove the blood, thereby removing the source of the imbalance, and then replenish what is lacking to achieve balance.

    2. 欲知背俞，先度其兩乳間，中折之，更以他草度去半已，即以兩隅相拄也，乃舉以度其背，令其一隅居上，齊脊大椎，兩隅在下，當其下隅者，肺之俞也。得下一度，心之俞也。復下一度，左角肝之俞也，右角脾之俞也。復下一度，腎之俞也。是謂五藏之俞，灸刺之度也。 To understand the position of the lung meridian, first locate the two nipples and find the midpoint between them. Then, fold one side over the other and use your finger to measure about halfway from the fold. The lung meridian is located at the intersection point where you stop touching. After mastering this, you can raise a hand to measure its position, with the lung meridian on top, the kidney meridian at the bottom, and both sides connecting at the lower end. The left side corresponds to the lung meridian, the right side corresponds to the spleen meridian. Further down is the kidney meridian. This describes the position of the five organs' meridians through acupuncture and moxibustion measurements.

    3. 形樂志苦，病生於脈，治之以灸刺。形樂志樂，病生於肉，治之以鍼石。形苦志樂，病生於筋，治之以熨引。形苦志苦，病生於咽嗌，治之以百藥。形數驚恐，經絡不通，病生於不仁，治之以按摩醪藥。是謂五形志也。 The Formulas for Treating the Five Constitutional Patterns:

    4. Form Rebellious and Difficult, disease arising at the pulse, treated with acupuncture and moxibustion.

    5. Form Joyful and Ease, disease arising in the flesh, treated with cupping and piercing.
    6. Form Painful and Joyful, disease arising in the tendons, treated with friction and pulling.
    7. Form Rebellious and Difficult, disease arising at the throat, treated with a hundred medicines.
    8. Form Numerous and Terrified, blockage of the channels and collaterals, disease arising in the area of the heart, treated with massage and aromatic medicine.

    These are the five constitutional patterns.

Piercing the Yang Sun brings forth pure energy, piercing the Yin Sun releases impure energy, and piercing the Yin and Yang extremes results in clear vision as well
