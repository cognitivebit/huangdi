    著至教論

    1: 黃帝坐明堂，召雷公而問之曰：子知醫之道乎。雷公對曰：誦而頗能解，解而未能別，別而未能明，明而未能彰，足以治群僚，不足至侯王。願得受樹天之度，四時陰陽合之，別星辰與日月光，以彰經術，後世益明，上通神農，著至教疑於二皇。帝曰：善。無失之，此皆陰陽表裏上下雌雄相輸應也，而道上知天文，下知地理，中知人事，可以長久，以教眾庶，亦不疑殆，醫道論篇，可傳後世，可以為寶。雷公曰：請受道，諷誦用解。 Yellow Emperor sat in the hall of clarity, summoned Master Lü and asked him, saying: Do you know the way of medicine? Lü replied, saying: I can recite it somewhat accurately, but cannot differentiate; after differentiation, I am unable to clarify; upon clarification, I am unable to distinguish; upon distinction, I am unable to make it evident. Sufficient for treating multitudes of officials, but not sufficient for the marquises and kings. He hoped to receive the teaching of Heaven's measure, understanding the harmony of yin and yang at the four seasons, differentiating the stars and constellations from the sun, moon, and light, using this to clarify the classics, thereby making later generations more enlightened, leading the way in understanding the divine farmer, and leaving behind a teachable method for the two emperors.

    The Yellow Emperor said, Good. There is no failure here; this is all about the yin and yang above and below, front and back, female and male corresponding and responding to each other. The Way understands the celestial theories of Heaven and knows the geography of the earth, as well as understanding human affairs, which can be long-lasting. By teaching the multitudes, there is no doubt that it will suffice, and the medical methodology can be passed down to future generations and become a treasure.

    Lü said, Please grant me the Way, and I will recite and use it.

    2: 帝曰：子不聞陰陽傳乎。曰：不知。曰：夫三陽天為業，上下無常，合而病至，偏害陰陽。雷公曰：三陽莫當，請聞其解。帝曰：三陽獨至者，是三陽并至，并至如風雨，上為巔疾，下為漏病。外無期，內無正，不中經紀，診無上下，以書別。雷公曰：臣治踈愈，說意而已。帝曰：三陽者，至陽也，積并則為驚，病起疾風，至如礔礰，九竅皆塞，陽氣滂溢，乾嗌喉塞，并於陰，則上下無常，薄為腸澼。此謂三陽直心，坐不得起，臥者便身全，三陽之病。且以知天下何以別陰陽，應四時，合之五行。雷公曰：陽言不別，陰言不理，請起受解，以為至道。帝曰：子若受傳，不知合至道以惑師教，語子至道之要。病傷五藏，筋骨以消，子言不明不別，是世主學盡矣。腎且絕，惋惋，日暮從容不出，人事不殷。 The Emperor said: Zi, do you not hear of the Yin and Yang tradition? Zi replied, I am ignorant. The Emperor said: Heaven has its three Yang lines, upper and lower changing, merging and becoming sick, with Yin and Yang being harmed. Thunder Lord asked, 'May I learn your prescription?' The Emperor said: Three Yang in isolation is as when the Three Yang fully merge, like wind and rain. Up above it is sharp, down below it is leak disease. With no set date for recovery and no proper course, diagnosis misses the mark high and low, and misjudges with written records. Thunder Lord said: My governance of treatment has been incomplete; I am but expressing my intentions. The Emperor said: Three Yang refers to great Yang, when they accumulate it becomes shock, and illness appears as a sudden wind, like a wild whirlwind, blocking all nine orifices, causing Yang Qi to overflow, stopping up the dryness in the throat, merging with Yin, then upper and lower are unstable, and it becomes shallow for the Stomach, harming the Intestines. This is called Three Yang directly striking the heart, causing it to be unable to stand or lie down, and a person who is lying down will suffer everywhere from Three Yang disease. Furthermore, knowing how to differentiate Yin and Yang throughout the world, and following the changes of the seasons, merging them with the Five Constancies, Thunder Lord asks for an explanation, so that I may understand the great Way. Zi, if you receive this transmission, and do not know how to harmonize with the great Way, you will confuse your teachers and students. You speak without clarity or distinction, and that is the limit of worldly wisdom. The kidneys are also on the verge of vanishing, day by day becoming less and less present, and people's affairs do not become deeper.
