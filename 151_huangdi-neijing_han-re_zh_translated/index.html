    寒熱

    1: 黃帝問于歧伯曰：寒熱瘰癧在於頸腋者，皆何氣使生？歧伯曰：此皆鼠瘻寒熱之毒氣也，留於脈而不去者也。 The Yellow Emperor asked Yi Bo, The chills and fevers of the body are all located in the area from the neck to the armpit; what kind of energy causes them? Yi Bo replied, These are the toxic energies of cold and heat from the mouse spirit. They lodge in the channels and do not leave.

    2: 黃帝曰：去之奈何？歧伯曰：鼠瘻之本，皆在於藏，其末上出於頸腋之間，其浮於脈中，而未內著於肌肉，而外為膿血者，易去也。 Yellow Emperor asked: How can it be removed? Huang Di said: The root of the mouse's liver is in the gallbladder. Its tip comes out from between the neck and armpit, floats in the blood vessels, but is not attached to the muscle tissue on the inside, and on the outside it is pus-like blood. It can be easily removed.

    3: 黃帝曰：去之奈何？歧伯曰：請從其本引其末，可使衰去，而絕其寒熱。審按其道以予之，徐往徐來以去之，其小如麥者，一刺知，三刺而已。 Yellow Emperor said: How to cure it? Huang Di (the Yellow Lord) replied: Take medicine from its root and treat the end, so that one can prevent further deterioration while not completely curing the cold and heat. After examining the pathogenesis and providing the medicine, approach gradually and eliminate it. If it is minor like millet, a needle prick can identify it, three needles and it's done.

    4: 黃帝曰：決其生死奈何？歧伯曰：反其目視之，其中有赤脈，上下貫瞳子，見一脈，一歲死；見一脈半，一歲半死；見二脈，二歲死；見二脈半，二歲半死；見三脈，三歲而死。見赤脈不下貫瞳子，可治也。 Yellow Emperor asked: How can one determine life and death? Huanbo replied: If you look at the eyes, there is a red pulse. If it is seen above and below the pupil, this person will die in one year. If only half of it is seen, the person will be half-dead in one year. If two pulses are seen, the person will die in two years. If only half of them are seen, the person will be half-dead in two years. If three pulses are seen, the person will die in three years. If a red pulse is not seen below the pupil, it can be treated.
