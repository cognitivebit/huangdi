    論痛

    1: 黃帝問于少俞曰：筋骨之強弱，肌肉之堅脆，皮膚之厚薄，腠理之疏密，各不同，其于鍼石火焫之痛何如？腸胃之厚薄堅脆亦不等，其於毒藥何如？願盡聞之。少俞曰：人之骨強、筋弱、肉緩、皮膚厚者，耐痛，其于鍼石之痛火焫亦然。 Emperor Yellow asked Shao Yong: The strength and weakness of tendons and bones, the firmness and brittleness of muscles, the thickness and thinness of skin, the sparseness and density of sinews - each is different. How does this apply to pain from needles, stones, and fire? The intestines and stomach are also not uniform in thickness, firmness, and brittleness. How does this apply to poisonous medicines? Please instruct me on this matter. Shao Yong said: In humans, those with strong bones, weak tendons, soft muscles, and thick skin can withstand pain. This applies to pain from needles, stones, and fire as well.

    2: 黃帝曰：其耐火焫者，何以知之？少俞答曰：加以黑色而美骨者，耐火焫。黃帝曰：其不耐鍼石之痛者，何以知之？少俞曰：堅肉薄皮者，不耐鍼石之痛，於火焫亦然。 Yellow Emperor said: How do I know if something can withstand fire? Shao Yong replied: Apply a black coating to it when it is beautiful, and it can withstand fire. Yellow Emperor said: How do I know if something cannot withstand the pain of being pierced by a sharp object? Shao Yong said: Something with tough flesh and thin skin cannot withstand the pain of being pierced by a sharp object; it is the same when it comes to withstanding fire.

    3: 黃帝曰：人之病，或同時而傷，或易已，或難已，其故何如？少俞曰：同時而傷，其身多熱者，易已；多寒者，難已。 Yellow Emperor asked: In the case of human illness, it can be simultaneous injury, already easy to cure, or difficult to cure. What are the reasons for this? Shao Yong replied: In the case of simultaneous injury, if there are many cases of fever, it is easily cured; if there are many cases of cold, it is difficult to cure.

    4: 黃帝曰：人之勝毒，何以知之？少俞曰：胃厚、色黑、大骨及肥者，皆勝毒；故其瘦而薄胃者，皆不勝毒也。 Yellow Emperor asked: How do you know that humans overcome toxicity? Shao Yong said: Those who have a thick stomach, black complexion, large bones, and are plump all overcome toxicity; therefore, those who become thin and have a weak stomach do not overcome toxicity.
