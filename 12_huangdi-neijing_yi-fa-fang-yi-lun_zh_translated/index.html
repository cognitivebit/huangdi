    異法方宜論

    1. 黃帝問曰：醫之治病也，一病而治各不同皆愈何也。歧伯對曰：地勢使然也。故東方之域，天地之所始生也，魚鹽之地，海濱傍水，其民食魚而嗜鹹，皆安其處，美其食，魚者使人熱中，鹽者勝血，故其民皆黑色踈理，其病皆為癰瘍，其治宜砭石，故砭石者，亦從東方來。 Yellow Emperor asked: Why is the treatment of diseases different for each individual and yet they all recover? Hua Tuo replied: It is due to geographical factors. Therefore, in the eastern region, where heaven and earth were first created, the land of salted fish, near the sea, its people eat salted fish and enjoy saltiness, they are satisfied with their location and enjoy their food. The fish makes people warm and the salt strengthens blood, so the people's complexion is dark, and they often have skin lesions and tumors. Their treatment should be with a touchstone (a type of stone), thus the touchstone comes from the east.

    2. 西方者，金玉之域，沙石之處，天地之所收引也，其民陵居而多風，水土剛強，其民不衣而褐薦，其民華食而脂肥，故邪不能傷其形體，其病生於內，其治宜毒藥，故毒藥者，亦從西方來。 In the west, there is a realm of gold and jade, a place of sand and stones, where heaven and earth converge and attract. The people live in tombs and are often visited by winds, their land has strong soil. Their people do not wear clothes but dwell in huts, they eat fine food and are fat with ointment, therefore evil cannot harm their bodies, their illnesses arise from within, and treatment should involve poisonous medicines; hence, poisonous medicines also come from the west.

    3. 北方者，天地所閉藏之域也，其地高陵居，風寒冰冽，其民樂野處而乳食，藏寒生滿病，其治宜灸焫，故灸焫者，亦從北方來。 Northern regions, where heaven and earth are sealed up and hidden away; the land is high and mountainous, with cold winds and icy coldness. The people there prefer to live in wild places and subsist on milk. They suffer from cold-related diseases, and acupuncture and moxibustion are suitable treatments. Therefore, practitioners of acupuncture and moxibustion also come from the north.

    4. 南方者，天地所長養，陽之所盛處也，其地下，水土弱，霧露之所聚也，其民嗜酸而食胕，故其民皆緻理而赤色，其病攣痺，其治宜微鍼，故九鍼者，亦從南方來。 In the southern region, it is nourished by heaven and earth, a place where yang is abundant. Below the ground, the soil is weak, a place where mist and dew collect. The people there like sour foods and eat millet. Therefore, their skin is smooth and red. Their diseases are mostly related to the hips and waist, and their treatment requires gentle acupuncture. Thus, the nine needles technique also originated from the southern region.

    5. 中央者，其地平以濕，天地所以生萬物也眾，其民食雜而不勞，故其病多痿厥寒熱，其治宜導引按蹻，故導引按蹻者，亦從中央出也。故聖人雜合以治，各得其所宜，故治所以異而病皆愈者，得病之情，知治之大體也。 The central area, where the land is flat and humid, is the place where heaven and earth produce myriad things. The people there eat diverse foods without much labor. Therefore, they have many diseases such as weakness, cold, and fever. Treatment should be by guidance and regulation, and manipulation of the joints, which also originate from the central area. Thus, a sage would use diverse methods to treat illnesses, each according to what is appropriate for the individual, so that treatment may differ but all recover. This shows an understanding of the nature of the disease and the method of treatment.
