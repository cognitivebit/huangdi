    方盛衰論

    1. 雷公請問氣之多少，何者為逆，何者為從。黃帝荅曰：陽從左，陰從右，老從上，少從下，是以春夏歸陽為生，歸秋冬為死，反之，則歸秋冬為生，是以氣多少逆皆為厥。問曰：有餘者厥耶。荅曰：一上不下，寒厥到膝，少者秋冬死，老者秋冬生。氣上不下，頭痛巔疾，求陽不得，求陰不審，五部隔無徵，若居曠野，若伏空室，緜緜乎屬不滿日。是以少氣之厥，令人妄夢其極至迷。三陽絕，三陰微，是為少氣。是以肺氣虛則使人夢見白物，見人斬血藉藉，得其時則夢見兵戰。腎氣虛，則使人夢見舟船溺人，得其時，則夢伏水中若有畏恐。肝氣虛，則夢見菌香生草，得其時，則夢伏樹下不敢起。心氣虛，則夢救火陽物，得其時，則夢燔灼。脾氣虛，則夢飲食不足，得其時，則夢築垣蓋屋。此皆五藏氣虛，陽氣有餘，陰氣不足。合之五診，調之陰陽，以在經脈。 Leigong asked about the abundance of Qi, what is reversed and what follows. The Yellow Emperor said: Yang follows to the left, Yin follows to the right, oldness follows above, youngness follows below. Therefore, in spring and summer, there is generation through Yang, and in autumn and winter, there is death through Yin. Reversed, it would be generation in autumn and winter, and that is why the abundance of Qi is excessive and leads to illness. Ask: If there is a surplus, is it an illness? The Yellow Emperor said: One who does not descend below, cold illness reaches the knees, young people die in autumn and winter, and old people live in autumn and winter. Qi does not descend, causing headache and dizziness, seeking Yang but not getting it, seeking Yin but not discerning it, the five organs are isolated without signs, whether living in sparse wilderness or hiding in empty rooms, they all belong to not having enough sunlight. Therefore, the deficient Qi causes people to have delusions of extreme situations. Three Yangs and three Yins vanish, and this is deficient Qi. Therefore, when Lung Qi is empty, it makes people dream of seeing white things, dream of seeing people cutting blood vessels, if one catches the time, then dreaming of seeing warfare. Kidney Qi being empty causes dreams of drowning people, catching the time, then dreaming of being afraid and dreading in water. Liver Qi being empty causes dreams of seeing weeds growing from grass, catching the time, then dreaming of hiding under trees and not daring to get up. Heart Qi being empty causes dreams of rescuing fire Yang things, catching the time, then dreaming of burning. Spleen Qi being empty causes dreams of insufficient food and drink, catching the time, then dreaming of building walls and roofs. All these are the five organs' deficient Qi, Yang Qi having a surplus, and Yin Qi not enough. Merge the five diagnoses, regulate Yin and Yang, it is in the meridians and vessels.

    2. 診有十度，度人脈度，藏度，肉度，筋度，俞度。陰陽氣盡，人病自具。脈動無常，散陰頗陽，脈脫不具，診無常行，診必上下，度民君卿，受師不卒，使術不明，不察逆從，是為妄行，持雌失雄，棄陰附陽，不知并合，診故不明，傳之後世，反論自章。至陰虛，天氣絕，至陽盛，地氣不足。陰陽並交，至人之所行。陰陽並交者，陽氣先至，陰氣後至。是以聖人持診之道，先後陰陽而持之，奇恒之勢乃六十首，診合微之事，追陰陽之變，章五中之情，其中之論，取虛實之要，定五度之事，知此乃足以診。是以切陰不得陽，診消亡，得陽不得陰，守學不湛，知左不知右，知右不知左，知上不知下，知先不知後，故治不久。知醜知善，知病知不病，知高知下，知坐知起，知行知止，用之有紀，診道乃具，萬世不殆。起所有餘，知所不足。度事上下，脈事因格。是以形弱氣虛，死。形氣有餘脈氣不足，死。脈氣有餘形氣不足，生。 Diagnosis in ten degrees, degree the pulse, degree the person's pulse, degree hidden, degree flesh, degree tendons, degree joint. Balanced yin and yang, and the person is sick by himself. Irregular pulse movement, scattering yin and solid yang, the pulse does not have a possession, diagnosis is not constant, diagnosis must be above and below, diagnosing the common people, officials and ministers, receiving teachers not dying, using techniques not clear, not discerning obeying and disobeying, this is called wrong actions, holding female losing male, abandoning yin attaching yang, not knowing blending and merging, diagnostic principles do not understand, pass it down to future generations, and debate from themselves. When yin is empty, heavenly spirit disappears, when yang is strong, earthly spirit does not suffice. Yin and yang blend, at people's actions. Whoever blends yin and yang, yang qi first arrives, and yin qi follows. Therefore, sages holding the diagnostic art, first and last yin and yang, and hold them. The mysterious power of six tens is complete, knowing the merging of micro-diagnostic events, following the changes of yin and yang, mastering five degrees' affairs, knowing this suffices for diagnosis. Therefore, do not cut yin and get yang, and diagnostic disappears. Do not get yin and guard academic studies, know left but not right, know right but not left, know up but not down, know before but not after, therefore treatment does not last long. Know ugly knows good, knows disease knows not disease, knows high knows low, knows sitting knows standing, knows stopping knows moving, using it has a record, diagnostic way is complete, endless throughout the ages. Starting from all remnants, knowing what is lacking. Degree affairs up and down, pulse events due to format. Therefore, weak form and empty spirit, die. Form spirit has remaining pulse qi does not suffice, die. Pulse qi has remaining form spirit does not suffice, live.

    3. 是以診有大方，坐起有常，出入有行，以轉神明，必清必淨，上觀下觀，司八正邪，別五中部，按脈動靜，循尺滑濇，寒溫之意，視其大小，合之病能，逆從以得，復知病名，診可十全，不失人情，故診之或視息視意，故不失條理，道甚明察，故能長久。不知此道，失經絕理，亡言妄期，此謂失道。 Therefore, being able to diagnose means understanding the large and small methods, sitting up having a routine, coming and going having a path, converting the spirit and mind, being certain to be clear and pure, observing the upper and lower, regulating yin and yang, differentiating the five zang organs, palpating the pulse and stillness, following the changes of cold and heat, seeing their degree, combining them, and being able to cure diseases according to their reversal, they know the disease's name, diagnosis can be perfected in ten aspects, not losing human feelings. For this reason, when diagnosing, one should look at both breath and mind. Therefore, it is not lost in reasoning. The way is very clear and accurate, so it can last for a long time. Not knowing this way leads to losing the classics and mistaken expectations, which is called losing the way.
